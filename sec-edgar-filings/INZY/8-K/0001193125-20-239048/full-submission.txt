 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="right"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Inozyme Pharma Reports Second Quarter 2020 Financial Results and Provides Business Highlights </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Upsized IPO in July&nbsp;2020 raised $128.8&nbsp;million in gross proceeds </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Submitted IND for <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">INZ-701</FONT> for the treatment of ENPP1 deficiency; currently on FDA clinical hold
pending completion of ongoing GLP toxicology studies </I></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Initiation of <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">INZ-701</FONT> Phase 1/2
clinical trials anticipated in early 2021, as previously planned </I></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>BOSTON, September</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;3, 2020 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) </B>&#150;
<U>Inozyme Pharma, Inc.</U> (Nasdaq: INZY), a rare disease biopharmaceutical company developing novel therapeutics for the treatment of diseases of abnormal mineralization impacting the vasculature, soft tissue and skeleton, today reported financial
results for the second quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2020 and provided recent business highlights. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We have made substantial progress over the past
quarter and in July, including completing our upsized initial public offering in July, acquiring additional ENPP1 deficiency program assets, and submitting our first investigational new drug application (IND) for INZ-701 for the treatment of ENPP1
deficiency,&#148; said Axel Bolte, MSc, MBA, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">co-founder,</FONT> president and chief executive officer of Inozyme Pharma. &#147;FDA has requested final study reports for our ongoing three-month GLP toxicology studies
