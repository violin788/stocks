<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">Effective September
5, 2013, Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;) was issued a Certificate of Filing for the assumed name of &ldquo;ZION

oil and gas drilling rig and equipment operations in Israel. The Company has the trademark &ldquo;ZION DRILLING&rdquo; filed with
the United States Patent and Trademark Office. Also, the Company has the trademark filed with the World Intellectual Property Organization
in Geneva, Switzerland, pursuant to the Madrid Agreement and Protocol, and with the Israel Trademark Office.</P>
