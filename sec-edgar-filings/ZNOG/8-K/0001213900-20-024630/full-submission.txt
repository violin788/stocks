<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><B>Item 3.01 <FONT STYLE="color: #333333; background-color: white">Notice
of Delisting or Failure to Satisfy a Continued Listing Rule or Standard; </FONT>Transfer of Listing</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-indent: 0.5in; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0">By letter dated March
13, 2020, the Nasdaq Hearing Panel (the &ldquo;Panel&rdquo;) granted the request of Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;)
for continued listing on The Nasdaq Stock Market LLC (&ldquo;Nasdaq&rdquo;), subject to the Company demonstrating compliance with
the minimum $1.00 bid price requirement, as set forth in Nasdaq Listing Rule 5550(a)(2) (the &ldquo;Rule&rdquo;), on or before
June 26, 2020.&nbsp; On April 16, 2020, the minimum bid price requirement was suspended by Nasdaq for all listed companies and
the Panel has extended for the Company the continued listing on Nasdaq, subject to the Company demonstrating compliance with the
minimum $1.00 bid price requirement, as set forth in the Rule, on or before September 8, 2020. In order to evidence compliance
with the Rule, the Company must evidence a closing bid price of at least $1.00 per share for a minimum of 10, through generally
not more than 20, consecutive business days.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-indent: 0.5in; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0">With the shutdowns by
the Covid-19 virus in the United States, Europe and Israel, many operations for the Company have been delayed beyond our control
by the respective governments resulting in difficulties in satisfying Nasdaq&rsquo;s closing bid price listing requirement until
the Company resumes operations in Israel. During this uncertain Covid-19 time, the officers and directors believe that it is in
the best interest of the Company and its shareholders to change the listing exchange of its stock to the OTCQX, the top tier of
the OTC Markets.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The Company also issued
a press release on August 31, 2020 titled &ldquo;Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. to Transition Trading of Shares to the OTCQX - an SEC
Recognized Established Public Market&rdquo; describing the transition from Nasdaq to OTC Markets. The press release is being filed
as Exhibit 99.01 and incorporated by reference into this Current Report on Form 8-K.</P>

<P STYLE="text-align: right; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black"><B>Exhibit 99.01</B></FONT></P>

<P STYLE="margin: 0; text-align: right"><FONT STYLE="color: Black"><B>&nbsp;</B></FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black"><B>Zion Oil &amp; Gas to Transition
Trading of Shares to the OTCQX &ndash; an SEC Recognized Established Public Market</B></FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black"><B>&nbsp;</B></FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black; background-color: white"><B>Move to
OTCQX should continue to provide adequate liquidity for the trading of Zion shares at less cost</B></FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black"><B>&nbsp;</B></FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black; background-color: white">DALLAS, Texas,
and CAESAREA, Israel, August 31, 2020 /PRNewswire/&nbsp;Zion Oil &amp; Gas notified the Nasdaq Stock Market LLC that Zion is de-listing
its shares from the Nasdaq Stock Market LLC&rsquo;s Capital Market and will have its shares quoted for trading on OTC Markets
Group Inc.&rsquo;s OTCQX Best Market, the top tier of the OTC markets. </FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; color: #444444"><FONT STYLE="color: Black">&ldquo;With the transition
to OTCQX, we are migrating to a more business-friendly market during these turbulent times,&rdquo; stated Zion CEO, Robert Dunn.
&ldquo;Zion will continue to provide all required disclosures to provide transparency regarding Zion and its business, and we
believe that trading on OTCQX should continue to provide adequate liquidity for the trading of Zion shares at less cost to Zion.&rdquo;</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black">As previously disclosed by Zion, Nasdaq
has advised Zion it will have through September 8, 2020, to regain compliance with the minimum closing bid price of $1.00. As
the management of Zion has determined that it is not in the best interest of Zion and its shareholders to implement a reverse
stock split to satisfy the minimum bid price, Zion applied for and meets the current standards for trading on the OTCQX.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black; background-color: white">W</FONT><FONT STYLE="color: Black">ith
the shutdowns by the Covid-19 virus in the United States, Europe, and Israel, all ground operations for Zion, including drilling
plans, have been delayed beyond Zion&rsquo;s control by the respective governments. Zion management believes that the extended
shutdown adds an additional layer of difficulty. Management also has determined that the transition to the OTCQX Market better
serves the interests of Zion and its shareholders at this stage in Zion&rsquo;s corporate development. With the transition to
the OTCQX, the OTC&rsquo;s top tier, Zion will continue to comply with its SEC reporting obligations as always. </FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><FONT STYLE="color: Black">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; background-color: white; color: #444444"><FONT STYLE="color: Black">Zion
is moving to a broker-dealer market from the matching engine technology used by the Nasdaq exchange. <FONT STYLE="background-color: white">OTC
Link ATS and OTC Link ECN are SEC-regulated ATS&rsquo;s operated by OTC Link LLC, a FINRA/SIPC</FONT> member. OTCQX is the premium
tier of OTC Markets Group and enables investors to trade through the broker of their choice easily. Investors will have access
to real-time bid-ask information and other information through the OTC Disclosure &amp; News Service, which publishes and distributes
data, news, and financials to a broad audience of investors.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; background-color: white; color: #444444"><FONT STYLE="color: Black">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; background-color: white; color: #444444"><FONT STYLE="color: Black">The
OTCQX Rules for U.S. companies are designed to create the most informed, efficient, and cost-effective market for securities.
The OTCQX rules require companies to meet high standards, make adequate, current information publicly available, provide timely
