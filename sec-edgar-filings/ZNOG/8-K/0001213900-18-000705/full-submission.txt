<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify"><B>Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain
Officers; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">On January 15, 2018, Mr.
Ralph DeVore, age 80, provided notice to Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. (&ldquo;Zion&rdquo; or the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;) of his resignation
from the Board of Directors. His resignation is effective as of January 15, 2018 from both the Board of Directors and the Nominating
and Corporate Governance Committee.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">Mr. DeVore serves as President
and board member of Christian Commerce Corporation, a 501(c)(3) private foundation which he founded in 1984. He wants to increase
the focus of the foundation in the promotion and education of Christian principles through world-wide evangelism and devote additional
time to other Christian ministry efforts.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">With his resignation, he
stated that &ldquo;I pray the Lord will continue to bless you and the efforts of the company to bring the Lord&rsquo;s promise
to fruition.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>
