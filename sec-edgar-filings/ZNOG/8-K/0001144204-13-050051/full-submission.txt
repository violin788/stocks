<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>Zion Oil &amp; Gas now accepts Direct
Stock Purchase Plan Electronic Payments</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>Dallas, Texas and Caesarea, Israel &ndash;
September 10, 2013 </B>&ndash; Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. (&ldquo;Zion&rdquo;) (NASDAQ GM: <B>ZN</B>) announced today, in connection
with their Direct Stock Purchase Plan (the &ldquo;DSPP&rdquo; or &ldquo;Plan&rdquo;) and Unit Offering, that they now accept electronic
payments through Telecheck Internet Check Acceptance Service. Zion is the first company to offer electronic enrollment and electronic
payment for stock purchases directly online.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">On August 26, 2013, Zion announced in
a <FONT STYLE="color: #1155CC"><U>press release</U></FONT> the extension of their Unit Offering&rsquo;s expiration date to November
29, 2013, partly so that investors can use this easy, one of a kind, simple way to invest.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Investors can now fill out the DSPP Enrollment
Form and pay for their investment electronically via Zion&rsquo;s website,<FONT STYLE="text-underline-style: none; color: black">
</FONT><FONT STYLE="color: #1155CC"><U>www.zionoil.com/dspp</U></FONT>. Investors can fill out a DSPP enrollment form, sign the
form electronically, and continue to the payment screen where they can verify the enrollment form information and enter their
specific banking information, all in an embedded and encrypted format. The submitted payment information is routed to our merchant,
First Data/CitiBank, for processing through Tempus, our payment gateway provider. The gateway provider is an e-commerce service
provider that authorizes payments and encrypts sensitive information to ensure that information is passed securely between the
Zion website and First Data/CitiBank.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt"><B><I>TeleCheck
Internet Check Acceptance Service and Tempus Technologies Payment Portal API provide the check security safeguards that follow
the PCI DSS guidelines for secure payment processing</I></B></FONT><FONT STYLE="color: #1F497D">.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Zion&rsquo;s common stock trades on the
NASDAQ Global Market under the symbol &ldquo;<B>ZN</B>&rdquo; and Zion&rsquo;s warrants are expected to trade under the reserved
symbol &ldquo;<B>ZNWOW</B>&rdquo;.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">This announcement is neither an offer nor
a solicitation of an offer. The securities are offered by prospectus only, and only within the States and other jurisdictions in
which the securities may be sold, and this announcement is neither an offer to sell nor a solicitation of any offer to buy in any
State or jurisdiction in which such offer, solicitation or sale would be unlawful prior to the registration or qualification under
the securities of any such state or jurisdiction. The DSPP was filed by Prospectus Supplement (File No. 333-174266).</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Zion Oil &amp; Gas, a Delaware corporation,
explores for oil and gas onshore in Israel and holds three petroleum exploration licenses, comprised of the Asher-Menashe License
(covering approximately 79,000 acres), the Joseph License (covering approximately 83,000 acres), and the Jordan Valley License
(covering approximately 56,000 acres). Collectively, our three license areas cover approximately 218,000 acres. The Company has
filed application for the Megiddo-Jezreel Valley License (covering approximately 98,000 acres), which borders the Jordan Valley
License area.</P>
