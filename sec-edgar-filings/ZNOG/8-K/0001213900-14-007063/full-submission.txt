<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify"><FONT STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif">Zion&rsquo;s
investor relations app, powered by theIRapp&reg; through the mobile IR services of the American Stock Transfer &amp; Trust Company,
LLC (&ldquo;AST&rdquo;), allows users to navigate the Company&rsquo;s investor relations materials as well as receive other important
Company information. Features in the app include the latest press releases and SEC filings as well as operational and background
information, videos, audio, and other Company presentations. Sharing functionality via email, Twitter and Facebook is available
as well as the ability for investors to be notified when new information is posted to the company&rsquo;s IR app.</FONT></P>

to have quick and simple access to Company information, including our exploration activities in Israel,&rdquo; commented John
Brown, Chairman and CEO. &ldquo;Recognizing the growing importance of mobile technology to all investors, Zion launched this application
to help ensure that our shareholders have instant access to our corporate and exploration operations. We are excited to be one
of the early adopters of mobile technology that helps drive shareholder value and creates broader awareness throughout the investment
community.&rdquo;</FONT></P>

Dividend Reinvestment and Common Stock Purchase Plan (&ldquo;DSPP&rdquo;). Zion&rsquo;s investor relations app will provide a
direct link to Zion&rsquo;s website and additional Company information such as the DSPP program. Zion&rsquo;s new app will serve
as an additional channel of distribution for key corporate information along with our website and other social media such as Facebook
and Twitter.</FONT></P>
