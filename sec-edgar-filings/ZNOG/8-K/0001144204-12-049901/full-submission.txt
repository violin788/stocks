<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Here is an update regarding events during August 2012.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B><U>Operations</U></B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">(i) <U>Asher-Menashe License Area</U></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">On August 8, 2012, we issued a press release regarding our operations
on our Elijah #3 well.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">In that press release, we noted that we had retrieved sidewall
core samples from the Elijah #3 wellbore and sent them to Houston, Texas for analysis and that both Baker Hughes and the Geophysical
Institute of Israel were processing the VSP data we recently obtained during our well re-entry operations.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Since then, we have received an initial report from Core Laboratories
in Houston. After reviewing Core Lab&rsquo;s initial report with staff and technical consultants, Zion decided that further testing
should be carried out and Core Lab sent some of the core samples for additional testing - a small sub-sample will be flash heated
and a chromatographic measurement will be made on the output. This test is an additional way to detect the presence of oil. Additional
tests are also being considered.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">We continue to work toward the final integration of the preliminary
VSP analysis (which we have received) with all of the pre-existing data into our geologic model. So, the scientific work continues
in order to help understand this license area and the logical &lsquo;next step&rsquo; that we should consider.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">(ii) <U>Joseph License Area</U></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">In June of 2012, we contracted with the Geophysical Institute
of Israel (GII) to acquire approximately 20 kilometers of 2-D seismic data in the southern section of this license area. GII plans
to acquire the data in September 2012.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">On August 13, 2012, the Israeli Petroleum Commissioner notified
us that he was approving our request for an extension to the scheduled expiration date of this License to April 10, 2013, in order
to accommodate unavoidable delays in seismic equipment and crews attributable to our contractor, GII, having their crews in Africa
for an extended period of time.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">We continue to make progress with our geological and geophysical
