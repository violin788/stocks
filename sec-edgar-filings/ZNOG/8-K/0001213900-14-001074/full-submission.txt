<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Dallas, Texas and Caesarea, Israel &#8211; February 21, 2014 &#8211; Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc.
