8.01 Other Events.</b></span></p>
<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0">Zion Oil and Gas, Inc., a Delaware corporation, which has an active petroleum
exploration license onshore Israel on their 99,000-acre Megiddo-Jezreel license area, has been granted an extension of its &#8220;New
Megiddo&#8221;/428 License.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">On November 29, 2021, the Ministry of Energy in Israel granted Zion
Oil &amp; Gas an extension of the &#8220;New Megiddo&#8221;/428 License until August 1, 2022 for the purpose of completing the activities
regarding its MJ-02 drilling and testing.</p>
