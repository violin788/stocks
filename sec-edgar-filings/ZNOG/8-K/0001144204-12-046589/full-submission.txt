<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>ITEM 1.01 ENTRY INTO A MATERIAL DEFINITIVE AGREEMENT</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B><U>Extension of Joseph Petroleum Exploration License</U></B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">On August 13, 2012,
the Israeli Petroleum Commissioner notified Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. (the &quot;Company&quot; or &ldquo;Zion&rdquo;) that it was
approving the Company&rsquo;s request for an extension to the scheduled expiration date of the Company&rsquo;s Joseph License (Petroleum
Exploration License No. 339) to April 10, 2013. In July 2012, the Company submitted a letter to Israel&rsquo;s Petroleum Commissioner
seeking an extension of the scheduled work program time frames which would in essence extend the Joseph License beyond the scheduled
expiration date of October 10, 2012 to accommodate unavoidable delays in seismic equipment and crews attributable to the Company&rsquo;s
contractor, Geophysical Institute of Israel (GII), having their crews in Africa for an extended period of time.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">Under the terms of the
Joseph License, as extended, the Company is required, among other things, to (i) obtain a seismic survey, process and integrate
the data and submit the process report and ancillary material to the National Geophysical Archive maintained at GII by October
15, 2012, (ii) interpret, process and integrate the results of the new seismic survey with existing seismic lines, update the geophysical
maps and submit a geophysical summary and file a report with the Israeli Petroleum Commissioner by December 15, 2012, (iii) identify
and prepare a drilling prospectus that includes a geological description of the geological background, the desired drilling depths,
a geological forecast and engineering plan for the proposed drilling by January 15, 2013, and (iv) execute a drilling contract
to drill a new well or drill the existing well by February 15, 2013.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The Company has contracted
with GII for a 20 kilometer seismic survey that it anticipates will be acquired in September 2012.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 27pt">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">At the option of the
Israeli Petroleum Commissioner, the Joseph License may be extended for additional one-year periods through October 10, 2014.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>
