<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><i>Dividend Reinvestment and Common Stock Purchase
Plan (the &#8220;Plan&#8221;)</i></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in"><span style="font-size: 10pt"></span>Our
Plan under the section <i>Obtaining Certificates and Transferring or Selling Shares</i> on page S-7 of the Prospectus Supplement reads
as follows:</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0pt 0 0pt 0.5in"><i>On receipt of a request to sell some of or all the Plan
shares, the Plan Agent will sell the shares on the open market no later than three business days after receipt of the request and will
send the proceeds less a service charge of $5 and applicable brokerage commissions of only $0.03 per share sold (e.g., if 100 shares sold,
commission is $3.00).</i></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0 0pt 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">On February 21, 2023, an Amendment to the <i>Transfer
Agency and Registrar Services Agreement</i> was executed between Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. and the American Stock Transfer &amp; Trust
Company, LLC, the Plan Agent, to change the Out-of-Pocket Pricing from the Sales of Shares at $5.00 per sale and $0.03 per share to Sales
of Shares at $5.00 per sale and $0.02 per share with an effective date of February 21, 2023. The section <i>Obtaining Certificates and
