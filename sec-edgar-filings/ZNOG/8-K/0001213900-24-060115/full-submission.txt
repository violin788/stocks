<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><b>Item 5.02 (b): Departure of a Director.</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">On July 8, 2024, Mr. John
Brown, Executive Chairman of the Board of Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;), was notified by Mr. John T. Seery of his
desire to retire from the Board of Directors. Mr. Seery has been on the Board since September 1, 2018 and served on the Compensation Committee.
Before serving on the Board, Mr. Seery had over forty years of experience in the oil and gas industry. Mr. Seery thanked Mr. Brown for
nominating him to the Board in 2018. &#8220;It has been a great opportunity for me&#8221; and he further said, &#8220;I&#8217;m at this
point resigning my position on the board. I have had great experience with Zion Oil &amp; Gas, enjoyed the office and board personal[ly].&#8221;
Mr. Brown has accepted his resignation from the Board effective on July 8, 2024.</p>
