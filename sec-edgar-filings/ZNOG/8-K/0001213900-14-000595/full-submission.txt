<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">Priority and Focus Now on Megiddo-Jezreel License Exploration</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">In our last update, we informed you that Israel's Petroleum Commissioner awarded Zion the Megiddo-Jezreel Petroleum Exploration License in December 2013.&#160;&#160;The License area is about 400 square kilometers or 98,842 acres.&#160;&#160;Our primary focus is now in this new area that appears to have all of the geologic ingredients of an active petroleum system with good potential.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">We continue to work with Geomage, an Israel-based geophysical company, to reprocess existing seismic lines in an effort to improve our subsurface imaging ability in the Megiddo-Jezreel and nearby Jordan Valley License areas. This effort will aid us in high-grading exploration leads to develop them into drillable prospects.&#160;&#160;We have almost completed reprocessing the first six seismic lines (86 kilometers) and this week instructed Geomage to reprocess another six lines (82 kilometers) for a total of <font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">12 lines (168 kilometers)</font>.&#160;&#160;We are also working with our Israel exploration staff to identify locations to <font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">acquire brand new seismic data </font>in conjunction with the Geophysical Institute of Israel.</font></div>
