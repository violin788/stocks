<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 10pt"><FONT STYLE="line-height: 115%">As 2011 draws to a close,
I have been reflecting on the past year and the progress that Zion has made during the last twelve months.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 10pt"><FONT STYLE="line-height: 115%">When I first invested in
Zion Oil &amp; Gas, I thought that I was joining a short-distance race - a sprint. I now realize that the race was long-distance
- a marathon... and to succeed in a marathon, you need both strong faith and firm resolve and determination. </FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 10pt"><FONT STYLE="line-height: 115%">After the events of the past
year, I believe that we are well along the path and I hope and pray that we will soon be turning into the final straight. With
G-d's help, I believe that we will soon see success with our oil &amp; gas exploration efforts.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 10pt"><FONT STYLE="line-height: 115%">Here are some of the events
of 2011:</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 10pt"><FONT STYLE="line-height: 115%">(i) In April 2011, we were
awarded a new petroleum exploration license, the Jordan Valley License (to the east of Zion&rsquo;s Joseph License area and Zion&rsquo;s
Asher-Menashe License area and to the south of the Sea of Galilee. It traverses south along the western Jordan River Valley).</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 10pt"><FONT STYLE="line-height: 115%">(ii) In June 2011, we reached
our target depth of approximately 19,357 feet (5,900 meters) in drilling our Ma&rsquo;anit-Joseph #3 well into the Permian geologic
layer in Northern Israel (in the Joseph License area).</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 10pt"><FONT STYLE="line-height: 115%">(iii) In June 2011, we submitted
an application to the Israeli Petroleum Commissioner&rsquo;s Office, requesting the grant of a new petroleum exploration permit
area adjacent to Zion&rsquo;s Joseph License area. Zion named the new permit application the Asher-Joseph Permit Application.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 10pt"><FONT STYLE="line-height: 115%">(iv) In July 2011, we joined
the broad-market Russell 3000&reg; Index, the U.S. small-cap Russell 2000&reg; Index, the Russell Microcap&reg; Index and the Russell
Global&reg; Index after Russell Investments (&ldquo;Russell&rdquo;) reconstituted its comprehensive set of U.S. and global equity
indexes on June 24, 2011.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 10pt"><FONT STYLE="line-height: 115%">(v) In July 2011, we were
awarded a one-year extension on our Asher-Menashe petroleum exploration license in Northern Israel.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 10pt">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0 0 10pt"><FONT STYLE="line-height: 115%">I continue to be optimistic
