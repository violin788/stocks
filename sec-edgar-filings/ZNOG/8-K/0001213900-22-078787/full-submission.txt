<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Zion Oil and Gas, Inc., a Delaware corporation, which has an active
petroleum exploration license onshore Israel on their 99,000-acre Megiddo-Jezreel license area,<span style="background-color: white">&#160;announces
the following completion of testing.</span></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Upon conclusion of the latest testing and completion procedures on
our MJ-2 well, and after a thorough analysis of the results by Zion and third-party experts, on December 7, 2022, Zion determined that
the well will not currently produce hydrocarbons in commercial quantities.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Although logs strongly indicate the presence of hydrocarbons, we have
determined the zone does not have sufficient permeability to be productive. &#160;We will set a temporary plug in the wellbore to preserve
its integrity and the opportunity to make future use of it, should circumstances and technological innovations warrant that.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Based on what we learned about the characteristics of this deep Mohilla
zone in the MJ-2 well, we are aggressively re-analyzing our logs and other data from the MJ-1 well, which was drilled on the same pad
site. Knowing this zone is structurally higher than the equivalent zone in MJ-2, we are diligently evaluating whether this zone was adequately
tested in the MJ-1 well.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Once we have completed that analysis, we may re-enter the MJ-1 wellbore,
which is cased to the bottom, and attempt a re-completion in the Mohilla zones.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">We will continue to explore the Megiddo-Jezreel license, as we are
confident that we are in an active petroleum system. We will provide material updates, when we have relevant information to share with
the public.</p>
