<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman,serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">On May 31, 2016, Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. (the
&ldquo;<B>Company</B>&rdquo;) filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the &ldquo;<B>SEC</B>&rdquo;) the Prospectus
Supplement dated May 31, relating to a &ldquo;best efforts&rdquo; offering of the Company&rsquo;s 12% Convertible Senior
Bonds (in a minimum aggregate amount of $2,500,000 up to a maximum amount of $12,000,000 (the &ldquo;<B>Offering</B>&rdquo;).
The Offering was scheduled to and did terminate on October 31, 2016.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman,serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman,serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">The minimum aggregate amount of $2,500,000 was not reached
in the Offering as of the scheduled termination date. Accordingly, all escrowed proceeds will be returned to the respective subscribers,
without interest or deduction.</P>
