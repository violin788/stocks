<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">ITEM 1.01 ENTRY INTO A MATERIAL DEFINITIVE AGREEMENT</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; TEXT-DECORATION: underline">Extension of Joseph Petroleum Exploration License</font></font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">On October 26, 2011, the Israeli Petroleum Commissioner notified Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. (the "Company" or &#8220;Zion&#8221;) that it was approving the Company&#8217;s request for a one-year extension on the Company&#8217;s Joseph License, extending the expiration date of the Joseph License until October 10, 2012.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">The Company&#8217;s &#8220;Joseph License&#8221; covers approximately 83,272 acres on the Israeli coastal plain south of the Asher-Menashe License between Caesarea in the north and Netanya in the south.&#160;&#160;The Joseph License had an initial three-year term that ran from October 10, 2007 through October 10, 2010 and was subsequently extended to October 10, 2011, and has now been extended for an additional one-year period ending October 10, 2012. At the option of the Israeli Petroleum Commissioner, the Joseph License may be extended for additional one-year periods up to 2014.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">Under the terms of the Joseph License, as extended, Zion is required (i) to submit to the Israeli Petroleum Commissioner a report analyzing the results of the last three exploratory wells that the Company drilled on the Joseph License within the Ma&#8217;anit structure and a regional geologic analysis of the Ma&#8217;anit structure by February 1, 2012, (ii)&#160;&#160;sign an agreement with an appropriate geological services provider to acquire approximately 15 kilometers of 2D seismic by March 1, 2012, (iii)&#160;&#160;perform the seismic survey, process and integrate the data and submit the process report and ancillary material to the National archives at the Geophysics Institute by June 1, 2012, (iv) process and integrate the results of the new seismic survey with existing seismic lines, update the geophysical maps and submit a geophysical summary&#160;&#160;and file a report with the Israeli Petroleum Commissioner by August 1, 2012,<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#160;</font>(v) to identify a new drilling prospect by September 1, 2012 that includes a description of the geological background, the purposes of the drilling,&#160;&#160;the petroleum system/s that are related therein and a geological forecast and engineering plan and (vi) to sign a drilling contract to drill a new well (or re-enter an existing well) by October 1, 2012.</font></div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt"><br>
</div>

<div style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman">Attached, as Exhibit 10.1, is an English translation from the original Hebrew of the extension letter.</font></div>
