<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>ITEM 1.01 ENTRY INTO A MATERIAL DEFINITIVE AGREEMENT</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B><U>Extension of Asher-Menashe Petroleum Exploration License</U></B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">On July 7, 2013, the Israeli Petroleum
Commissioner notified Zion Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. (the &quot;Company&quot; or &ldquo;Zion&rdquo;) that it was approving the Company&rsquo;s
request for an extension to the scheduled expiration date of the Company&rsquo;s Asher-Menashe License (Petroleum Exploration License
No. 334) to June 9, 2014.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Under the terms of the Asher-Menashe License,
as extended, the Company is required, among other things, to (i) transfer the rig to the Elijah #3 well (&ldquo;E-3&rdquo;) location
no later than November 1, 2013; (ii) perform a perforation and stimulation operation at E-3 at the zone within the Jurassic that
is suspected to have oil and make a production test (drill stem test); (iii) in case the outcome of the tests is negative, the
well is to be abandoned according to the approved plugging and abandonment (&ldquo;P&amp;A&rdquo;) plan immediately upon completion
of the test; and (vi) within 60 days after P&amp;A, a final and detailed report should be submitted and the license is to be returned.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>
