<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:9%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The information regarding the Restructuring Support Agreement (as defined below) set forth in Item 1.03 of this Current Report on Form <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">8-K</FONT> is incorporated into this Item 1.01 by reference. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;1.03 Bankruptcy or Receivership </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:9%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On June&nbsp;15, 2020, Pyxus International, Inc. (&#147;Pyxus&#148;) and its subsidiaries Alliance One International, LLC, Alliance One North
America, LLC, Alliance One Specialty Products, LLC and GSP Properties, LLC (collectively, the &#147;Company&#148;) filed voluntary petitions (the &#147;Chapter 11 Cases&#148;) under Chapter 11 of the United States Bankruptcy Code (the
&#147;Bankruptcy Code&#148;) with the Bankruptcy Court for the District of Delaware (the &#147;Bankruptcy Court&#148;) to implement a prepackaged chapter 11 plan of reorganization (&#147;Prepack Plan&#148;) that effectuates a financial restructuring
of the Company&#146;s secured debt (the &#147;Restructuring&#148;). The Company commenced solicitation of the Prepack Plan with a related disclosure statement (&#147;Disclosure Statement&#148;) on June&nbsp;14, 2020, and anticipates completing the
solicitation during the Chapter 11 Cases. The Company has requested that the Bankruptcy Court administer the Chapter 11 Cases jointly under the caption <I>In re Pyxus International, Inc., et al.</I> </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:9%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Company filed motions with the Bankruptcy Court seeking authorization to continue to operate its businesses as <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap"><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">&#147;debtors-in-possession&#148;</FONT></FONT> under the jurisdiction of the Bankruptcy Court and in accordance with the applicable provisions of the Bankruptcy Code and orders of the
Bankruptcy Court. To ensure its ability to continue operating in the ordinary course of business both domestically and internationally, the Company also has filed with the Bankruptcy Court a variety of &#147;first day&#148; relief motions, including
authority to pay employee wages and benefits and vendors and suppliers in the ordinary course of business. The Prepack Plan and the &#147;first day&#148; relief anticipate that vendors and other unsecured creditors who continue to work with the
Company on existing terms will be paid in full and in the ordinary course of business. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:9%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">A copy of the Prepack Plan is attached to the
Disclosure Statement included as Exhibit 99.2 hereto and is incorporated herein by reference. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>Restructuring Support Agreement </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:9%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In furtherance of the Restructuring, on June&nbsp;14, 2020, the Company entered into a Restructuring Support Agreement (the &#147;RSA&#148;)
with holders (collectively, the &#147;Consenting Noteholders&#148;) of greater than 92% of the Company&#146;s 8.500% Senior Secured First Lien Notes due 2021 (the &#147;First Lien Notes&#148;) and greater than 67% of the Company&#146;s 9.875% Senior
Secured Second Lien Notes due 2021 (the &#147;Second Lien Notes&#148;). As set forth in the RSA, including in the term sheets attached thereto (including all exhibits, annexes and schedules attached thereto, the &#147;Term Sheets&#148;), the Company
and Consenting Noteholders (collectively, the &#147;RSA Parties&#148;) agreed to the terms of the Restructuring, which is contemplated to be implemented through the Prepack Plan. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:9%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As described above, on June&nbsp;15, 2020, the Company commenced the Chapter 11 Cases to implement the Prepack Plan. </P>
