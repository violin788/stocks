<font FACE="Times New Roman PS" SIZE="5"><b>
<p align="center">American Lorain Begins Trading on NYSE Amex on Tuesday,
September 8, 2009 </p>
</b></font><font FACE="Times New Roman PSMT" SIZE="2">
<p align="justify">JUNAN COUNTY, China, September 3, 2009 -- American Lorain
Corporation (OTCBB:ALRC and NYSE AMEX:ALN) (&quot;American Lorain&quot; or the &quot;Company&quot;),
an international processed foods company based in Shandong Province, People's
Republic of China (&quot;PRC&quot;), today announced that it has received authorization to
list its common stock on the NYSE Amex Exchange and will begin trading on
Tuesday, September 8, 2009 under the ticker symbol &quot;ALN.&quot; Until then, the
Company&#146;s common stock trades on the Over the Counter Bulletin Board (&quot;OTC BB&quot;)
under the ticker symbol &quot;ALRC.OB.&quot; </p>
<p align="justify">&quot;Moving from the OTC BB to NYSE Amex is a major milestone for
American Lorain, as we strive to improve liquidity and transparency for our
shareholders. Trading on NYSE Amex is also an important endorsement of the
Company as a major player in the food processing industry in China and helps us
build credibility with our customers worldwide,&quot; said Mr. Si Chen, CEO and
Chairman of American Lorain. &quot;We are excited and honored to join the NYSE Amex
group of companies and expect to attract additional interest from the investment
community following our listing on this senior exchange. We wish to thank our
management team, dedicated employees, shareholders and loyal customers for their
ongoing support.&quot; </p>
<p align="justify">&quot;We are pleased to welcome the listing of American Lorain
Corporation, a major processed foods company, on NYSE Amex,&quot; said Michael Yang,
Chief Representative of Beijing Office, NYSE Euronext. &quot;We look forward to a
lasting partnership with the company and its shareholders.&quot; </p>
<p align="justify">About American Lorain Corporation <br>
American Lorain Corporation (&quot;American Lorain&quot; or the &quot;Company&quot;) is a Delaware
corporation that develops, manufactures and sells various food products. The
Company's products include chestnut products; convenience foods products
(including ready-to-cook foods, ready-to-eat foods, and meals-ready-to-eat); and
frozen, canned and bulk foods products. The Company currently sells over 234
products to 26 provinces and administrative regions in China as well as to 42
foreign countries. The Company operates through its four direct and indirect
subsidiaries and one leased factory located in China. For more information about
American Lorain, please visit our website at <font color="#0000FF"><u>http://www.americanlorain.com
.. </u></font></p>
