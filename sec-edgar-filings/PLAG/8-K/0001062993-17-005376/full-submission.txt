<P align=justify><B>Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement</B>
</P>
<P align=justify style="text-indent:5%">On December 28, 2017, American Lorain Corporation (the
&#147;<B>Company</B>&#148;) entered into a Securities Purchase Agreement (the &#147;<B>Purchase
Agreement</B>&#148;), pursuant to which Yi Li and Beili Zhu, each an individual
residing in the People&#146;s Republic of China, agreed to invest an aggregate of
$1.275 million in the Company (the &#147;<B>Financing</B>&#148;) in exchange for an
aggregate of 7,500,000 shares (the &#147;<B>Shares</B>&#148;) of the Company&#146;s common
stock, par value $0.001 per share, representing a purchase price of $0.17 per
Share. The Purchase Agreement contains customary representations and warranties
by the Company and customary closing conditions. The Company expects to use the
proceeds of the Financing for general corporate purposes. </P>

<P align=center><B>American Lorain Corporation Has Entered into Agreement for
Financing</B> </P>
<P align=justify>LINYI CITY, China, December 28, 2017 /PRNewswire/ --<B>
American Lorain Corporation</B> (the "<U>Company</U>") (NYSE MKT: ALN) announced
on December 28, 2017, that it has entered into a securities purchase agreement
pursuant to which Yi Li and Beili Zhu, each an individual residing in the
People&#146;s Republic of China, agreed to invest an aggregate of $1.275 million in
the Company (the &#147;<U>Financing</U>&#148;) in exchange for an aggregate of 7,500,000
shares (the &#147;<U>Shares</U>&#148;) of the Company&#146;s common stock, par value $0.001 per
share, representing a purchase price of $0.17 per Share. The Purchase Agreement
contains customary representations and warranties by the Company and customary
closing conditions. The Company expects to use the proceeds of the Financing for
general corporate purposes. </P>
