Yunchu Supply Chain Co., Ltd. (&#8220;<b>Target</b>&#8221;), and each of shareholders of the Target (collectively, the &#8220;<b>Sellers</b>&#8221;),
pursuant to which, among other things and subject to the terms and conditions contained therein, the Subsidiary agreed to effect an acquisition
of the Target by acquiring from the Sellers 100% of the outstanding equity interests of the Target (the &#8220;<b>Acquisition</b>&#8221;).
The target is a regional leading beef products importer and distributor in China.</p>
