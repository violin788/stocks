<P align=justify><B>Item 1.01. Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement</B>
</P>
<P align=justify style="text-indent: 5%">On April 14, 2018, American Lorain Corporation (the
&#147;<B>Company</B>&#148;) entered into a Securities Purchase Agreement (the &#147;<B>Purchase
Agreement</B>&#148;), pursuant to which eight individuals residing in the People&#146;s
Republic of China agreed to invest an aggregate of approximately $1.6 million in
the Company (the &#147;<B>Financing</B>&#148;) in exchange for an aggregate of 9,050,000
shares (the &#147;<B>Shares</B>&#148;) of the Company&#146;s common stock, par value $0.001 per
share, representing a purchase price of $0.18 per Share. The Purchase Agreement
contains customary representations and warranties by the Company and customary
closing conditions. The Company expects to use the proceeds of the Financing for
general corporate purposes. </P>
