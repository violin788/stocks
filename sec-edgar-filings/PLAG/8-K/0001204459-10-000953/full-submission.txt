<P align=center><font size="5">American Lorain Corporation Launches &#147;Lunch Box&#148; Products<BR>in
550 <I>7-Eleven&#174;</I> Convenience Stores</font><font size="2"><BR></font></P>
<P align=justify><B><font size="2">JUNAN COUNTY, China</font></B><font size="2">, April 28, 2010 -- American Lorain
Corporation (NYSE Amex: ALN) ("American Lorain" or the "Company"), an
international processed snack foods and convenience food manufacturer based in
the Shandong Province, China today announced that 550 7-Eleven convenience
stores in Guangzhou, China began selling Lorain&#174;-branded &#147;Lunch Box&#148;
products.</font></P>
<P align=justify><font size="2">American Lorain entered into a supplier agreement with 7-Eleven
in March 2010 and for the last month has executed a roll-out of its
ready-to-heat-and-eat &#147;Lunch Box&#148; products to 550 7-Eleven stores located in the
city of Guangzhou, China. Lorain&#146;s &#147;Lunch Box&#148; is a rice-based meal which
includes a protein and a vegetable as part of the packaged meal. The entire meal
is sealed and sterilized using Lorain&#146;s proprietary technology that preserves
product freshness and flavor. Consumers heat the meal at work or home in a
microwave oven, open the package and eat the food. Consumers can choose from a
variety of Lorain&#174;- branded &#147;Lunch Box&#148; products and meal flavors in the
refrigerated aisles of 7-Eleven stores. </font> </P>
<P align=justify><font size="2">In additional to &#147;Lunch Box&#148; products, American Lorain has
several additional convenience type foods currently under review by 7-Eleven
including; chestnuts, pickled vegetables and candied beans. The Company&#146;s
candied beans product was launched recently with a nationwide advertising
campaign featuring Taiwanese actor Youpeng &#147;Alec&#148; Su.</font></P>
<P align=justify><font size="2">&#147;We anticipate favorable results from the &#147;Lunch Box&#148; rollout
to 7-Eleven stores in Guangzhou and hope to expand our product offerings to
7-Eleven as our relationship grows,&#148; said American Lorain Chairman and CEO Si
Chen. &#147;7-Eleven has always been a target customer for us because of their strict
product quality requirements, convenience shopping model and its large customer
base. We expect that selling our products at 7-Eleven convenience stores will
help drive incremental revenue growth, strengthen our brand awareness and
increase our popularity amongst Chinese convenience food shoppers.&#148; Chen
concluded. </font> </P>

<P align=justify><B><font size="2">About American Lorain Corporation </font> </B></P>
<P align=justify><font size="2">American Lorain Corporation is a Nevada corporation that
develops, manufactures and sells various food products. The Company's products
include chestnut products, convenience food products and frozen food products.

regions in China as well as to 42 foreign countries. The Company operates
through its four direct and indirect subsidiaries and one leased factory located
in China. For further information about American Lorain Corporation, please
