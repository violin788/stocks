<P align=justify><B>Item 3.02. Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities.</B> </P>
<P
align=justify>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As
previously reported in the Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q filed on August 14,
2018 (the &#147;<B>Form 10-Q</B>&#148;) by Planet Green Holdings Corp. f/k/a American
Lorain Corporation (the &#147;<B>Company</B>&#148;), on August 8, 2018, the Company
entered into an amended and restated securities purchase agreement with Yimin
Jin, the Company&#146;s chief strategy officer and director, and Hongxiang Yu, the
Company&#146;s chairman, chief executive officer and president (collectively, the
&#147;<B>Purchasers</B>&#148;), pursuant to which the Purchasers agreed to invest an
aggregate of $10 million in the Company in exchange for an aggregate of
2,352,942 shares (on a post-reverse stock split basis) of the Company&#146;s common
stock (the &#147;<B>Shares</B>&#148;), representing a purchase price of approximately
$4.25 per share (the &#147;<B>Financing</B>&#148;). </P>
<P
align=justify>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;On October 16, 2018, the Company closed the Financing,
which was approved at the Company's 2018 annual meeting of stockholders held on
September 26, 2018. At the closing, the Company received gross proceeds of
$10,000,000 in the aggregate, in exchange for the issuance of the Shares. </P>
<P
align=justify>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The
issuance of the Shares was exempt from the registration requirements of the
Securities Act, pursuant to Section 4(a)(2) of the Securities Act of 1933, as
amended, and Rule 506 of Regulation D promulgated thereunder. </P>

<P align=center><B>PLANET GREEN HOLDINGS CORP. CLOSES PREVIOUSLY ANNOUNCED
FINANCING TRANSACTION </B></P>
<P align=justify>&nbsp;</P>
<P align=justify>SHANGHAI, China, October 17, 2018 /PRNewswire/-- Planet Green
Holdings Corp. (the &#147;Company&#148;) (NYSE American: PLAG) announced today that on
October 16, 2018 the Company closed the private placement transaction (the
&#147;Transaction&#148;), pursuant to the agreement entered into on August 8, 2018 with
Yimin Jin, the Company&#146;s chief strategy officer and director, and Hongxiang Yu,
the Company&#146;s chairman. At the closing, the Company received gross proceeds of
$10,000,000 in the aggregate, in exchange for the issuance of an aggregate of
2,352,942 shares of the Company&#146;s common stock, representing a purchase price of
approximately $4.25 per share. The Transaction had been approved at the
Company&#146;s 2018 annual meetings of stockholders held on September 26, 2018.</P>
