<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><b>Item 2.01 Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets.</b></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">As previously reported, on
April 8, 2022, Planet Green Holdings Corp. (the &#8220;<b>Company</b>&#8221;) entered into a Share Exchange Agreement (the &#8220;<b>Share
Exchange Agreement</b>&#8221;) with Allinyson Ltd., a company incorporated in the State of Colorado (&#8220;<b>Target</b>&#8221;), and
each of shareholders of the Target (collectively, the &#8220;<b>Sellers</b>&#8221;), pursuant to which, among other things and subject
to the terms and conditions contained therein, the Company agreed to effect an acquisition of the Target by acquiring from the Sellers
100% of the issued and outstanding ordinary shares of the Target (the &#8220;<b>Acquisition</b>&#8221;). The target develops and operates
online games, and sells advertising placements.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">On April 18, 2022, the Parties
completed the transaction. Upon the closing of the transaction, the Company acquired 100% shares issued and outstanding ordinary shares
of the Target and the Company issued 7,500,000 shares of common stock to the Sellers.</p>
