<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0"><B>Item 3.02. Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">As
previously reported on Form 8K filed on April 27, 2021 by Planet Green Holdings Corp. (the &ldquo;<B>Company</B>&rdquo;), on April 26,
2021, the Company entered into a Securities Purchase Agreement with three individuals residing in the People&rsquo;s Republic of China
(collectively, the &ldquo;<B>Purchasers</B>&rdquo;), pursuant to which the Purchasers agreed to invest an aggregate of $7,600,000 in the
Company in exchange for an aggregate of 4,000,000 shares (the &ldquo;<B>Shares</B>&rdquo;) of the Company&rsquo;s common stock, representing
a purchase price of $1.90 per share (the &ldquo;<B>Financing</B>&rdquo;). The closing of the Financing was subject to customary closing
conditions of this type of transaction.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">On May 20, 2021, the Company
closed the Financing. At the closing, the Company received gross proceeds of $7,600,000 in the aggregate, in exchange for the issuance
of the Shares.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-right: 0; margin-left: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">The
issuance of the Shares was exempt from the registration requirements of the Securities Act, pursuant to Section 4(a)(2) of the Securities
Act of 1933, as amended.</P>
