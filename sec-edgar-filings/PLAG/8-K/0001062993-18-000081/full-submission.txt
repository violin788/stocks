<P align=center><B>American Lorain Receives Notice of Non-compliance from NYSE
<BR>American </B></P>
<P align=justify>LINYI CITY, China, January 5, 2018 /PRNewswire/ -- <B>American
Lorain Corporation (the "Company")</B> (NYSE MKT: ALN) announced today that the
Company has received notice from the NYSE American that the Company no longer
satisfies Section 704 of the NYSE American Company Guide due to the Company&#146;s
failure to hold an annual meeting for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2016 on
or before December 31, 2017.</P>
<P align=justify>As disclosed via a Current Report on Form 8-K dated December
28, 2017, the Company circulated a proxy statement and attempted to hold an
annual meeting for 2016 on December 27, 2017; however, it failed to obtain the
requisite quorum and therefore determined to postpone the meeting. </P>
<P align=justify>In accordance with the requirements under SEC Rule 14a-3(b),
the Company now plans to hold a combined annual meeting for 2016 and 2017
subsequent to the filing of the Company&#146;s audited financial statements for the
fiscal year ended December 31, 2017.</P>
