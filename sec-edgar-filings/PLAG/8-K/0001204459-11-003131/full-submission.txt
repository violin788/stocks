<P align=center><B><font size="3">American Lorain Corporation Reports 2011 Third Quarter

<P align=center><B>- Company to Hold Conference Call Wednesday, November 16,

<P align=justify>JUNAN COUNTY, China, Nov. 15, 2011 /PRNewswire-Asia-FirstCall/
-- <B>American Lorain Corporation </B>(NYSE Amex: ALN)<B> </B>("American Lorain"
or the "Company"), an international processed snack foods, convenience foods,
and frozen foods company based in the Shandong Province, China, today announced
financial results for its third quarter ended September 30, 2011. </P>
<P align=justify><B>Q2 2011 Operational Highlights </B></P>
<UL style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify">
  <LI>Chestnut and convenience food sales increased by 15.2% and 20.1%
  year-over-year, respectively
  <LI>Convenience food segment continued to grow as a percent of total revenue
  year-over-year
  <LI>Frozen food increased at a slower rate of 7.2%, and represented lower
  weight in total sales year-over-year
  <LI>Continued channel and brand building efforts </LI></UL>
<P align=justify><B>Q2 2011 Financial Highlights</B></P>
<UL style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify">
  <LI>Total revenues of $55.6 million, an increase of 15.7% year over year
  <LI>Gross margins decreased slightly to 22.2%, compared to 22.4%
  year-over-year and 22.7% at 12/31/2010
  <LI>Net income attributable to common stockholders of $7.1 million, up 39.1%
  year-over-year
  <LI>Diluted earnings per share of $0.20 </LI></UL>

<P align=justify>American Lorain's Chairman and CEO, Mr. Si Chen, stated, "We
are satisfied with the Company's performance in the third quarter, during which
we have again achieved solid growth in each of our three business lines, despite
the tightened credit environment and inflation pressure. We believe the largest
contributor to our growth in the fourth quarter will be the continued expansion
of our convenience foods segment, as well as the chestnut food segment which is
seasonally strong in fourth quarter. We believe the continuous focus on product
quality and building our distribution channels and brand equity is key to the
Company's success in a competitive landscape and will continue to execute on
these strategies in the coming months." </P>

  three months ended September 30, 2011 from $5.1 million in the same period of
  last year. Please refer to note 22 to the financial statements for details. </LI></UL>
