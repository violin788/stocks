<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-align: center"><B>Planet Green Holdings Corp Subsidiary Allinyson
Ltd. Forges Strategic Partnership<BR>
 with MetaMind AI Limited on AI Technology Solutions</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: left">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">NEW YORK, October 10, 2023 /PRNewswire/ -- Planet
Green Holdings Corp. (&ldquo;Planet Green&rdquo;, the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;) (NYSE American: PLAG) a provider of consumer products, chemical
products, and online advertising and mobile games on the Chinese and North American markets, announced today that its subsidiary Allinyson
Ltd. (&ldquo;Allinyson&rdquo;) has achieved an one-year-term groundbreaking strategic partnership with MetaMind AI Limited (&ldquo;MetaMind&rdquo;),
aiming to revolutionize the landscape of artificial intelligence (AI) technology services. This collaboration, established through a Cooperation
Agreement, is set to drive innovation, and propel both companies into the forefront of the AI industry.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Under the Cooperation Agreement, Allinyson and MetaMind
mutually acknowledge each other as strategic partners and grant permission to establish partner logo links or text links on their respective
websites. Both parties also authorize their partner to repost relevant information from the other party&rsquo;s website on their sites, with
proper attribution. In addition, Allinyson and MetaMind collaborate to jointly promote and endorse each other&rsquo;s brands both online and
offline, including exhibitions, seminars, forums, and brochures. MetaMind will also provide application programming interface and technical
support tailored to meet Allinyson&rsquo;s specific business requirements.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Mr. Bin Zhou, Chairman and CEO of Green Planet Holdings
Corp., expressed enthusiasm for this collaboration, stating, &ldquo;The strategic partnership with MetaMind marks a significant advancement
in promotion of our AI capabilities and services. We believe this partnership will underscore our unwavering commitment to innovation
of AI-driven technologies in the game product development and intelligent gaming experiences, as we previously unveiled our breakthrough
product, ChatAlpha - the next generation integrated AI conversational robot, which is set to transform the way game players engage with
AI-powered assistants. &ldquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>About Allinyson Ltd.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Allinyson Ltd., a company incorporated in the State
of Colorado and a wholly owned subsidiary of Planet Green Holdings Corp., is committed to delivering innovative and captivating gaming
experiences. A prominent player in the artificial intelligence solutions industry, Allinyson focuses on leveraging advanced technologies,
and consistently pushes the boundaries of game and interactive tools development, creating high-quality games that captivate players world
widely. For more information about Allinyson&rsquo;s games products, please visit: http://www.allinyson.com.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>About Planet Green Holdings Corp</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Based in Flushing, New York, Planet Green Holdings
Corporation (NYSE American: PLAG) is a Nevada holding company that has been in operation since 1994. Over the years, The Company has expanded
its portfolio from consumer products to a well-diversified range of offerings, including energy products and AI-driven services. The Company&rsquo;s
reach has extended to both the Chinese and North American markets. For more information, please visit: https://planetgreenholdings.com/.</P>
