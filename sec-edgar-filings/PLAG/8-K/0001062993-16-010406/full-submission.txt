<P align=justify><B>Item 5.07 Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security
Holders </B></P>
<P align=justify>On June 30, 2016 at 10:00 a.m. local time, American Lorain
Corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;) held its 2016 Annual Meeting of Stockholders
(&#147;Annual Meeting&#148;) at its corporate office located at Beihuan Zhong Road, Junan
County, Shandong, People&#146;s Republic of China, 276600. There were 38,259,490
shares of common stock outstanding and entitled to vote at the meeting and
23,446,382 shares were represented at the meeting in person or by proxy,
constituting a quorum. The voting results are presented below.</P>
