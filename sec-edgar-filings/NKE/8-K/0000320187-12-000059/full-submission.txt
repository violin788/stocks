Parker, Chief Executive Officer and President of the Company.&#160;&#160;The grant consists of 189,682 RSUs.&#160;&#160;Each RSU obligates the Company upon vesting of the RSU to issue to the RSU holder one share of NIKE Class B Common Stock plus a cash payment equal to the total amount of dividends paid per share between grant and vesting of the RSU.&#160;&#160;One hundred percent of the RSUs are scheduled to vest on May 18, 2017, subject to Mr. Parker&#8217;s continued employment with the Company through such date.&#160;&#160;In addition, the RSUs will become 100% vested upon (i) the earlier death or disability of Mr. Parker, or (ii) if a &#8220;change in control&#8221; of the Company occurs and within two years after the change in control, Mr. Parker&#8217;s employment is terminated by the Company without &#8220;cause&#8221; or by him for &#8220;good reason.&#8221;&#160;&#160;The terms &#8220;change in control,&#8221; &#8220;cause&#8221; and &#8220;good reason&#8221; have the same meanings for purposes of the RSUs as they have in the Company&#8217;s current form of Non-Statutory Stock Option Agreement.&#160;&#160;Shares acquired under the RSUs are subject to the Company&#8217;s Policy for Recoupment of Incentive Compensation.</font></div>
