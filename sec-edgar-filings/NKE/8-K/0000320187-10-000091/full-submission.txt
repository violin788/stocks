has lasted or is expected to last for a continuous period of 12 months or more and that, in the opinion of the Company and two independent physicians, causes the Optionee to be unable to perform duties as an employee, director, officer or consultant of the Employer and unable to be engaged in any substantial gainful activity.&#160;&#160;Total disability shall be deemed to have occurred on the first day after the two independent physicians have furnished their written opinion of total disability to the Company
