        a total of nine directors and appointed Lorinda Burgess as a Class III director of AngioDynamics, effective immediately.</div>

      <div style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><br />
      </div>

      <div style="text-align: left; text-indent: 22.5pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt;">There are no arrangements or understandings between Ms. Burgess and any other person pursuant to which she was elected as a director. There are no
        transactions in which Ms. Burgess has an interest requiring disclosure under Item 404(a) of Regulation S-K.</div>

      <div style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><br />
      </div>

      <div style="text-align: left; text-indent: 22.5pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt;">Ms. Burgess was also appointed to the Audit Committee and the Compensation Committee of the Board.&#160; The Board has determined that Ms. Burgess is
        independent under the Nasdaq listing standards.</div>

      <div style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><br />
      </div>

      <div style="text-align: left; text-indent: 22.5pt; font-family: 'Times New Roman'; font-size: 10pt;">On July 19, 2023, the Board also approved a change to its non-employee director compensation program, which effective immediately will apply to Ms.
        Burgess and all other directors as modified. AngioDynamics&#8217; non-employee directors will continue to receive compensation as described in AngioDynamics&#8217; definitive proxy statement filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on September 22,
        2022, except that the annual equity grant payable to all non-employee directors will now be fully vested at the time of grant.</div>

      <div style="font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><br />
