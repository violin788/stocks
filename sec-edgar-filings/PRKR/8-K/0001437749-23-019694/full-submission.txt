<div style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt 0pt 0pt 8pt;">On July 6, 2023, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors (&#8220;Committee&#8221;) of ParkerVision, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) approved cash bonuses for its named executive officers. Jeffrey Parker, the Company&#8217;s chief executive officer, was awarded a cash bonus equal to 25 percent of his annual salary, or $71,000. Cynthia French, the Company's chief financial officer, was awarded a cash bonus equal to 25 percent of her annual salary, or $45,000. The bonuses were awarded in consideration of, among other things, each of the executive's contributions to the Company's successful intellectual property protection and monetization program and their continuation of approximately 20% voluntary base salary reductions since 2019. &#xa0;</div>
