<font style="font-weight: bold; font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">Item
1.01.Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">&#xA0;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: left; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">On
January 5, 2021, the Company entered into securities purchase
agreements (the &#x201C;<font style="text-decoration: underline">Purchase Agreements</font>&#x201D;)
with the accredited investors identified on Exhibit 10.3 hereof
(the &#x201C;<font style="text-decoration: underline">Investors</font>&#x201D;) for the sale
of an aggregate of 2,976,430 shares (&#x201C;<font style="text-decoration: underline">Shares</font>&#x201D;) of the
Company&#x2019;s common stock, par value $0.01 per share, at a price
of $0.35 per share for aggregate proceeds of $1,041,750. The
Purchase Agreements also provide the Investors with a contingent
payment right whereby the Company will pay each Investor an
allocated portion of the Company&#x2019;s net proceeds from its
patent claims, after taking into account fees and expenses payable
to law firms representing the Company and amounts payable to the
Company&#x2019;s litigation financer. The Investor&#x2019;s allocated
portion of such net proceeds will be determined by multiplying (i)
the net proceeds recovered by the Company up to $10 million by (ii)
the quotient of such Investor&#x2019;s subscription amount divided
by $10 million, up to an amount equal to each Investor&#x2019;s
