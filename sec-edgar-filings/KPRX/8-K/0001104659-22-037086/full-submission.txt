<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">On March 18, 2022, Michael Meluzio resigned as
the Corporate Controller of Kiora Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) to pursue other opportunities. Mr. Meluzio had served
as the Company&#8217;s principal financial officer and principal accounting officer on an interim basis beginning on February 25, 2022.
Mr. Meluzio&#8217;s resignation did not result from any disagreement regarding the Company&#8217;s operations, policies or practices.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Effective as of March 18, 2022, Brian M. Strem,
Ph.D., the Company&#8217;s President and Chief Executive Officer, was appointed as the Company&#8217;s principal financial officer and
principal accounting officer. The Company has engaged Danforth Advisors, LLC to provide interim finance and accounting services until
a permanent replacement is found.</p>
