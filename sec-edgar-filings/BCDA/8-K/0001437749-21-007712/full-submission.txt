<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt;"><i>Annual Meeting of Stockholders</i></p>

<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;text-align:justify;margin:0pt;text-indent:36pt;">BioCardia, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) announces that its Board of Directors (the &#8220;Board&#8221;) has established Tuesday, June 15, 2021 as the date for the next Annual Meeting of Stockholders of the Company (the &#8220;Annual Meeting&#8221;), to be held at the Company&#8217;s principal executive offices located at 125 Shoreway Road, Suite B, San Carlos, California 94070. The Board also established the close of business on April 20, 2021 as the record date for the determination of stockholders entitled to receive notice of and to vote at the Annual Meeting. The time and attendance instructions of the Annual Meeting will be set forth in the Company&#8217;s proxy statement for the Annual Meeting, to be electronically filed prior to the Annual Meeting with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (&#8220;SEC&#8221;).</p>

<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt;"><i>Stockholder Nomination of Directors</i></p>

<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;text-align:justify;margin:0pt;text-indent:36pt;">Because the Annual Meeting will be held more than 30 days from the anniversary date of the Company&#8217;s last annual meeting of stockholders on December 22, 2020, in accordance with Rule 14a-5(f) under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the &#8220;Exchange Act&#8221;), the Company is hereby informing its stockholders of the following:</p>

<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;text-align:justify;margin:0pt;text-indent:36pt;">For stockholders who desire to submit a proposal for consideration at the Annual Meeting and wish to have such proposal included in the Company&#8217;s proxy statement, the Company has set a new deadline for the receipt of such proposals in accordance with Rule 14a-8 under the Exchange Act. In order to be considered timely, the proposal must be received at the Company&#8217;s principal executive offices no later than April 16, 2021, which the Company has determined is a reasonable time before the Company begins to print and mail its proxy materials. Such stockholder proposals must also comply with the other requirements of Rule 14a-8 of the Exchange Act.</p>

<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;text-align:justify;margin:0pt;text-indent:36pt;">In addition, if a stockholder of the Company intends to nominate a person for election to the Board at the Annual Meeting or who intends to submit a proposal regarding any other matter of business at the Annual Meeting but who does not intend for such proposal to be included in the Company&#8217;s proxy materials, the deadline for submitting the notice of such nomination or other proposal is the close of business on April 16, 2021. Any notice should be delivered to BioCardia, Inc., 125 Shoreway Road, Suite B, San Carlos, California 94070, Attention: Secretary. Any such notice must also comply with the requirements of Delaware law, the rules and regulations promulgated by the SEC and our amended and restated bylaws, as applicable.</p>
