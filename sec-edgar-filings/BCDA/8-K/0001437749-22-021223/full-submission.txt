<div style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt;"><b>Item 7.01 Regulation FD Disclosure.</b></div>

<div style="font-family:'Times New Roman';font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt;">&#xa0;</div>

<div style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt;">On August 24, 2022, BioCardia, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) issued a press release announcing it has entered into an agreement with BlueRock Therapeutics LP for the delivery of BlueRock&#8217;s cell therapy product candidates for the treatment of heart failure utilizing BioCardia&#8217;s minimally invasive biotherapeutic delivery product candidates.
