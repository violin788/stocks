<p style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;text-indent:17.25pt;">On December 24, 2018, BioCardia, Inc. (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;) entered into a Securities Purchase Agreement (the &ldquo;Securities Purchase Agreement&rdquo;) with entities affiliated with the Stertzer Family Trust and with Frost Gamma Investments Trust, our largest beneficial owners (the &ldquo;Investors&rdquo;), relating to an offering and sale (the &ldquo;Offering&rdquo;) of an aggregate of 5,333,332 shares (the &ldquo;Shares&rdquo;) of the Company&rsquo;s common stock, par value $0.001 per share (the &ldquo;Common Stock&rdquo;), at a purchase price of $0.75 per share, and warrants (the &ldquo;Investor Warrants&rdquo;), to purchase up to one-half of the number of shares of Common Stock sold to an Investor, up to an aggregate for all Investors of 2,666,666 shares of Common Stock (the &ldquo;Warrant Shares&rdquo;) at an exercise price of $0.75 per share, for aggregate gross proceeds of $4 million. The Investor Warrants will expire on December 24, 2023. The Investor Warrants contain customary adjustments and are exercisable immediately for cash and after six months will also be exercisable on a cashless basis if there is no effective registration statement registering the resale of the Warrant Shares. The Investors do not have registration rights in connection with any securities purchased in the Offering. The closing of the Offering took place on December 24, 2018.</p>
