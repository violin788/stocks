<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;text-align:justify;margin:0pt;"><b>Sunnyvale, Calif.</b>&nbsp;&#8211;<b> September 18, 2024 -</b>&nbsp;<u>BioCardia, Inc.</u> [Nasdaq: BCDA], a global leader in cellular and cell-derived therapeutics for the treatment of cardiovascular and pulmonary diseases, announced today that the Company has regained full compliance with the Nasdaq Capital Market&#8217;s Listing Requirements as required by the Hearing Panel&#8217;s (the &#8220;Panel&#8221;) decision on May 13, 2024.</p>
