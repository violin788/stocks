<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;"><b>SAN CARLOS, Calif. &#8211; </b><b>February 17</b><b>, 2021</b><b>&nbsp;&#8211;&nbsp;</b><u>BioCardia&#174;, Inc.</u>&nbsp;[Nasdaq: BCDA] a leader in the development of comprehensive solutions for cardiovascular regenerative therapies,&nbsp;today announced clinical leadership changes.</p>

<p style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;">Dr. Sujith Shetty who has been with the Company for almost two years in the capacity of Vice President of Clinical and Regulatory will assume the role of Chief Medical Officer, replacing Dr. Eric Duckers, who resigned as Chief Medical Officer of the Company to pursue other opportunities, effective as of February 15th, 2021. Simultaneous with his resignation, Dr. Duckers entered into a consulting agreement to continue to provide support for the ongoing CardiAMP Heart Failure Trial and its leadership team.</p>

<p style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;">Ms. Debby Holmes-Higgin who has been with the Company for more than three years will continue to serve as Vice President of Clinical with expanded responsibilities.</p>

<p style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;">Both Dr. Shetty and Ms. Holmes-Higgin will be supported by Dr. Hans-Peter Stoll, who recently assumed the role of consulting Medical Monitor for the CardiAMP clinical study. Dr. Stoll has extensive clinical experience having been most recently the Chief Medical Officer at Biosensors International, and served in key senior clinical roles at GE Healthcare, and Cordis, Johnson &amp; Johnson.</p>

<p style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;">&#8220;Evolution of the clinical team after the recent successful Data Safety Monitoring Board Review of the CardiAMP Heart Failure Trial is an important step for the success of the ongoing trial,&#8221; said Peter Altman, Ph.D., BioCardia&#8217;s President and CEO. &#8220;Dr. Shetty and Ms. Holmes-Higgin are world class executives in whom we have every confidence, and I am delighted that they will be supported both by Dr. Stoll as Medical Monitor and Dr. Duckers on a consulting basis.&#8221;</p>

<p style="background-color:#FFFFFF;font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;"><b>About BioCardia&#174;</b></p>

<p style="background-color:#FFFFFF;font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;"><u>BioCardia, Inc.</u>, headquartered in San Carlos, California, is developing regenerative biologic therapies to treat cardiovascular and respiratory disease.
