<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt;">StemCardia, Inc., is developing cell and gene therapies engineered to repair the heart. CardiAMP autologous and CardiALLO allogeneic cell therapies are the Company&#8217;s biotherapeutic platforms for the treatment of heart disease. BioCardia also works with partners to provide its proprietary Helix transendocardial biotherapeutic delivery system, as well as technology and services for the development and commercialization of partners&#8217; therapeutic agents.</p>
