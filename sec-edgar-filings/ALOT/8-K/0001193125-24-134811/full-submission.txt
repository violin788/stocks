<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>West Warwick, R.I., May&nbsp;9, 2024 &#151;</B> AstroNova, Inc. (Nasdaq: ALOT) (&#147;AstroNova&#148; or the &#147;Company&#148;), a global leader in data
visualization technologies, today announced that on May&nbsp;6, 2024, it acquired MTEX NS, S.A. for a total enterprise value of &#128;24.3&nbsp;million. Additionally, the transaction provides for up to a &#128;4&nbsp;million earnout payable to the
seller over three years based on the achievement of specified revenue growth targets. AstroNova expects the transaction to add an additional $8&nbsp;million to $10&nbsp;million in revenue in this fiscal year. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;MTEX NS is a highly innovative manufacturing and technology development company whose multiple lines of industrial grade products significantly expand

&#147;Recently, MTEX NS introduced a breakthrough printer for <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap"><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">direct-to-film</FONT></FONT> (DTF) transfers that enables printing on a wide variety of materials including fabrics,
leather, and hard-surface materials. The acquisition also further expands our sustainable packaging capabilities, such as recycled cardboard printing and environmentally friendly, water-based inks. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;The MTEX NS team possesses broad and deep inkjet and UV piezo technology experience, allowing the company to consistently develop and deliver highly
differentiated, color digital printers that provide customers with industry-leading speed and quality,&#148; Woods said. &#147;This experience will add to and complement our own technical expertise.&#148; </P>
