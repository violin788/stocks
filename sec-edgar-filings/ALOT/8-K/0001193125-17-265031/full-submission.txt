Inc. (NASDAQ: ALOT), a global leader in data visualization technologies, today reported total revenue of $27.5 million for the fiscal 2018 second quarter ended July&nbsp;29, 2017, up from $25.3 million for the same period a year earlier. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;The revenue growth in the second quarter was dominated by our Product Identification segment,&#148; said AstroNova President and CEO Greg Woods.
&#147;The segment recorded a record $20.8 million in revenue for the quarter, as both our printer hardware and supplies product lines achieved double digit growth. The Product Identification revenue growth was derived from both organic growth in our
QuickLabel product lines as well as contributions from our acquisition of TrojanLabel earlier this year. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;Our Test&nbsp;&amp; Measurement segment
reported lower year-over-year revenues at $6.6 million for the second quarter of this year, as deferrals in the release of Aerospace avionic printer orders tempered the second quarter&#146;s growth profile. The delay was partly offset by increased
demand for our T&amp;M recorder line of products, which gained traction as a result of new sales and marketing initiatives deployed this year,&#148; Woods said. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;AstroNova&#146;s second quarter revenue growth is also traceable to our international channels where revenues of $10.3 million grew 34.2 percent from
the prior year&#146;s second quarter,&#148; Woods continued. &#147;The addition of new dealers in Asia and Latin America and the opening of a new office in India have contributed to that top-line growth. Another important recent step was the
formation of a Wholly Owned Foreign Enterprise in Shanghai&#146;s free trade zone, which makes our import and export activity in China more efficient and cost effective.&#148; </P>

<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Gross margin in the second quarter of fiscal 2018 was 37.3 percent compared with 40.7 percent for the same period in fiscal 2017, partially as a result of
integrating the TrojanLabel and traditional AstroNova businesses, as well as changes in product mix and factory absorption. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Second-quarter fiscal 2018
operating expenses were $9.3 million versus $8.6 million for the comparable period of last year, due primarily to the addition of TrojanLabel operating expenses, including amortization of the related identifiable intangibles, increased investments
in new products and higher selling expenses related to strong international sales. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Net income was $0.7 million, or $0.11 per diluted share, in the second
quarter of fiscal 2018 compared with net income of $1.3 million, or $0.17 per diluted share, in the year-earlier period. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Bookings in the second quarter
of 2018 increased 15.5 percent to $27.8 million from $24.1 million in the same period last year. The year-over-year increase in bookings was evident in both the Product Identification and Test&nbsp;&amp; Measurement segments. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Company&#146;s backlog increased 3.8 percent from the end of the first quarter of fiscal 2018 to $18.9 million as of July&nbsp;29, 2017. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Cash and marketable securities were $14.0 million at quarter-end, compared with $24.8 million at the end of fiscal 2017, primarily reflecting the repurchase
of AstroNova shares during the second quarter. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Board Declares Quarterly Dividend </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On August&nbsp;21, 2017, the Board of Directors of AstroNova, Inc. declared a regular quarterly cash dividend of $0.07 per share. The dividend, which
