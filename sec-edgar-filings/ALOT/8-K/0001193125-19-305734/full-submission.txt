announced financial results for the fiscal 2020 third quarter ended November&nbsp;2, 2019. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><U>CEO Commentary </U></B></P>

AstroNova President and CEO Greg Woods. &#147;In Product Identification, similar to the second quarter, we experienced some softness primarily as a result of weaker demand from a few customers in Asia. In spite of these short-term challenges, we
believe that the underlying fundamentals in both businesses remain strong. We continue to invest in product innovation, strengthen our global marketing organization, and enhance our Aerospace service capabilities to drive and support future growth.

&#147;We&#146;re also particularly excited about the new TrojanLabel<SUP STYLE="font-size:85%; vertical-align:top">&reg;</SUP> T5, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">all-in-one</FONT> digital print and finish

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><U>Business Outlook </U></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;Our overall outlook remains positive, and we continue to expect modest revenue growth in fiscal 2020,&#148; Woods said. &#147;While the combination of

business.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><U>Operating Segment Results </U></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Product Identification</B> segment revenue in the third quarter of fiscal 2020 was $21.7&nbsp;million, consistent with the prior-year period. Segment
operating income was $1.9&nbsp;million, or 8.6% of revenue, versus $2.0&nbsp;million, or 9.3% of revenue, in the prior year. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Test</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;&amp; Measurement</B> segment revenue in the third quarter of fiscal 2020 was $11.6&nbsp;million compared with $12.5&nbsp;million
in the same period of fiscal 2019. Segment operating income was $1.4&nbsp;million, or 12.1% of revenue, for the third quarter of fiscal 2020 compared with $3.2&nbsp;million, or 25.4% of revenue, in the comparable period of fiscal 2019. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Hardware revenue was $12.2&nbsp;million, compared with $13.1&nbsp;million in the prior-year period. Supplies revenue was $17.7&nbsp;million, down 2.5% from
$18.1&nbsp;million in the same period of fiscal 2019. Service/other revenue was $3.5&nbsp;million, up 17.1% from same period a year earlier. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><U>Third-Quarter Financial Summary </U></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Revenue totaled
$33.3&nbsp;million versus $34.2&nbsp;million in the third quarter of fiscal 2019, reflecting lower sales in the Test&nbsp;&amp; Measurement segment. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Gross profit was $12.3&nbsp;million, or 36.9% of revenue, compared with $13.9&nbsp;million, or 40.7% of revenue, for Q3 fiscal 2019. The decrease in gross
profit dollars and margin was attributable primarily to lower revenue and less favorable sales mix.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Operating expenses were
$11.9&nbsp;million compared with $11.5&nbsp;million in the same period of fiscal 2019. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Operating income was $0.4&nbsp;million, or 1.3% of revenue,
compared with $2.4&nbsp;million, or 6.9% of revenue, in the third quarter of fiscal 2019. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Net income was $0.5&nbsp;million, or $0.06 per diluted share,
versus net income of $1.4&nbsp;million, or $0.20 per diluted share, in the third quarter of fiscal 2019. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Bookings were $32.6&nbsp;million compared with
$34.2&nbsp;million in the third quarter of 2019. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Backlog at November&nbsp;2, 2019 was $20.8&nbsp;million, compared with $24.5&nbsp;million at the end of
the fiscal 2019 third quarter. </P>
