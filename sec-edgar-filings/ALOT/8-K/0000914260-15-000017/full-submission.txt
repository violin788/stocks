<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 10pt; FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><font style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">WEST WARWICK, R.I., March 18, 2015 </font>-- Astro-Med, Inc. (NASDAQ: ALOT), a leading manufacturer of data visualization technology products for the specialty printing and test &amp; measurement markets, today reported financial results for the fiscal 2015 fourth quarter and full year ended January 31, 2015.</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 10pt; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#8220;Strong demand throughout our business drove double-digit increases in net sales for the fourth quarter and full year of fiscal 2015,&#8221; said Astro-Med President and Chief Executive Officer Gregory A. Woods. &#8220;QuickLabel Systems increased approximately 15% and 22% for the quarter and full-year periods, respectively, while our Test &amp; Measurement segment posted corresponding growth of 51% and 46%.&#8221;</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 10pt; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#8220;In 2015 we also continued to make significant strides in key strategic areas such as new product development, geographic expansion and manufacturing efficiencies,&#8221; Woods continued. &#8220;Through focused investments in technology, marketing and personnel, we continue to put the pieces in place to generate sustained margin improvement and profitable growth.&#8221;</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 10pt; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Net sales for the three months ended January 31, 2015 increased 24.5% to $22.1 million from $17.7 million for the same period of fiscal 2014. Net sales for the year ended January 31, 2015 were $88.3 million, an increase of 28.8% from $68.6 million for the prior-year period.</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; MARGIN-BOTTOM: 10pt; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Net income under Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2015 was $543,000, or $0.07 per diluted share. GAAP net income for the corresponding period of fiscal 2014 was $1.9 million, or $0.24 per diluted share, which included $1.5 million, or $0.19 per diluted share, from discontinued operations.&#160; Excluding discontinued operations, net income for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2014 would have been $399,000, or $0.05 per diluted share.</div>
