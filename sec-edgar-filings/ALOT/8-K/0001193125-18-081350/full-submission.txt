<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Financial Summary </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Revenue in the fourth quarter totaled
$32.7&nbsp;million, an increase of 27% from $25.7&nbsp;million in the fourth quarter of fiscal 2017, reflecting higher sales in both of the Company&#146;s segments &#150; Product Identification and Test&nbsp;&amp; Measurement. For the full year,
revenue increased 15.2% to a record $113.4&nbsp;million from $98.4&nbsp;million in fiscal 2017. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Gross profit for the fourth quarter was
$12.6&nbsp;million, or 38.7% of revenue, compared with $10.1&nbsp;million, or 39.3% of revenue, for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2017. The increase in gross profit dollars reflected higher revenue in the 2018 period, while the decrease in gross
margin percentage was due to expenses associated with the Company&#146;s asset purchase and licensing agreement with Honeywell International&#146;s Aerospace division in September 2017. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Fiscal 2018 gross profit was $44.0&nbsp;million, or 38.8% of revenue, compared with $39.5&nbsp;million, or 40.1% of revenue, in fiscal 2017. Lower gross
margin percentage in fiscal 2018 reflected the Honeywell transaction as well as expenses associated with the Company&#146;s February 2017 acquisition of Trojanlabel ApS. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Operating expenses for the fourth quarter were $10.5&nbsp;million compared with $8.9&nbsp;million in the same period of fiscal 2017. The increase was due
primarily to higher selling and marketing and R&amp;D expenses associated with the introduction of new products in Product Identification and Test&nbsp;&amp; Measurement. Fiscal 2018 operating expenses increased to $38.6&nbsp;million from
$33.2&nbsp;million a year earlier. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Operating income for the fourth quarter was $2.1&nbsp;million, or 6.5% of revenue, compared with $1.2&nbsp;million, or
4.5% of revenue, in the fourth quarter of fiscal 2017, reflecting higher revenue in the 2018 period. Operating income for fiscal 2018 was $5.4&nbsp;million, or 4.8% of revenue, compared with $6.3&nbsp;million, or 6.4% of revenue, in fiscal 2017,
primarily reflecting the effect of integration expenses in the 2018 period. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">GAAP net income for the fourth quarter was $628,000, or $0.09 per diluted
share, down from $766,000, or $0.10 per diluted share, in the fourth quarter of fiscal 2017. GAAP net income for fiscal 2018 was $3.3&nbsp;million, or $0.47 per diluted share, compared with $4.2&nbsp;million, or $0.56 per diluted share, in fiscal
2017. Results for both the fourth-quarter and full-year periods of 2018 were negatively impacted by $1.1&nbsp;million, or $0.16 per share, in taxes due to the enactment of the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act. Of this total, $1.0&nbsp;million was related to
revaluation of deferred tax assets at the new lower corporate tax rate and $104,000 was related to the Transition Tax on <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">un-repatriated</FONT> earnings of foreign subsidiaries. On a
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-GAAP</FONT> basis, excluding this impact, net income for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2018 was $1.7&nbsp;million, or $0.25 per diluted share, while net income for full-year fiscal 2018 was $4.4&nbsp;million, or
$0.63 per diluted share. </P>

<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>About AstroNova </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">AstroNova, Inc. (NASDAQ: ALOT), a global
leader in data visualization technologies, designs, manufactures, distributes and services a broad range of products that acquire, store, analyze and present data in multiple formats. The Product Identification segment offers a variety of hardware
and software products and associated supplies that allow customers to mark, track and enhance the appearance of their products. The segment&#146;s two business units are QuickLabel<SUP STYLE="font-size:85%; vertical-align:top">&reg;</SUP>, the
industry leader in tabletop digital color label printing and TrojanLabel&#153;, a leader in the light-production color label press and specialty printer markets. The Test and Measurement segment includes the Test&nbsp;&amp; Measurement business
unit, which offers a suite of products and services that acquire, record and analyze electronic signal data from local and networked sensors. The segment also includes the Aerospace business unit, which makes printers, networking hardware and
related accessories. These products are used in the aircraft flight deck to print flight plans, navigation information and performance data and in the aircraft cabin to print maintenance data, receipts and passenger manifests. AstroNova is a member
of the Russell Microcap<SUP STYLE="font-size:85%; vertical-align:top">&reg;</SUP> Index and the LD Micro Index (INDEXNYSEGIS: LDMICRO). Additional information is available by visiting <U>www.astronovainc.com</U>. </P>
