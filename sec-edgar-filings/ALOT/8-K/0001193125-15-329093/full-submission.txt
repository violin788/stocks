on a worldwide basis, effective immediately. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;As we developed our plans to modernize the company and transform it into a business that could deliver

references our strong roots in the aerospace business. &#145;Nova&#146; comes from the Latin word &#145;novus,&#146; meaning &#145;new.&#146; Our new brand emphasizes our traditional strengths in aerospace, along with our expanding presence in
test&nbsp;&amp; measurement, product identification, and other new areas where we can apply our data visualization technology,&#148; Woods said. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The
Company&#146;s business includes: </P> <P STYLE="font-size:6pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
