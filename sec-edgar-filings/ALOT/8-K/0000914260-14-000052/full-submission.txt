<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">WEST WARWICK, RI &#8211; May 22, 2014</font> <font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">&#8211;</font> Astro-Med, Inc. (NASDAQ: ALOT), a leading manufacturer of specialty high-tech printing systems and data acquisition products, today reported net sales of $20.8 million for the three months ended May 3, 2014, an increase of 34.2% from the same period a year earlier.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#8220;Strong demand in both our QuickLabel Systems (QLS) and Test &amp; Measurement (T&amp;M) segments drove excellent results for Astro-Med in the first quarter of fiscal 2015,&#8221; said Gregory A. Woods, the Company&#8217;s President and Chief Executive Officer. &#8220;QLS product sales increased 27% in the first quarter to $14.4 million. Our QLS products continue to benefit from a targeted marketing program that is accelerating new customer growth.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#8220;T&amp;M segment sales grew 55% to $6.4 million,&#8221; added Woods. &#8220;A portion of our T&amp;M growth can be attributed to our acquisition of the Miltope ruggedized aerospace printer business in January, but we also saw increasing demand for high-speed data acquisition systems &#8211; our TMX and TMX-18 products, in particular.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#8220;We posted first-quarter orders of $23.5 million, a 42% increase over last year,&#8221; Woods said. &#8220;Both the QuickLabel Systems and Test &amp; Measurement segments reported double-digit increases. We exited the quarter with a record backlog of $16.2 million.</font></div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#8220;We continue to focus on expanding our global sales channel to complement our direct presence in the United States, Canada and Western Europe,&#8221; said Woods. &#8220;In the first quarter we added salespeople in both Europe and North America. One of our sales channel expansion goals for fiscal 2015 is to establish a direct presence in Asia, where we see additional opportunity for growth. We expect to have an office up and running by the end of this fiscal year to serve customers in China and Southeast Asia.&#8221;</font></div>
