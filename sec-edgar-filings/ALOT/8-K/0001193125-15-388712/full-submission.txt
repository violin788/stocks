OFFICERS; COMPENSATORY ARRANGEMENTS OF CERTAIN OFFICERS </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On November&nbsp;22, 2015,&nbsp;April Ondis, 41, was appointed to the Board of Directors of
Astro-Med, Inc. (the &#147;Issuer&#148;).</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Under the terms of the Issuer&#146;s Non-Employee Director Annual Compensation Program, upon her appointment to

restricted stock award with a value equal to $20,000 granted to each non-employee director. The restricted stock vests on the earlier of 12 months after the grant date or the date immediately prior to the next annual meeting (or special meeting in
lieu of annual meeting) of shareholders. In addition, under the terms of the Issuer&#146;s 2015 Equity Incentive Plan, Ms.&nbsp;Ondis was granted non-qualified options to purchase 5,000 shares of the Issuer&#146;s common stock in connection with her
initial election to the board. Such options vest immediately prior to the next annual meeting of the Issuer. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Ms.&nbsp;Ondis is the daughter of the late
Mr.&nbsp;Albert&nbsp;W. Ondis, the Issuer&#146;s founder. As a result of her appointment as a co-executor of the Estate of Albert W. Ondis (the &#147;Estate&#148;), Ms.&nbsp;Ondis is deemed to have acquired beneficial ownership of the shares of the
Issuer&#146;s common stock held by the Estate. On December&nbsp;4, 2014, the Issuer entered into a Stock Repurchase Agreement with Ms.&nbsp;Ondis and the other co-executors of the Estate to repurchase 500,000 shares of the Issuer&#146;s common stock
at a per share price of $12.50, for an aggregate purchase price of $6,250,000. The transaction was consummated on December&nbsp;5, 2014. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">A copy of the
Issuer&#146;s press release regarding the appointment of Ms.&nbsp;Ondis to the Board of Directors is attached hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;99.1 and is incorporated by reference. </P> <P STYLE="font-size:18pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>AstroNova Elects April Ondis to its Board of Directors </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">WEST WARWICK, RI &#150; November&nbsp;24, 2015 &#150; Astro-Med, Inc., d/b/a AstroNova (NASDAQ: ALOT) announced that April Ondis has been elected to its Board
of Directors. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Ms.&nbsp;Ondis, 41, has been the Digital Content Marketing Manager at Crossref, a not-for-profit membership organization for scholarly
