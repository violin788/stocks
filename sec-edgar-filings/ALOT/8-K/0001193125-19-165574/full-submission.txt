<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>West Warwick, R.I. &#150; June</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;5, 2019</B> &#150; AstroNova, Inc. (NASDAQ: ALOT), a global leader in data visualization technologies,
today announced financial results for the fiscal 2020 first quarter ended May&nbsp;4, 2019. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><U>CEO Commentary </U></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We executed well in the first quarter, generating strong year-over-year gains in revenue, operating income and earnings per share,&#148; said President
and CEO Greg Woods. &#147;Our financial and operational performance reflects the strategic investments we are making in new product development, technology enhancements and geographic expansion, all driven by our AstroNova Operating System (AOS).
Through the AOS standard set of tools and processes, we are realizing increased efficiencies, enhancing operating leverage and driving growth to the bottom line. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;Both segments contributed to our positive results in Q1,&#148; Woods said. &#147;In Product Identification, key business drivers included demand for our
new, five-color <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">QL-300</FONT> tabletop printer, which allows commercial printers and other customers to expand their capability to produce high-quality, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">short-run</FONT> specialty
labels across a range of industries. In the Test&nbsp;&amp; Measurement segment, our results reflected solid momentum in our key end markets.&#148; </P>

<Center><DIV STYLE="width:8.5in" align="left">

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"> AstroNova Reports Q1 FY 2020 Financial Results/

 </P> <p STYLE="margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt ; font-size:8pt">&nbsp;</P>
 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><U>Business Outlook </U></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;For fiscal 2020 we continue to expect modest revenue growth and increased operating leverage for the full year as we expand the application of the
AstroNova Operating System across our business,&#148; Woods said. &#147;However while so far we have been largely unaffected by the grounding of the Boeing 737 MAX, we expect that the prolonged situation may result in a slight impact on our
second-quarter operating margin. Whether or not this impact continues beyond the second quarter is dependent on when the aircraft is returned to service.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><U>Operating Segment Results </U></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Product
Identification</B> segment revenue in the first quarter of fiscal 2020 was $23.6&nbsp;million, an 18.2% increase compared with $20.0&nbsp;million in the prior year, on higher sales of hardware, supplies and services. Segment operating income was
$2.9&nbsp;million, or 12.2% of revenue, versus $1.7&nbsp;million, or 8.3% of revenue, in the prior year, primarily reflecting higher revenue and improved operating leverage. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Test</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;&amp; Measurement</B> segment revenue in the first quarter of fiscal 2020 increased 9.2% to $12.6&nbsp;million from
$11.5&nbsp;million in the same period of fiscal 2019. Segment operating income was $2.6&nbsp;million, or 20.5% of revenue, for the first quarter of fiscal 2020 compared with $2.3&nbsp;million, or 19.6% of revenue, in the comparable period of fiscal
2019. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><U>Financial Summary </U></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Revenue in the first
quarter totaled $36.2&nbsp;million, an increase of 14.9% from $31.5&nbsp;million in the first quarter of fiscal 2019, reflecting higher sales in both the Product Identification and Test&nbsp;&amp; Measurement segments. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Gross profit in the first quarter was $14.2&nbsp;million, or 39.4% of revenue, compared with $12.1&nbsp;million, or 38.5% of revenue, for Q1 fiscal 2019. The
increase in gross profit dollars and margin reflected higher revenue in the 2020 period. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Operating expenses for the first quarter were $11.8&nbsp;million
compared with $10.8&nbsp;million in the same period of fiscal 2019. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Operating income for the first quarter was $2.5&nbsp;million, or 6.8% of revenue,
compared with $1.3&nbsp;million, or 4.0% of revenue, in the first quarter of fiscal 2019. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Net income for the first quarter was $1.7&nbsp;million, or
$0.23 per diluted share, versus $814,000, or $0.12 per diluted share, in the first quarter of fiscal 2019. </P>

<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">AstroNova, Inc. (NASDAQ: ALOT), a global leader in data visualization technologies since 1969, designs, manufactures, distributes and services a broad range of
products that acquire, store, analyze and present data in multiple formats. The Product Identification segment offers a variety of hardware and software products and associated supplies that allow customers to mark, track and enhance the appearance

STYLE="font-size:85%; vertical-align:top">&reg;</SUP>, an innovative leader for professional label presses and specialty printers. The Test and Measurement segment includes the Test&nbsp;&amp; Measurement business unit, which offers a suite of
products and services that acquire, record and analyze electronic signal data from local and networked sensors. The segment also includes the AstroNova Aerospace business unit, which designs and manufactures avionics equipment and systems that serve
the world&#146;s aerospace and defense industries with proven advanced airborne technology solutions for both the cockpit and the cabin. The key products include flight deck printers, networking hardware and related accessories. AstroNova is a
member of the Russell Microcap<SUP STYLE="font-size:85%; vertical-align:top">&reg;</SUP> Index and the LD Micro Index (INDEXNYSEGIS: LDMICRO). Additional information is available by visiting www.astronovainc.com. </P>
