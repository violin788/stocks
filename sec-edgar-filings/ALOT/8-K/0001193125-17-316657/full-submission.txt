<TD WIDTH="12%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left"><B>Item&nbsp;3.01.</B></TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Notice of Delisting or Failure to Satisfy a Continued Listing Rule or Standard; Transfer of Listing. </B></TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On October&nbsp;6, 2017, AstroNova, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) notified The NASDAQ Stock Market LLC (&#147;Nasdaq&#148;) that with the
passing of Dr.&nbsp;Hermann Viets on September&nbsp;30, 2017, the number of directors comprising the Company&#146;s board of directors was reduced from seven to six, three of whom are independent directors, and that as a result, the Company is no
longer compliant with Nasdaq Listing Rule 5605(b)(1), which requires that a majority of the board of directors of a Nasdaq-listed company be comprised of&nbsp;independent&nbsp;directors. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On October&nbsp;19, 2017, the Company received a letter from Nasdaq acknowledging the Company&#146;s
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-compliance</FONT> with Nasdaq&#146;s&nbsp;independent&nbsp;director requirements as set forth in Nasdaq&#146;s Listing Rule 5605. The Nasdaq letter further provides that, consistent with Nasdaq&#146;s Listing
Rule 5605(b)(1)(A), Nasdaq will provide the Company a cure period in order to regain compliance as follows: (1)&nbsp;until the earlier of the Company&#146;s next annual shareholders&#146; meeting or October&nbsp;1, 2018; or (2)&nbsp;if the next
annual shareholders&#146; meeting is held before March&nbsp;29, 2018, then the Company must evidence compliance no later than March&nbsp;29, 2018. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The board of directors intends to identify candidates to replace Dr.&nbsp;Viets and appoint a new independent director who satisfies the
applicable requirements of the Nasdaq Listing Rules to serve on the Company&#146;s board of directors as soon as it is practical and prior to the expiration of the cure period. </P>
