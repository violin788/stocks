<p style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;">Q1&#8217;20 operating expenses declined to $1.7 million from $1.8 million, reflecting the benefit of cost reduction efforts. BIO-key&#8217;s preliminary first quarter results include $0.5M in non-cash compensation expense that will be allocated to each expense category in the final results filed on form 10-Q.</p>

<p style="font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;">Reflecting the substantial improvement in gross profit and the decrease in operating expenses, BIO-key&#8217;s operating loss was reduced to $1.3M in Q1&#8217;20 versus $1.8M in Q1&#8217;19.
