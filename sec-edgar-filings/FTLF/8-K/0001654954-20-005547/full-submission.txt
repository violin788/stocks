<font style="color: #000000; font-weight: bold; font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">
FitLife Brands Announces First Quarter 2020 Results</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">&#xA0;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: left; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">OMAHA,
NE &#x2013; May 15, 2020 -- FitLife Brands, Inc.
(&#x201C;FitLife&#x201D; or the &#x201C;Company&#x201D;) (OTC Pink:
FTLF), an international provider of innovative and proprietary
nutritional supplements for health-conscious consumers marketed
under the brand names NDS Nutrition&#x2122;, PMD&#xAE;,
SirenLabs&#xAE;, CoreActive&#xAE;, Metis Nutrition&#x2122;,
iSatori&#x2122;, Energize, and BioGenetic Laboratories, today
announced results for the three months ended March 31,
2020.</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">&#xA0;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: left; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-weight: bold; font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">
Highlights for the quarter ended March 31, 2020
include:</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">&#xA0;</font></div>
<div style="margin-left: 24px; display: table">
<div style="display: table-row">
<div style="text-align: left; display: table-cell; width: 24px">
<font style="font-size: 13px">&#x25CF;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; display: table-cell"><font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">Total revenue
increased 4.6% to $6.2 million.</font></div>
</div>
</div>
<div><font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 10">&#xA0;</font></div>
<div style="margin-left: 24px; display: table">
<div style="display: table-row">
<div style="text-align: left; display: table-cell; width: 24px">
<font style="font-size: 13px">&#x25CF;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; display: table-cell"><font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">Direct-to-consumer
online sales increased to 14% of total revenue, compared to 10% in
the same quarter last year.</font></div>
</div>
</div>
<div><font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 10">&#xA0;<br></font></div>
<div style="margin-left: 24px; display: table">
<div style="display: table-row">
<div style="text-align: left; display: table-cell; width: 24px">
<font style="font-size: 13px">&#x25CF;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; display: table-cell"><font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">Gross profit
improved 7.7% to $2.7 million.</font></div>
</div>
</div>
<div><font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 10">&#xA0;<br></font></div>
<div style="margin-left: 24px; display: table">
<div style="display: table-row">
<div style="text-align: left; display: table-cell; width: 24px">
<font style="font-size: 13px">&#x25CF;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; display: table-cell"><font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">Gross margin
increased to 44.5% compared to 43.2% in the same quarter last
year.</font></div>
</div>
</div>
<div><font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 10">&#xA0;<br></font></div>
<div style="margin-left: 24px; display: table">
<div style="display: table-row">
<div style="text-align: left; display: table-cell; width: 24px">
<font style="font-size: 13px">&#x25CF;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; display: table-cell"><font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">Net income
increased 20.3% to $1.4 million.</font></div>
</div>
</div>
<div><font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 10">&#xA0;<br></font></div>
<div style="margin-left: 24px; display: table">
<div style="display: table-row">
<div style="text-align: left; display: table-cell; width: 24px">
<font style="font-size: 13px">&#x25CF;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; display: table-cell"><font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">Net income per
share increased to $1.36 per share, or $1.27 per diluted share,
compared to $1.07 per share, or $0.94 per diluted share, in the
same quarter last year.</font></div>
</div>
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 10">&#xA0;</font>
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px"><br>
</font></div>
<div style="text-align: left; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="color: #000000; font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">
For the first quarter ended March 31, 2020, total revenue was $6.2
million versus $5.9 million in the same quarter last year, an
increase of 4.6%. The increase was primarily attributable to
continued growth in our online direct-to-consumer business. During
the first quarter of 2020, online sales accounted for approximately
14% of the Company&#x2019;s revenue, compared to 10% during the
first quarter of 2019.</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">&#xA0;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: left; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="color: #000000; font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">
Gross profit improved to $2.7 million, an increase of 7.7% from the
first quarter of 2019. Gross margin improved from 43.2% to 44.5%
over the same time period. The improvement in gross margin was
driven by product mix and higher online sales volumes.</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">&#xA0;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: left; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="color: #000000; font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">
Total operating expenses increased 5.8% from $1.3 million to $1.4
million, driven by an increased investment in sales and
marketing.</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">&#xA0;</font></div>
<div style="text-align: left; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="color: #000000; font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">
Net income for the first quarter of 2020 was $1.4 million, an
increase of 20.3% over the same quarter in 2019. The Company
delivered basic earnings per share of $1.36 in the first quarter of
2020, compared to $1.07 in the same quarter last year. Diluted
earnings per share increased from $0.94 in the first quarter of
last year to $1.27 this year.</font></div>
<div style="text-align: justify; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; text-indent: 0px">
<font style="font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">&#xA0;</font></div>

Dayton Judd, the Company&#x2019;s Chairman and CEO, commented
&#x201C;I am very pleased with the Company&#x2019;s performance
during the first quarter, especially given the disruption that
began in mid-March due to the COVID-19 pandemic. That said, given
the uncertainty brought on by the current environment, I know our
stakeholders are more interested in how the business is performing
during the second quarter. Therefore, to be as transparent as
possible, the Company provides the following information, not
subject to any procedures by our Independent Registered Public
Accounting Firm, regarding its performance and position as of May
14, 2020.&#x201D;</font></div>

<div style="text-align: justify; display: table-cell"><font style="color: #000000; font-family: Times New Roman; font-size: 13px">Total

<div style="margin-left: 24px; display: table">
