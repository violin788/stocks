<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><i>Departure of Director</i></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><i>&#160;</i></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in"><span style="background-color: white">On September
1, 2023, Marc Tessier-Lavigne, Ph.D. notified Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Regeneron</span>&#8221; or the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Company</span>&#8221;)
of his decision to retire, effective as of that date, as a director of the Company. Dr. Tessier-Lavigne informed Regeneron that his decision
to retire was due to potential conflicts that may arise as he becomes more involved with other companies and was not caused in whole or
in part by a disagreement with Regeneron on any matter relating to Regeneron&#8217;s operations, policies, or practices.</span></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><i>Departure of Chief Financial Officer </i></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in; background-color: white">On September 8, 2023,
Robert E. Landry notified the Company of his intention to retire from his position as Executive Vice President, Finance and Chief Financial
Officer of the Company in February 2024. Mr. Landry has served as Regeneron&#8217;s principal financial officer since 2013. The Company
intends to appoint Christopher Fenimore, currently the Company&#8217;s Senior Vice President, Controller, to succeed Mr. Landry as Chief
Financial Officer of the Company effective upon Mr. Landry&#8217;s retirement.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><i>Election of Directors</i></p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: left; text-indent: 0.25in; background-color: white">On September 8, 2023,
Regeneron&#8217;s board of directors (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Board</span>&#8221;) elected Kathryn Guarini, Ph.D. as a Class III director and David
P. Schenkein, M.D. as a Class II director to fill the two vacancies on the Board. Drs.&#160;Guarino and Schenkein have also been appointed
to serve on the Audit Committee of the Board and the Technology Committee of the Board, respectively. In accordance with the requirements
of the New York Business Corporation Law, each of Drs.&#160;Guarino and Schenkein will hold office until the 2024 Annual Shareholder Meeting
of the Company.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in; background-color: white">Each of Drs.&#160;Guarino
and Schenkein will receive compensation from the Company for their service as a director in accordance with the Company&#8217;s compensation
practices for non-employee directors, as described under the caption &#8220;Compensation of Directors&#8221; in the Company&#8217;s definitive
proxy statement filed on April&#160;21, 2023.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&#160;</p>
