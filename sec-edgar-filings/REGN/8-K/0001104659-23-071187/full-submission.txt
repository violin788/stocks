<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in"><span style="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, Serif">On
June 9, 2023, the Board of Directors (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Board</span>&#8221;) of Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. </span>(&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Regeneron</span>&#8221;
or the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Company</span>&#8221;), based upon the recommendation of the Corporate Governance and Compliance Committee of the Board,
approved an amendment (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Amendment</span>&#8221;) to Regeneron&#8217;s Amended and Restated By-Laws required in connection with
the implementation of the previously adopted Board leadership succession plan, as disclosed in the Company&#8217;s Current Report on Form
8-K filed April 17, 2023 and the Company&#8217;s definitive proxy statement on Schedule 14A (under &#8220;Board Governance&#8212;Board
Leadership Structure&#8212;Expected Future Board Leadership Structure&#8221;) filed April 21, 2023 (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Proxy Statement</span>&#8221;).
The Amendment provides that the Board from time to time may elect two persons to serve as Co-Chairs of the Board and that, if there are
Co-Chairs, either Co-Chair may exercise the full powers and authorities of the office.</p>
