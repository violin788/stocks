<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in">Regeneron Pharmaceuticals,&#160;Inc. (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Regeneron</span>&#8221;
or the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Company</span>&#8221;) currently expects that its financial results calculated in accordance with U.S. generally accepted
accounting principles (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">GAAP</span>&#8221;) and its non-GAAP financial results for the third quarter 2023 will include an acquired
in-process research and development (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">IPR&amp;D</span>&#8221;) charge of $100 million on a pre-tax basis. This charge relates to
a development milestone payment that became due upon the achievement of specified clinical proof-of-principle criteria for the Phase 1 ALN-APP
program in early-onset Alzheimer&#8217;s disease in connection with the Company&#8217;s collaboration with Alnylam Pharmaceuticals,&#160;Inc.
This acquired IPR&amp;D charge is expected to negatively impact each of GAAP and non-GAAP net income per diluted share for the third quarter
of 2023 by approximately $0.78.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in">Acquired IPR&amp;D charges may include IPR&amp;D
acquired in connection with asset acquisitions as well as up-front, opt-in, and certain development milestone payments related to collaboration
and licensing agreements. Regeneron does not forecast such acquired IPR&amp;D charges due to the uncertainty of the future occurrence,
magnitude, and timing of these transactions in any given period.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in">Regeneron&#8217;s results for the third quarter
2023 have not been finalized and are subject to Regeneron&#8217;s financial statement closing procedures. There can be no assurance that
actual results will not differ from the preliminary (unaudited) estimates described herein.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in">&#160;</p>
