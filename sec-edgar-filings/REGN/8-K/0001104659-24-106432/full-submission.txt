<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in">Regeneron Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Regeneron</span>&#8221;
or the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Company</span>&#8221;) currently expects that its financial results calculated
in accordance with U.S. generally accepted accounting principles (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">GAAP</span>&#8221;)
and its non-GAAP financial results for the third quarter 2024 will include an acquired in-process research and development (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">IPR&amp;D</span>&#8221;)
charge of approximately $56 million on a pre-tax basis. This charge relates to development milestone and up-front payments in connection
with collaboration and licensing agreements. This acquired IPR&amp;D charge is expected to negatively impact each of GAAP and non-GAAP
net income per diluted share for the third quarter 2024 by approximately $0.43.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in">Acquired IPR&amp;D charges may include IPR&amp;D
acquired in connection with asset acquisitions as well as up-front, opt-in, certain development milestone payments, and premiums paid
on equity securities related to collaboration and licensing agreements. Regeneron does not forecast such acquired IPR&amp;D charges due
to the uncertainty of the future occurrence, magnitude, and timing of these transactions in any given period.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.25in">Regeneron&#8217;s results for the third quarter 2024 have not been finalized and are subject to Regeneron&#8217;s financial statement
