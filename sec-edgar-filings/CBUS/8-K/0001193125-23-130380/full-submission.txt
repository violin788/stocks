<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Calyxt Reports First Quarter 2023 Financial Results and Provides Corporate Update </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>&#151;Proposed merger with Cibus Global Expected to Close in Q2 2023&#151;</I> </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap"><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">&#151;1-for-10</FONT></FONT> reverse stock split executed&#151; </I></P>

STYLE="font-size:75%; vertical-align:top">&#153;</SUP> and BioFactory<SUP STYLE="font-size:75%; vertical-align:top">TM</SUP> technologies&#151; </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Roseville, MN &#150; May 1, 2023 &#150;</B> Calyxt, Inc. (Nasdaq: CLXT), a plant-based synthetic biology company, today announced operating and financial
results for its first quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2023. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><I>Merger Agreement with Cibus</I> </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On January&nbsp;17, 2023,&nbsp;Calyxt announced it had entered into a definitive merger agreement (the Merger Agreement) with Cibus Global, LLC (Cibus), a
leader in precision gene editing in agriculture, under which Calyxt and Cibus will merge in an <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">all-stock</FONT> transaction. The merger will create a new industry-leading company that combines the two pioneers in
agriculture-based gene editing and establishes one of the world&#146;s most sophisticated facilities for trait development and next-generation plant breeding. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On April&nbsp;18, 2023, Calyxt announced that it has scheduled a special meeting of stockholders (the Special Meeting) on May&nbsp;18, 2023, at 9:00 a.m.
Central Time to seek approval of the merger from such stockholders. Calyxt&#146;s stockholders of record as of April&nbsp;12, 2023, are entitled to vote at the Special Meeting. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Boards of Directors of both companies have unanimously approved the transaction. Concurrently with the execution of the Merger Agreement, certain of
Calyxt&#146;s stockholders and Calyxt&#146;s directors and officers, who as of January&nbsp;13, 2023, collectively beneficially held voting power of approximately 49.8% of outstanding Calyxt common stock, and certain of Cibus&#146; equity holders
and Cibus&#146; directors and officers, who as of January&nbsp;13, 2023, collectively held voting power of approximately 54.3% of the outstanding voting Cibus membership units, entered into support agreements agreeing to vote their shares or units,
as applicable, in favor of the Merger Agreement and the transactions contemplated by it. </P>
