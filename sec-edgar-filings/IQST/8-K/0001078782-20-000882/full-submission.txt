<P align=justify style='font:10pt Times New Roman;margin:0'>&nbsp;</P>
<P align=justify style='font:10pt Times New Roman;margin:0'>New York, NY &#8211;&nbsp;November 16, 2020 &#8211;&nbsp;iQSTEL, Inc. (USOTC: IQST), <FONT style=color:#1D2228;background-color:#FFFFFF>an international telecommunication and fintech service provider</FONT>, today announced reporting $29 million in revenue for the nine months ended September 30, 2020 &#8211;&nbsp;a 133% increase over the $12 million reported for the same period the previous year. IQST posted with the SEC its Form 10-Q including results of operations for the 3<SUP>rd</SUP> quarter of the 2020 fiscal year on Friday, November 13, 2020.</P>
<P align=justify style='font:10pt Times New Roman;margin:0'>&nbsp;</P>
<P align=justify style='font:10pt Times New Roman;margin:0'>In addition to reporting dramatic revenue growth, the company&#8217;s shareholder base has increased and now exceeds 2,000. Despite the fact that the Company still recorded losses for the three and nine months ended September 30, 2020 and 2019,the bottom line is improving and moving toward profitability showing a loss of only $0.01. </P>

<P align=justify style='font:10pt Times New Roman;margin:0'>Now that the company has published its Q3 earnings report, Goldman Small Cap Research plans to issue an update to its recent Analyst Research Report published October 27<SUP>th</SUP> with a $0.54 Target PPS following IQST. </P>
