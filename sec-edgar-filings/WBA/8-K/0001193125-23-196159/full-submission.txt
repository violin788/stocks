<P STYLE="margin-top:20pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">DEERFIELD, Ill. &#150; July&nbsp;27, 2023 &#150;&nbsp;Walgreens Boots Alliance, Inc. (Nasdaq: WBA) today announced the departure of James Kehoe, Executive Vice

<P STYLE="margin-top:20pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Mr.&nbsp;Mahajan joined Walgreens Boots Alliance in 2016 where he has held several global senior roles in finance, including Vice President, Assistant Global
