<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">24 November 2023: Walgreens Boots Alliance (Nasdaq: WBA) today announces that, with the financial support of Boots, the Trustee of the Boots Pension Scheme
