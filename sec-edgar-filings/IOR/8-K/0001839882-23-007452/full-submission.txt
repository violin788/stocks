<P STYLE="font: 11pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center"><B>Income Opportunity Realty Investors, Inc. reports
Earnings for Quarter Ended December&nbsp;31, 2022</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.25in">DALLAS (March&nbsp;24, 2023) -- Income Opportunity
Realty Investors, Inc. (NYSE American:IOR) is reporting its results of operations for the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2022. For the
three months ended December&nbsp;31, 2022, we reported net income attributable to common shares of $1.3 million or $0.30 per diluted share
compared to a net income of $0.7 million or $0.17 per share for the same period in 2021. Our increase in net income is attributable to
an increase in interest income due to an increase in interest rates in 2022.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.25in">&nbsp;</P>

ownership and partnerships. For more information, visit the Company&#8217;s website at www.incomeopp-realty.com.</P>
