<P STYLE="font: 11pt/115% New times roman,serif; margin-top: 0; text-align: center; margin-bottom: 10pt"><B>Income
Opportunity Realty Investors, Inc. Reports Second Quarter 2021 Results</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px"><FONT STYLE="color: Black">DALLAS
(May 17, 2021) - Income Opportunity Realty Investors, Inc. (NYSE MKT: IOR), a Dallas-based real estate investment company, today reported
results of operations for the second quarter ended June 30, 2021.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">For the three months ended June 30, 2021, the Company reported net income of $819,000 or $0.20 per share, as compared to net income of
$1,352,000 or $0.30 per share for the same period ended 2020.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">Our primary business is investing in real estate and mortgage note receivables.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px"><B>Expenses</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">General
and administrative expenses were $100 thousand for the three months ended June 30, 2021. This represents a decrease of $28 thousand, compared
to general and administrative expenses of $128 thousand for the three months ended June 30, 2020. This decrease was primarily driven by
a decrease in legal fees of $12 thousand and audit fees of approximately $15 thousand.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">Advisory fees were $201 thousand for the three months
ended June 30, 2021 compared to $191 thousand for the same period in 2020 for an increase of $10 thousand. Advisory fees are computed
based on a gross asset fee of 0.0625% per month (0.75% per annum) of the average of the gross asset value.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">Net income fee to related party
was $55 thousand for the three months ended June 30, 2021. This represents a decrease of $57 thousand, compared to the net income fee
of $112 thousand for the three months ended June 30, 2020. The net income fee paid to our Advisor is calculated at 7.5% of net income.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px"><B>Other income
(expense)</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">Interest income decreased to $1.2 million for the three months ended June 30, 2021 compared to $1.3 million for the same
period in 2020. The decrease of $100 thousand was primarily due to a decrease in interest recognized due to some notes being paid off
in 1Q 2021.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">Other income was $162 thousand for the three months ended June 30, 2021 compared to $742 thousand for the three months ended
June 30, 2020. The decrease was due to a tax increment reimbursement from City of Farmers Branch received in 2020.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px"><B><I>About
Income Opportunity Realty Investors, Inc.</I></B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">Income
Opportunity Realty Investors, Inc., a Dallas-based real estate investment company, currently holds a portfolio of notes receivable. The
Company invests in real estate through direct equity ownership and partnerships. For more information, visit the Company&rsquo;s
website at&nbsp;www.incomeopp-realty.com.</P>
