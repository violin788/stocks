<P STYLE="font: 11pt/115% New times roman,serif; margin-top: 0; text-align: center; margin-bottom: 10pt"><B>Income
Opportunity Realty Investors, Inc. Reports Third Quarter 2020 Results</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px"><FONT STYLE="color: Black">DALLAS
(November 12, 2020) - Income Opportunity Realty Investors, Inc. (NYSE American: IOR), a Dallas-based real estate investment company, today reported results of operations for the third quarter ended September 30, 2020.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">For
the three months ended September 30, 2020, the Company reported net income of $761 thousand or $0.18 per diluted share, as compared
to $1.0 million or $0.25 per diluted share for the same period in 2019.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">Our
primary business is investing in real estate and mortgage note receivables.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px"><B>Expenses</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">General
and administrative expenses were $94 thousand for the three months ended September 30, 2020. This represents a decrease of $5 thousand,
compared to general and administrative expenses of $99 thousand for the three months ended September 30, 2019. This decrease was
primarily driven by a decrease in professional fees.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">Advisory
fees were $194 thousand for the three months ended September 30, 2020 compared to $186 thousand for the same period in 2019 for
an increase of $8 thousand. Advisory fees are computed based on a gross asset fee of 0.0625% per month (0.75% per annum) of the
average of the gross asset value.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">Net
income fee to related party was $51 thousand for the three months ended September 30, 2020. This represents a decrease of $32 thousand,
compared to the net income fee of $83 thousand for the three months ended September 30, 2019. The net income fee paid to our Advisor
is calculated at 7.5% of net income.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px"><B>Other
income (expense)</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">Interest
income decreased to $1.3 million for the three months ended September 30, 2020 compared to $1.7 million for the same period in
2019. The decrease of $400 thousand was primarily due to a decrease in the prime interest rate used to calculate interest on the
receivable amount owed from our Advisor and other related parties.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px"><B>About
Income Opportunity Realty Investors, Inc.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 400 16px Helvetica Neue; margin: 0 0 1.5em; letter-spacing: normal; word-spacing: 0px; text-indent: 0px">Income
Opportunity Realty Investors, Inc., a Dallas-based real estate investment company, holds a portfolio of equity real estate in Texas,
including undeveloped land. The Company invests in real estate through direct equity ownership and partnerships. For more information,
visit the Company&rsquo;s website at&nbsp;www.incomeopp-realty.com.</P>
