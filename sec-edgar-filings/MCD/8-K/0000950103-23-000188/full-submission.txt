<P STYLE="margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Employee Message </B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>FROM: Chris Kempczinski </B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>TO: Global Employees</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>SUBJECT: Introducing Accelerating the Arches 2.0</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B><I>This message is intended for McDonald&rsquo;s Global Employees</I></B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">When we launched our Accelerating the Arches growth strategy in 2020,
we made a commitment to embrace the changes taking place around us &ndash; to grow faster, to meet evolving customer expectations and
to make our brand even stronger. We can take pride in what we&rsquo;ve accomplished. We continue to outperform the industry in global
comparable sales and market share in most of our major markets. Shareholders also recognize our success as McDonald&rsquo;s stock trades
near an all-time high, generating returns that eclipse our restaurant peers, the Dow 30 and the S&amp;P 500. As I said at Worldwide Convention
in April, &ldquo;there&rsquo;s never been a better time to be part of brand McDonald&rsquo;s.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">As we begin 2023 from a position of strength, we cannot stand still.
As Ray Kroc used to say, &ldquo;If you&rsquo;re not green and growing, you&rsquo;re ripe and rotting.&rdquo; Fortunately, there remains
so much growth potential within our &ldquo;M-C-D&rdquo; pillars. However, to realize the full potential of our strategy, we must also
objectively assess areas we can do better.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">When we say, &ldquo;we put our customers and people first,&rdquo; it
means that we have a laser-like focus on solving their problems. For our customers: Is our app the fastest? Are we providing a truly seamless
experience? Are we offering the most delicious, innovative menu items? Are we expanding our restaurant footprint to places that are convenient
for them? For our people: How can we make our restaurant teams&rsquo; jobs easier? Are we offering everyone the potential for professional
development and personal impact?</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">While there&rsquo;s a lot for us to be proud of, you&rsquo;ve also told
us that there&rsquo;s more we can do. <B>We&rsquo;re performing at a high level, but we can do even better</B>. That&rsquo;s what I love
about the McDonald&rsquo;s System &ndash; our restless ambition. We&rsquo;re never satisfied, which is why McDonald&rsquo;s continues
to be one of the world&rsquo;s leading corporations after almost 70 years.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Over the past several months, the Global SLT and I have discussed how
we can further elevate our performance. We concluded that to realize the full potential of the external opportunity, we must first address
our internal opportunity &ndash; namely, how we work with each other. Today, we&rsquo;re divided into silos with a center, segments, and
markets. This approach is outdated and self-limiting &ndash; we are trying to solve the same problems multiple times, aren&rsquo;t always
sharing ideas and can be slow to innovate. Our customers and people don&rsquo;t think of our brand from the viewpoint of markets or segments,
and neither can we.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">As we look to the future: <B>it must be the collective focus of our
entire System to solve problems for our customers and people once and scale our innovations faster than ever before. </B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">We&rsquo;ll do that through <B>Accelerating the Arches 2.0 </B>where
we&rsquo;ll double down on our &ldquo;M-C-Ds,&rdquo; while adding a 4<SUP>th</SUP> &ldquo;D,&rdquo; Restaurant Development. We must accelerate
the pace of our restaurant openings to fully capture the increased demand we&rsquo;ve driven over the past few years. We will also modernize
our ways of working through a new effort, <B><I>Accelerating the Organization (AtO)</I></B><I>.</I></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Through <I>Accelerating the Organization</I>, we will evolve our culture
to foster greater collaboration and develop &ldquo;one McDonald&rsquo;s way&rdquo; to solve problems for our customers and people together.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">This work is guided by a commitment to be:</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%" STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top">

smarter risks to solve customer problems once together.</FONT></TD></TR></TABLE>

<P STYLE="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">I have long believed the best solutions &ndash; and the blueprints for
our future &ndash; exist in our markets, and in our restaurants. The next great ideas will come from within our System. We proved that
during the pandemic by making big decisions and scaling them with speed and agility &ndash; driving growth while operating together. We
must build on this momentum.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">In the coming weeks, we will have meetings with leaders to share more
about <I>Accelerating the Organization</I> and what it means for our business. Certain initiatives will be de-prioritized or stopped altogether.
This will help us move faster as an organization, while reducing our global costs and freeing up resources to invest in our growth.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0pt 0">As part of this work, we will evaluate roles and staffing levels in
parts of the organization and there will be difficult discussions and decisions ahead. We will look to our strategy and our values to
guide how we reach those decisions and support every impacted member of the company. We expect to finalize and begin to communicate key
decisions by April 3.</P>
