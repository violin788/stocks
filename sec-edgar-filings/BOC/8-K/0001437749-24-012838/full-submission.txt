<div style="font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;"><b>&#160;</b></div>

<div style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-size: 10pt; font-variant: normal; margin: 0pt -9pt 0pt -1pt;">On April 23, 2024,&#160;Boston Omaha Corporation (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) issued a press release entitled &#160;"Boston Omaha Corporation Announces Rescheduling of 2024 Annual Meeting of Stockholders." The press release indicates that the Company is rescheduling the previously planned Annual Meeting&#160;of Stockholders, originally&#160;set for May 1, 2024,&#160;for later this summer or early fall in order to allow for an in-person Annual Meeting of Stockholders rather than holding a virtual meeting.&#160;The Company expects to announce the date of the Annual Meeting once the date and a location has been finalized.&#160;The full text of the press release is attached to this Current Report on Form 8-K as Exhibit 99.1. The press release was also simultaneously filed on the Company&#8217;s website.&#160;</div>

<div style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-size: 10pt; font-variant: normal; margin: 0pt -9pt 0pt -1pt;">&#160;</div>

<div style="font-family: &quot;Times New Roman&quot;; font-size: 10pt; font-variant: normal; margin: 0pt 0pt 0pt -1pt;">Because the 2024 Annual Meeting of Stockholders will be held more than 30 days from the anniversary date of the Company's 2023 Annual Meeting of Stockholders, the deadlines set forth in the Company's definitive proxy statement filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (the "SEC") on March 27, 2023 for stockholder proposals and director nominations for consideration at the 2024 Annual Meeting of Stockholders no longer apply.</div>
