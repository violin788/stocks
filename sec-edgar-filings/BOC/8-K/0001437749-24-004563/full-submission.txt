<div style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt 0pt 0pt 8pt;">On&#160; February 14, 2024, Link Media Holdings, Inc. (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline;">Link</span>&#8221;), a wholly-owned subsidiary of Boston Omaha Corporation (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline;">BOC</span>&#8221;), which owns and operates BOC&#8217;s billboard businesses, entered into an Eighth&#160;Amendment to Credit Agreement (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline;">Eighth&#160;Amendment</span>&#8221;) with First National Bank of Omaha (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline;">Lender</span>&#8221;).
