<div style="font-family:'Times New Roman', Times, serif;font-size:10pt;margin:0pt;text-align:left;"><b>&#xa0;</b></div>

<div style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt 0pt 0pt 8pt;">On&#xa0; September 22, 2023, Link Media Holdings, Inc. (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline;">Link</span>&#8221;), a wholly-owned subsidiary of Boston Omaha Corporation (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline;">BOC</span>&#8221;), which owns and operates BOC&#8217;s billboard businesses, entered into a Seventh&#xa0;Amendment to Credit Agreement (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline;">Seventh&#xa0;Amendment</span>&#8221;) with First National Bank of Omaha (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline;">Lender</span>&#8221;). On August 12, 2019, Link entered into a Credit Agreement, as amended (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline;">Credit Agreement</span>&#8221;) with the Lender under which Link could&#xa0;borrow up to $30,000,000&#xa0;in the aggregate under two term loans and $5,000,000 under a revolving line of credit, each guaranteed by Link&#8217;s subsidiaries.&#xa0; &#xa0;The Seventh&#xa0;Amendment modifies&#xa0;the&#xa0;Credit Agreement&#xa0;to provide additional flexibility for Link&#xa0;by increasing the revolving line of credit from $5,000,000 to $10,000,000.&#xa0; The fee&#xa0;to maintain the revolving&#xa0; line of credit is 20 basis points per year.&#xa0; &#xa0;All other terms regarding the Revolving Line of credit are unchanged.</div>

<div style="font-family:Times New Roman;font-size:10pt;font-variant:normal;margin:0pt 0pt 0pt 8pt;">&#xa0;</div>
