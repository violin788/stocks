    <p style="text-align: left;"><b><img src="exhibit99-1xu001.jpg"></b></p>
    <p style="text-align: center;"><b>Leatt Corp Announces Record Breaking Year; Reports Results for Fourth<br>Quarter and Full Year 2021</b></p>
    <p style="margin-bottom: 0pt; text-align: center;"><i>88% Increase in Global Revenues to $72.5 million </i></p>
    <p style="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; text-align: center;"><i>184% Increase in Net Income to $12.6 million</i></p>
    <p style="margin-top: 0pt; text-align: center;"><i>179% increase in Earnings Per Share to $2.29</i></p>
    <hr style="border-top-style: none; border-bottom: 1.5pt solid #000000;">
    <p style="text-align: justify;"><b>CAPE TOWN, South Africa</b>, (March 10, 2022) - Leatt Corporation (OTCQB: LEAT), a leading developer and marketer of protective equipment and ancillary products for many forms of sports, especially extreme high-velocity sports, today announced record financial results for the fourth quarter and full year ending December 31, 2021. All financial numbers are in U.S. dollars.</p>
    <p style="text-align: justify;"><b>2021 Highlights</b></p>
    <p style="margin-left: 35.45pt; text-indent: -35.45pt; text-align: justify;">&#8226;<font style="width: 31.93pt; text-indent: 0pt; display: inline-block;">&#160;</font>Record Fourth quarter revenues of $23.2 million, up 82%, compared to 2020.</p>
    <p style="margin-left: 35.45pt; text-indent: -35.45pt;
