<td style="width:11%;vertical-align:top;text-align:left"><span style="font-weight:bold">Item&#8201;2.05</span></td>
<td style="vertical-align:top;text-align:left"> <p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Costs Associated with Exit or Disposal Activities. </p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On February&#160;17, 2025, Southwest Airlines Co. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) announced a planned reduction in workforce (the &#8220;Reduction&#8221;) intended to reduce operating costs, increase efficiency, and create a leaner and more agile organization as part of the Company&#8217;s transformational plan. The Reduction is focused almost entirely on corporate overhead and leadership positions. The Company committed to the Reduction on February&#160;5, 2025, and on February&#160;17, 2025, the Company informed Employees of the Reduction, which provides for a reduction of the Company&#8217;s current workforce by approximately 1,750 Employee roles, or 15% of corporate positions. Separations associated with the Reduction are expected to be substantially complete by the end of second quarter 2025. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As a result of the Reduction, the Company estimates partial year 2025 savings to be approximately $210&#160;million and full-year 2026 savings of approximately $300&#160;million.
