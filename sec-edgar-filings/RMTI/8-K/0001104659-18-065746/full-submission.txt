<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">Appointment of Chief Financial Officer</font></i></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On November&nbsp;1, 2018, Rockwell Medical,&nbsp;Inc., a Michigan corporation (the &#147;<i>Company</i>&#148;), announced the appointment of Angus Smith as the Company&#146;s Chief Financial Officer.&#160; Mr.&nbsp;Smith will begin serving as the Company&#146;s Chief Financial Officer on or around November&nbsp;28, 2018.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Smith, age 35, has significant experience in a variety of finance roles in the health care industry.&#160; Most recently, Mr.&nbsp;Smith served as Senior Vice President, Chief Business Officer and Principal Financial Officer at Pernix Therapeutics Holdings,&nbsp;Inc. (&#147;<i>Pernix</i>&#148;), from February&nbsp;2018 until present, where Mr.&nbsp;Smith was responsible for the oversight of Pernix&#146;s financial operations and management of Pernix&#146;s business development activities, as well as relationships with investors and other key constituents in the financial community.&#160; Previously at Pernix, Mr.&nbsp;Smith served as Vice President, Business Development&nbsp;&amp; Strategic Planning from July&nbsp;2016 to February&nbsp;2018 and as Vice President, M&amp;A and Corporate Finance from September&nbsp;2014 to July&nbsp;2016. From October&nbsp;2011 to September&nbsp;2014, Mr.&nbsp;Smith served as a Director in the Healthcare Investment Banking Group at Cantor Fitzgerald, a financial services firm. Mr.&nbsp;Smith previously held several positions of increasing responsibility in the Healthcare Investment Banking group at Gleacher&nbsp;&amp; Company, a financial advisory firm, and various predecessor firms from July&nbsp;2005 to August&nbsp;2011. Mr.&nbsp;Smith earned a B.A. in Mathematical Economics from Colgate University in 2005.&#160; There are no arrangements between Mr.&nbsp;Smith and any other person pursuant to which Mr.&nbsp;Smith was appointed to serve as our Chief Financial Officer, nor are there any material transactions in which the Company is a participant in which Mr.&nbsp;Smith has a material interest.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">Employment Agreement</font></i></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On October&nbsp;26, 2018, the Company entered into an employment agreement&nbsp;with Angus Smith, pursuant to which he will serve as the Company&#146;s Chief Financial Officer (the &#147;<i>Employment Agreement</i>&#148;). The Employment Agreement provides that Mr.&nbsp;Smith will serve as an at-will employee. Mr.&nbsp;Smith will receive an annualized base salary of $400,000 (&#147;<i>Base Salary</i>&#148;) and a sign-on bonus of $125,000 (Mr.&nbsp;Smith is required to repay a pro-rated portion of his sign-on bonus if the Company terminates his employment for Cause or if Mr.&nbsp;Smith resigns without Good Reason within 12 months of the Commencement Date). Beginning in 2019, Mr.&nbsp;Smith will be eligible to earn year-end performance bonuses (to be paid in either cash or equity or both) with a target bonus of 50% of his Base Salary (&#147;<i>Target Bonus</i>&#148;). Mr.&nbsp;Smith shall be eligible for annual long-term incentive grants in cash or equity or both and will receive an initial equity grant on his commencement of employment representing the right to acquire a total of up to 500,000 shares of Company common stock, compromised of time-based and performance-based options and time-based and performance-based restricted stock units.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Under the&nbsp;Employment Agreement, upon a termination of Mr.&nbsp;Smith&#146;s employment by the Company for &#147;Cause&#148; or by Mr.&nbsp;Smith without &#147;Good Reason&#148; (each, as defined), Mr.&nbsp;Smith will be entitled to receive: (i)&nbsp;unpaid compensation accrued through the last day of his employment, (ii)&nbsp;a lump sum payment of accrued but unused vacation days, and (iii)&nbsp;payment of any other amounts owing to Mr.&nbsp;Smith but not yet paid (including any bonus earned but not paid).</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Under the&nbsp;Employment Agreement, upon a termination of Mr.&nbsp;Smith&#146;s employment by the Company without Cause or by Mr.&nbsp;Smith for Good Reason, Mr.&nbsp;Smith will be entitled to receive the compensation entitled to him upon a termination of his employment by the Company for Cause or by Mr.&nbsp;Smith without Good Reason in addition to: (i)&nbsp;his Base Salary then in effect plus 100% of the Target Bonus payable for such one-year period, and (ii)&nbsp;COBRA coverage for one year.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Smith will also be eligible to receive certain benefits following a Change of Control.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In connection with the Employment Agreement, Mr.&nbsp;Smith also entered into the Company&#146;s form of Employee Confidentiality, Assignment of Inventions, Non-Interference and Non-Competition Agreement.</font></p>
