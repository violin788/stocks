<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 5.02</font></u></b>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b><u style="font-weight:bold;">Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers</u>.</b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On June&nbsp;14, 2019, Rockwell Medical,&nbsp;Inc., a Michigan corporation (the &#147;<i>Company</i>&#148;), appointed Paul E. McGarry as the Company&#146;s Vice President, Corporate Controller and Principal Accounting Officer.&nbsp; Mr.&nbsp;McGarry will begin serving as the Company&#146;s Vice President, Corporate Controller and Principal Accounting Officer on or around June&nbsp;24, 2019. Mr.&nbsp;McGarry will succeed David Kull as principal accounting officer, and Mr.&nbsp;Kull will remain with the Company in a senior accounting position.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;McGarry, age 51, has significant experience in a variety of finance roles in the health care industry.&nbsp; Most recently, Mr.&nbsp;McGarry served as Corporate Financial Controller at Alyvant,&nbsp;Inc. (&#147;<i>Alyvant</i>&#148;) from November&nbsp;2018 until present, where Mr.&nbsp;McGarry was responsible for oversight of Alyvant&#146;s financial management.&nbsp; From July&nbsp;2011 to November&nbsp;2018, Mr.&nbsp;McGarry served as Corporate Controller at Champions Oncology,&nbsp;Inc. (&#147;<i>Champions</i>&#148;), where he was responsible for the oversight of Champions&#146; accounting and financial operations. Mr.&nbsp;McGarry began his accounting career at Deloitte&nbsp;&amp; Touche LLP, serving as Audit Manager in Assurance and Advisory Services, where he was tasked with implementing worldwide Sarbanes-Oxley (<i>&#147;SOX&#148;</i>) procedures and performed SOX training for international audit teams. Mr.&nbsp;McGarry earned a B.S. in Accounting from Penn State in 1998 and is a Certified Public Accountant in the state of New York.&nbsp; There are no arrangements between Mr.&nbsp;McGarry and any other person pursuant to which Mr.&nbsp;McGarry was appointed to serve as Vice President, Corporate Controller and Principal Accounting Officer, nor are there any material transactions in which the Company is a participant in which Mr.&nbsp;McGarry has a material interest.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Pursuant to the terms of Mr.&nbsp;McGarry&#146;s employment offer letter, he will serve as an at-will employee. Mr.&nbsp;McGarry will receive an annualized base salary of $265,000 (&#147;<i>Base Salary</i>&#148;). Mr.&nbsp;McGarry will be eligible to earn year-end performance bonuses with a target bonus of 35% of his Base Salary. For 2019, Mr.&nbsp;McGarry will be eligible for a pro-rated bonus based on his start date. Mr.&nbsp;McGarry will receive an initial grant on his commencement of employment of stock options to purchase 50,000 shares of Company common stock, which are subject to time-based vesting.</font></p>
