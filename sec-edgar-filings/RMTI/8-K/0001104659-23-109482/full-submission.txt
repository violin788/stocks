of Rockwell Medical,&#160;Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) increased the size of the Board from six to seven directors and appointed Joan
Lau, PhD to the Board as a Class&#160;II director to serve until the Company&#8217;s 2026 Annual Meeting of Stockholders and until her
successor is duly elected and qualified, effective immediately.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Dr.&#160;Lau, age&#160;53, co-founded Spirovant Sciences Inc., formerly
Talee Bio,&#160;Inc., a company focused on the discovery and development of gene therapies for respiratory diseases, in 2016 and has served
as its Chief Executive Officer since May&#160;2016. In 2013, Dr.&#160;Lau co-founded, and is currently a partner in, Militia Hill Ventures,
a firm that creates and builds innovative life science entities. Dr.&#160;Lau has served as a trustee of Brandywine Realty Trust (NYSE:
BDN), a real estate company, since February&#160;2023, and serves as a director for RiboNova Inc., a privately-held biopharmaceutical
company. Dr.&#160;Lau previously served as a director of Renovacor,&#160;Inc. (NYSE: RCOR), a biotechnology company, from September&#160;2021
until it was acquired by Rocket Pharmaceuticals,&#160;Inc. in December&#160;2022. Dr.&#160;Lau earned an MBA from the Wharton School at
the University of Pennsylvania, a Ph.D. in Medical Neuroscience from the University of Cincinnati College of Medicine, and a BSE in Bioengineering
from the University of Pennsylvania.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">In accordance with the Company&#8217;s non-employee director compensation
policy, Dr.&#160;Lau will receive an annual cash retainer of $45,000 for her service as a director, which will be pro-rated through the
Company&#8217;s 2024 Annual Meeting of Stockholders. In addition, Dr.&#160;Lau was granted an option to purchase 25,000 shares of the
Company&#8217;s common stock at an exercise price equal to the closing price of the Company&#8217;s common stock on The Nasdaq Capital
Market on October&#160;16, 2023, and 25,000 restricted stock units for her service as a director. The equity awards were made under the
Company&#8217;s Amended and Restated 2018 Long Term Incentive Plan. The restricted stock units and the shares underlying the option will
vest and become exercisable on October&#160;16, 2024, subject to Dr.&#160;Lau&#8217;s continued service to the Company. Dr.&#160;Lau will
enter into the Company&#8217;s standard form of indemnification agreement.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">There are no arrangements or understandings between Dr.&#160;Lau and
any other persons pursuant to which she was elected as a director of the Company. There are no family relationships between Dr.&#160;Lau
and any director or executive officer of the Company, and she has no direct or indirect material interest in any transaction required
to be disclosed pursuant to Item&#160;404(a)&#160;of&#160;Regulation S-K.</p>
