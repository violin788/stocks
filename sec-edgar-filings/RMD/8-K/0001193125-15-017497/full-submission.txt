 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="right"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>ResMed Inc. Announces Results for the Second Quarter of Fiscal Year 2015 </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Second quarter revenue increased 10% to $423 million; up 14% on a constant currency basis </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Second quarter GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.64 </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Second quarter operating cash flow of $106.0 million </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Company repurchased 667,000 shares in the second quarter </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>San Diego, January&nbsp;22, 2015</B> &#150; ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced results for its quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2014. Revenue for the
quarter was $423.0 million, a 10 percent increase compared to the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2013 (a 14 percent increase on a constant currency basis). Net income was $91.2 million, an increase of 5 percent compared to the quarter ended
December&nbsp;31, 2013. Diluted earnings per share for the quarter were $0.64, an increase of 7 percent compared to the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2013. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We are pleased to report strong double-digit revenue growth, demonstrating excellent progress with our new product launches,&#148; said
Mick&nbsp;Farrell,&nbsp;ResMed&#146;s&nbsp;chief&nbsp;executive&nbsp;officer. &#147;We achieved robust commercial performance across all regions, including double-digit growth in the Americas.&#148;</P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;During the quarter, we&nbsp;continued to roll out the ResMed<B> Air Solutions</B> Platform with the launch of our <B>AirCurve&#153; 10</B> series of
cloud-connected bilevel devices. We also continued the global roll-out of our new life support ventilation system, the <B>Astral</B><SUP STYLE="font-size:85%; vertical-align:top">&#153;</SUP> platform. We drove consumer awareness of sleep by
launching the <B>S+ by ResMed</B>, the world&#146;s first non-contact sleep management solution that helps you monitor and improve your sleep health; we also integrated the S+ with Apple HealthKit. On the legal front, we won a patent infringement
lawsuit as we defend our broad portfolio of over 5,000 patents and designs,&#148; said Farrell. &#147;We drove top-line revenue growth in the first half of this&nbsp;fiscal&nbsp;year&nbsp;by&nbsp;launching a strong, innovative portfolio of products
and solutions. Longer term, we are focused on our strategy to continue to grow our core sleep apnea market, as well as to invest in high-potential growth opportunities: helping patients with COPD, neuromuscular disease, and cardio-respiratory
conditions. Our results this quarter show that our solutions continue to meet the needs of our key customer groups, including patients, physicians, healthcare providers and payers. We are executing to our mission: improving patient quality-of-life,
lowering healthcare costs, and preventing chronic disease progression.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>Analysis of second quarter results </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In the second quarter of fiscal year 2015, revenue in the Americas was $231.0 million, a 12 percent increase over the prior year&#146;s quarter. Revenue in
combined Europe and Asia Pacific was $192.0 million, an 8 percent increase compared to the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2013 (a 16 percent increase on a constant currency basis). </P>
