<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On April&nbsp;23, 2014, we announced that our board of directors declared a cash dividend of US $0.25 per share of outstanding common

dividend to holders of CHESS Depositary Instruments (CDIs) trading on the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX) at an equivalent amount in Australian currency, based on the exchange rate on May&nbsp;20, 2014, and reflecting the 10:1 ratio between
CDIs and NYSE shares. Because the two exchanges have different settlement and transfer procedures, the ex-dividend periods before the record date will be different for common stock and for CDIs.&nbsp;The ex-dividend date will be May&nbsp;14, 2014
for CDI holders and May&nbsp;16, 2014 for common stock holders. As a result of these differences, ResMed has received a waiver from the ASX&#146;s settlement operating rules, which will allow us to defer processing conversions between its common

<TD VALIGN="bottom"> <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>RESMED INC. ANNOUNCES RECORD FINANCIAL RESULTS FOR</B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:1pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>THE QUARTER ENDED AND NINE MONTHS ENDED MARCH 31, 2014</B></P></TD></TR>
</TABLE> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P> <P STYLE="line-height:3.0pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:2pt;border-bottom:1px solid #000000">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>SAN DIEGO, California, April&nbsp;23, 2014</B> &#150; ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced record revenue and income for the quarter ended
March&nbsp;31, 2014. Revenue for the quarter was $397.8 million, a 4 percent increase (a 3 percent increase on a constant currency basis) over the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013. Net income was $90.0 million, an increase of 6 percent compared to
the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013. Diluted earnings per share for the quarter were $0.63, an increase of 9 percent compared to the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Selling, general and administrative expenses were $115.1 million for the quarter, an increase of 5 percent (a 7 percent increase on a constant currency basis)
over the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013. SG&amp;A costs were 28.9 percent of revenue in the quarter, compared to 28.6 percent in the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Research and development expenses were $29.5 million for the quarter, or 7.4 percent of revenue. R&amp;D expenses decreased by 5&nbsp;percent (a 7 percent
increase on a constant currency basis) compared to the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013. R&amp;D expenses were favorably impacted by the depreciation of the Australian dollar against the U.S. dollar. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">ResMed amortized acquired intangibles of $2.5 million ($1.9 million, net of tax) during the quarter. Stock-based compensation costs incurred during the
quarter of $11.2 million ($8.1 million, net of tax) consisted of expenses associated with stock options, restricted stock units, and the company&#146;s employee stock purchase plan. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">For the nine months ended March&nbsp;31, 2014, revenue was $1,139.8 million, an increase of 4 percent over the nine months ended March&nbsp;31, 2013 (a 3
percent increase on a constant currency basis). For the nine months, net income was $257.5 million, an increase of 10 percent compared to the nine months ended March&nbsp;31, 2013. Diluted earnings per share for the nine months were $1.78 per
diluted share, an increase of 11 percent compared to the nine months ended March&nbsp;31, 2013. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In the third quarter of fiscal year 2014, revenue outside
the Americas was $181.6 million, an increase of 8 percent over the prior year&#146;s quarter. In constant currency terms, revenue outside the Americas increased by 6 percent. Revenue in the Americas, at $216.1 million, remained flat compared to the
prior year&#146;s quarter of $215.2 million. Operating profit for the third quarter grew to $104.7 million, cash flow from operations was $101.1 million, while gross margin was 63.3 percent, all demonstrating robust operating performance. During the
