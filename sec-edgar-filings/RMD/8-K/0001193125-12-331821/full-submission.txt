 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:24px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>RESMED INC. ANNOUNCES RECORD FINANCIAL RESULTS FOR THE </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>QUARTER AND TWELVE MONTHS ENDED JUNE 30, 2012 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SAN DIEGO, California, August&nbsp;2, 2012 - ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced record revenue and income for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012. Revenue for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012
was $371.9 million, a 9% increase (a 13% increase on a constant currency basis) over the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2011. For the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012, net income was $76.8 million, an increase of 31% compared to the quarter ended
June&nbsp;30, 2011. Diluted earnings per share for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012 were $0.53, an increase of 43% compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2011. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SG&amp;A expenses were $105.9 million for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012, an increase of $3.6 million, or 3% (a 9% increase on a constant currency basis) compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30,
2011. SG&amp;A costs were 28.5% of revenue in the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012, compared to 29.9% for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2011. SG&amp;A expenses were favorably impacted by the depreciation of the Australian dollar and euro against
the U.S. dollar. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">R&amp;D expenses were $27.9 million for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012, or 7.5% of revenue. R&amp;D expenses increased
by 3% (a 10% increase on a constant currency basis) compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2011. R&amp;D expenses were favorably impacted by the depreciation of the Australian dollar against the U.S. dollar. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The company amortized acquired intangibles of $2.9 million ($2.2 million, net of tax) during the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012. Stock-based
compensation costs incurred during the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012 of $8.1 million ($5.9 million, net of tax) consisted of expenses associated with stock options, restricted stock units, and our employee stock purchase plan. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">For the year ended June&nbsp;30, 2012, revenue was $1.37 billion, an increase of 10% over the year ended June&nbsp;30, 2011 (11% increase on a constant
