 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="right"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>ResMed Inc. Announces Results for the Third Quarter of Fiscal Year 2016 </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Revenue increased 7% to $453.9 million; up 9% on a constant currency basis </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.63; non-GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.68 </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Operating cash flow of $122.1 million in the third quarter </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>San Diego, April&nbsp;26, 2016</B> &#150; ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced results for its quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2016. Revenue for the
quarter was $453.9 million, a 7 percent increase compared with the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2015; this is a 9 percent increase on a constant currency basis. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We have seen sustained success with the execution of our healthcare informatics strategy driving seven consecutive quarters of device growth, since the
Q1 2015 launch of our Air Solutions platform. This quarter our team delivered strong double-digit growth in the Americas region, with solid global constant currency growth of 9%. Customers have responded to the value proposition of our Air Solutions
platform, including the efficiencies it brings to their businesses and its ability to help them drive even better patient outcomes through connected care,&#148; said Mick Farrell, ResMed&#146;s chief executive officer. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We continue to build on our offerings across our strategy for both products and informatics solutions. This quarter we announced the close of the Inova
transaction, which allows us to expand our respiratory care offerings to include portable oxygen concentrators. With the acquisition of Brightree in April, we have established ourselves as the outright leader for cloud-based end-to-end solutions in
HME, home health, and hospice channels.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Farrell continued, &#147;This expansion into new products and informatics solutions allows ResMed to
continue its global leadership as a tech-driven medical device company. We are liberating healthcare data and unlocking value by providing actionable connected care solutions for patients, physicians, providers, and&nbsp;payers.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>Analysis of third quarter results </I></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In the third
quarter of fiscal year 2016, revenue in the Americas was $282.2 million, a 12 percent increase over the prior year&#146;s quarter. Revenue in combined EMEA and APAC was $171.7 million, flat compared with the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2015; this
is a 3 percent increase on a constant currency basis. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Gross margin in the third quarter was 57.3 percent; this is lower than the prior year&#146;s
quarter, mainly due to changes in product mix, average selling prices and geographic mix. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Income from operations for the quarter was $104.3 million, a 1
percent decrease compared with the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2015. Non-GAAP income from operations for the quarter was $112.4 million, a 4 percent increase compared with the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2015. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Selling, general and administrative expenses were $119.4 million for the third quarter of fiscal year 2016, a 3 percent increase over the quarter ended
March&nbsp;31, 2015; this is a 5 percent increase on a constant currency basis. SG&amp;A expenses improved to 26.3 percent of revenue in the quarter, compared with 27.5 percent in the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2015. </P>

<p Style='page-break-before:always'>
<HR  SIZE="3" style="COLOR:#999999" WIDTH="100%" ALIGN="CENTER">

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Research and development expenses were $28.1 million for the third quarter of fiscal year 2016, or 6.2 percent of
revenue. R&amp;D expenses increased by 4 percent compared with the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2015; this is an 11 percent increase on a constant currency basis. </P>
