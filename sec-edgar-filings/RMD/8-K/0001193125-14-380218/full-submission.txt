 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="right"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>RESMED Inc. Announces Results for the First Quarter of Fiscal Year 2015 </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>First quarter revenue increased by 6% to $380 million </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>First quarter GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.58 </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>First quarter operating cash flow of $86.5 million </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Company repurchased 835,000 shares in the first quarter </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Company successfully launches AirSense 10 platform globally </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>SAN DIEGO, Oct 23, 2014</B> &#150; ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced results for its quarter ended September&nbsp;30, 2014. Revenue for the quarter
was $380.4 million, a 6 percent increase compared to the quarter ended September&nbsp;30, 2013 (also a 6 percent increase on a constant currency basis). Net income was $83.3 million, an increase of 3 percent compared to the quarter ended
September&nbsp;30, 2013. Diluted earnings per share for the quarter were $0.58, an increase of 4 percent compared to the quarter ended September&nbsp;30, 2013. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We had a successful start to fiscal&nbsp;year 2015, as new product launches drove&nbsp;revenue&nbsp;growth,&#148; said
Mick&nbsp;Farrell,&nbsp;ResMed&#146;s&nbsp;chief&nbsp;executive&nbsp;officer. &#147;Our results&nbsp;showed&nbsp;very strong performance from our European and Asia Pacific regions, as well as solid growth in the Americas.&#148;</P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;During the quarter, we&nbsp;launched&nbsp;innovative products for the&nbsp;sleep-disordered breathing market, including our new AirSense 10 platform, as
well as a new life support ventilator for the U.S. respiratory care market, our new Astral platform. We have also recently made progress in&nbsp;building&nbsp;consumer awareness through two initiatives: an education and product-focused campaign
called &#145;Better Sleep for Women&#146;;&nbsp;and the recent launch of a new product &#145;S+&nbsp;by ResMed&#146;, the first non-contact sleep wellness tracking device for individuals,&#148; said Farrell. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We are delivering on our promise to drive top-line revenue growth this&nbsp;fiscal&nbsp;year&nbsp;by&nbsp;launching a strong portfolio of new products,
services,&nbsp;and solutions. This portfolio will provide exceptional value to all key players in our value chain: physicians, providers, payors, and most importantly, to patients.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>Analysis of first quarter results </I></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In the first
quarter of fiscal year 2015, revenue outside the Americas was $173.2 million, an 11 percent increase over the prior year&#146;s quarter (also an 11 percent increase on a constant currency basis). Revenue in the Americas was $207.2 million, a 3
percent increase compared to $201.5 million in the prior year&#146;s quarter. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Gross margin in the first quarter was 62.4 percent, 130 basis points lower

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">SG&amp;A expenses were 29.1 percent of revenue in the quarter, compared to 28.3 percent in the quarter ended

<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Research and development expenses were $30.0 million for the quarter, or 7.9 percent of revenue. R&amp;D expenses increased by 10 percent (a 9 percent
increase on a constant currency basis) compared to the quarter ended September&nbsp;30, 2013, primarily reflecting incremental investment in healthcare informatics and cardiology. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Operating profit for the quarter was $94.7 million and cash flow from operations was $86.5 million. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Amortization of acquired intangible assets was $2.1 million ($1.6 million, net of tax) during the quarter. Stock-based compensation costs incurred during the
quarter of $11.4 million ($8.1 million, net of tax) consisted of expenses associated with employee equity grants, and the company&#146;s employee stock purchase plan. </P>
