 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="right"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B>ResMed Inc. Announces Results for the First Quarter of Fiscal Year 2016 </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Revenue increased 8% to $412 million; up 15% on a constant currency basis </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.57, or non-GAAP earnings per share of $0.58 </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Operating cash flow of $122.1 million </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Company repurchased 1.2&nbsp;million shares in the first quarter </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL"><B>San Diego, October&nbsp;22, 2015</B> &#150; ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced results for its quarter ended September&nbsp;30, 2015. Revenue for the quarter
was $411.6 million, an 8 percent increase compared with the quarter ended September&nbsp;30, 2014 (a 15 percent increase on a constant currency basis). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">&#147;Our
global team was able to drive strong double-digit constant currency growth in the first quarter of fiscal year 2016. This result was led by 39 percent flow generator and 9 percent mask growth in the Americas,&#148; said Mick Farrell, ResMed&#146;s
chief executive officer. &#147;We are investing for ongoing expansion and leadership in our high-growth regions. In October, we completed the acquisition of Curative Medical, making our combined ResMed and Curative business the market leader in
sleep-disordered breathing and respiratory care in China.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Farrell continued, &#147;It has been a year since we launched our Air Solutions Platform. We have
made incredible progress, with over one million patients with cloud-connected devices on bedside tables, providing actionable data every day for patients, customers, providers and payors. Our leading informatics capabilities enable our global team
to enhance and deliver the most innovative products and solutions that improve patient outcomes, create efficiencies for our customers, help providers better manage chronic disease and lower healthcare costs. We have established ourselves as leaders
in the field of connected care and digital health.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL"><B><I>Analysis of first quarter results </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">In the first quarter of fiscal year 2016, revenue in the Americas was $254.2 million, a 23 percent increase over the prior year&#146;s quarter. Revenue in combined EMEA
and APAC was $157.5 million, a 9 percent decrease compared with the quarter ended September&nbsp;30, 2014 (a 5 percent increase on a constant currency basis). </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Gross margin was 58.0 percent in the first quarter of fiscal year 2016, lower than the prior year&#146;s quarter mainly due to an unfavorable product mix, declines in
average selling prices and an unfavorable geographic mix. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Income from operations for the quarter was $98.0 million, a 4 percent increase compared with the quarter
ended September&nbsp;30, 2014. Non-GAAP income from operations for the quarter was $100.3 million, a 4&nbsp;percent increase compared with the quarter ended September&nbsp;30, 2014. </P>

<p Style='page-break-before:always'>
<HR  SIZE="3" style="COLOR:#999999" WIDTH="100%" ALIGN="CENTER">

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Selling, general and administrative expenses were $111.1 million for the first quarter of fiscal year 2016, a
1&nbsp;percent increase (an 11 percent increase on a constant currency basis) over the quarter ended September&nbsp;30, 2014. SG&amp;A expenses improved to 27.0 percent of revenue in the quarter, compared with 29.1 percent in the quarter ended
September&nbsp;30, 2014. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Research and development expenses were $27.2 million for the first quarter of fiscal year 2016, or 6.6 percent of revenue. R&amp;D
expenses decreased by 9 percent (a 12 percent increase on a constant currency basis) compared with the quarter ended September&nbsp;30, 2014. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Amortization of
acquired intangible assets was $2.3 million during the quarter. Stock-based compensation costs incurred during the quarter of $12.4 million consisted of expenses associated with employee equity grants, and the company&#146;s employee stock purchase
