 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>RESMED INC. ANNOUNCES RECORD FINANCIAL RESULTS FOR THE </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>QUARTER AND TWELVE MONTHS ENDED JUNE 30, 2013 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SAN DIEGO, August&nbsp;1, 2013&#151;ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced record revenue and income for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013. Revenue for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013 was $414.6
million, an 11% increase (also an 11% increase on a constant currency basis) over the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012. For the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013, GAAP income from operations was $86.2 million, while GAAP net income was $73.0 million
or $0.50 per diluted share. These results were impacted by a $24.8 million ($17.7 million, net of tax) charge for our previously announced Sydney University education, research and settlement expenses. For the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013,
non-GAAP operating income and non-GAAP net income were $110.9 million and $90.7 million, an increase of 24% and 18%, respectively, compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012 (non-GAAP measures exclude the impact of the charge for Sydney
University education, research and settlement expenses, as described below). Non-GAAP diluted earnings per share for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013 were $0.62, an increase of 17% compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SG&amp;A expenses were $115.1 million for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013, an increase of $9.1 million, or 9% (also a 9% increase on a constant
currency basis) compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012. SG&amp;A costs were 27.8% of revenue in the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013, compared to 28.5% for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">R&amp;D expenses were $31.4 million for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013, or 7.6% of revenue. R&amp;D expenses increased by 12% (a 14% increase on a
constant currency basis) compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2012. R&amp;D expenses were favorably impacted by the depreciation of the Australian dollar against the U.S. dollar. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The company amortized acquired intangibles of $2.5 million ($1.9 million, net of tax) during the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013. Stock-based compensation costs incurred during the quarter ended
June&nbsp;30, 2013 of $10.5 million ($6.6 million, net of tax) consisted of expenses associated with stock options, restricted stock units, and our employee stock purchase plan. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As disclosed in our May press release, we reached an agreement with the University of Sydney (&#147;University&#148;) to pay $24.8 million for the establishment of two perpetual academic chairs, the
funding of future research in the fields of sleep medicine and biomedical engineering and the settlement of legal proceedings between the parties. We have expensed the full amount of $24.8 million ($17.7 million, net of tax) in the current quarter
within our operating expenses and we have separately disclosed the amount as an education, research and settlement charge. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">For the year ended
June&nbsp;30, 2013, revenue was $1.5 billion, an increase of 11% over the year ended June&nbsp;30, 2012 (a 12% increase on a constant currency basis). For the year ended June&nbsp;30, 2013, GAAP net income was $307.1 million, an increase of 21%
compared to the year ended June&nbsp;30, 2012. GAAP diluted earnings per share for the year ended June&nbsp;30, 2013 were $2.10, an increase of 23% compared to the year ended June&nbsp;30, 2012. For the year ended June&nbsp;30, 2013, non-GAAP
