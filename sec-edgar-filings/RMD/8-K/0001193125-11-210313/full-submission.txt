 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px">

 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>RESMED INC. ANNOUNCES RECORD FINANCIAL RESULTS FOR THE </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>QUARTER AND TWELVE MONTHS ENDED JUNE 30, 2011 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>- ResMed also Announces Acquisition of Gr&uuml;ndler GmbH </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SAN DIEGO, California,
August&nbsp;4, 2011 - ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced record revenue and income for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2011. Revenue for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2011 was $341.9 million, a 17% increase (an 11% increase on a constant
currency basis) over the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2010. For the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2011, net income was $58.5 million, an increase of 10% compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2010. Diluted earnings per share for the quarter ended
June&nbsp;30, 2011 were $0.37, an increase of 9% compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2010. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SG&amp;A expenses were $102.3 million for
the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2011, an increase of $18.5 million, or 22% (a 13% increase on a constant currency basis) compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2010. SG&amp;A costs were 29.9% of revenue in the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2011,
compared to 28.8% for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2010. SG&amp;A expenses were negatively impacted by the appreciation of the Australian dollar and euro against the U.S. dollar. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">R&amp;D expenses were $27.0 million for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2011, or 7.9% of revenue. R&amp;D expenses increased by 35% (an 11% increase on a constant currency basis) compared to the quarter
ended June&nbsp;30, 2010. R&amp;D expenses were primarily impacted by the appreciation of the Australian dollar and euro against the U.S. dollar. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The company amortized acquired intangibles of $2.9 million ($1.9 million, net of tax) during the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2011. Stock-based compensation costs incurred during the quarter ended
June&nbsp;30, 2011 of $7.5 million ($5.4 million, net of tax) consisted of expenses associated with stock options, restricted stock units, and our employee stock purchase plan. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">For the year ended June&nbsp;30, 2011, revenue was $1.2 billion, an increase of 14% over the year ended June&nbsp;30, 2010 (a 13% increase on a constant currency basis). For the year ended June&nbsp;30,
