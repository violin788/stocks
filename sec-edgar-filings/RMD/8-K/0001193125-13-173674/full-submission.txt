 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>RESMED INC. ANNOUNCES RECORD FINANCIAL RESULTS FOR THE </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>QUARTER ENDED AND NINE MONTHS ENDED MARCH 31, 2013 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px">&nbsp;</P>
<P STYLE="line-height:0px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:2px;border-bottom:0.5pt solid #000000">&nbsp;</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SAN DIEGO, April&nbsp;25, 2013 &#150; ResMed
Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced record revenue and income for the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013. Revenue for the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013 was $383.6 million, a 10% increase (also a 10% increase on a constant currency basis) over the
quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2012. For the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013, net income was $84.9 million, an increase of 31% compared to the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2012. Diluted earnings per share for the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013
were $0.58, an increase of 32% compared to the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2012. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SG&amp;A expenses were $109.6 million for the quarter ended
March&nbsp;31, 2013, an increase of $7.7 million, or 8% (also an 8% increase on a constant currency basis) compared to the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2012. SG&amp;A costs were 28.6% of revenue in the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013, compared to
29.2% for the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2012. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">R&amp;D expenses were $31.2 million for the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013, an increase
of $2.8 million, or 10% (an 11% increase on a constant currency basis) compared to the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2012. R&amp;D expenses were 8.1% of revenue in the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013, consistent with 8.1% for the quarter ended
March&nbsp;31, 2012. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The company amortized acquired intangibles of $2.5 million ($1.9 million, net of tax) during the quarter ended
March&nbsp;31, 2013. Stock-based compensation costs incurred during the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2013 of $10.2 million ($7.2 million, net of tax) consisted of expenses associated with stock options, restricted stock units, and our employee stock
purchase plan. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">For the nine months ended March&nbsp;31, 2013, revenue was $1.1 billion, an increase of 10% over the nine months ended
March&nbsp;31, 2012 (a 12% increase on a constant currency basis). For the nine months ended March&nbsp;31, 2013, income from operations and net income were $268.7 million and $234.1 million, an increase of 31% and 32%, respectively, compared to the
