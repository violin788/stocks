 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="right"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

<B>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</B> </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>ResMed Inc. Announces Results for the First Quarter of Fiscal Year 2017
</B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Revenue increased 13% to $465.4 million; also up 13% on a constant currency basis </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.54; non-GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.62 </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Operating cash flow of $86.2 million in the first quarter </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>San Diego, October 25, 2016 </B>&#150; ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced results for its quarter ended September 30, 2016. Revenue for the quarter
was $465.4 million, a 13 percent increase compared to the same period of the prior year. Excluding the contribution from the Brightree acquisition, revenue for the quarter was $432.4 million, a 5 percent increase.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We had a solid start to our fiscal year with 13 percent constant currency revenue growth led by our Brightree software offerings and global device
sales,&#148; said Mick Farrell, ResMed&#146;s chief executive officer. &#147;We continue to demonstrate the value of our solutions, and earlier today, announced the world&#146;s largest study on patient adherence. During the quarter, we drove
consumer sleep awareness through a new U.S. media partnership, and sponsored a positive clinical study showing that non-invasive ventilation significantly reduces the risk of re-hospitalization and death for COPD.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Farrell concluded, &#147;In 2017, we continue to focus on developing and commercializing new, innovative products and solutions that improve patient outcomes,
create efficiencies for our customers, and help physicians and providers better manage chronic disease and lower healthcare costs. We have an exciting pipeline of new products, and look forward to bringing them to market.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>Analysis of first quarter results </I></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">First quarter
revenue in the Americas was $301.0 million, an 18 percent increase over the same period of the prior year. This included Brightree revenue of $33.1 million.&nbsp;Excluding Brightree, revenue in the Americas was $267.9 million, a 5 percent increase
over the prior year. Revenue in combined EMEA and APAC was $164.5 million, an increase of 5 percent on a constant currency basis compared to the same period of the prior year. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Gross margin in the first quarter was 57.8 percent, which included a field safety notification expense of $5.1 million relating to the battery in our Astral
ventilation device.&nbsp;Excluding the field safety notification expense, the gross margin for Q1 FY17 was 58.9 percent, higher than the prior year&#146;s quarter, mainly due to manufacturing and procurement efficiencies and an incremental
contribution from the Brightree acquisition, partly offset by changes in product mix and average selling prices. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Income from operations for the quarter
was $94.1 million, a 4 percent decline compared with the quarter ended September 30, 2015. Non-GAAP income from operations for the quarter was $111.0 million, an 11 percent increase compared to the same period of the prior year.</P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Selling, general and administrative expenses were $128.9 million, a 16 percent increase over the same period in the prior year, or a 15 percent increase on a
constant currency basis. SG&amp;A expenses increased to 27.7 percent of revenue in the quarter, compared with 27.0 percent reported in the quarter ended September 30, 2015.</P>

<p Style='page-break-before:always'>

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Research and development expenses were $34.4 million, or 7.4 percent of revenue. R&amp;D expenses increased by 27

$0.54. Non-GAAP diluted earnings per share increased 5 percent to $0.62.</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Cash flow from operations for the quarter was $86.2 million compared to net
