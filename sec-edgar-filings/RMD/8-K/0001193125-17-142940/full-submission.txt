</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>ResMed Inc. Announces Results for the Third Quarter of Fiscal Year 2017 </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Revenue increased 13% to $514.2&nbsp;million; up 14% on a constant currency basis </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.62; <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-GAAP</FONT> diluted earnings per share of $0.71
</I></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Operating cash flow of $67.6&nbsp;million in the third quarter </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>SAN DIEGO, April</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;27, 2017</B> &#150; ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced results for its quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2017. Revenue
for the quarter was $514.2&nbsp;million, a 13&nbsp;percent increase compared to the same period of the prior year. Excluding the contribution from the Brightree business acquired in April 2016, revenue for the quarter was $479.2&nbsp;million, a 6
percent increase. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We had solid double-digit constant currency revenue growth in Q3, led by our Brightree software solutions as well as mask and
device sales,&#148; said Mick Farrell, ResMed&#146;s chief executive officer. &#147;This quarter we saw strong demand for our new AirFit 20 range of masks. We also made significant progress on software innovation with the launch of enhanced
integration capabilities with Brightree and our AirSolutions cloud-based software platform.&nbsp;In our current quarter, we are launching our latest market-leading innovation: the ResMed AirMini &#150; the world&#146;s smallest CPAP.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Farrell concluded, &#147;Our focus on changing 20&nbsp;million lives by 2020 has driven a pipeline of new products and connected care solutions that improve
patient outcomes, create efficiencies for our homecare customers, and help physicians and providers better manage chronic disease while lowering healthcare costs.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>Analysis of third quarter results </I></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Third quarter
revenue in the Americas was $332.1&nbsp;million, an 18&nbsp;percent increase over the same period of the prior year. This included Brightree revenue of $35.0&nbsp;million. Excluding Brightree, revenue in the Americas was $297.1&nbsp;million, a
5&nbsp;percent increase over the prior year. Revenue in combined EMEA and APAC was $182.1&nbsp;million, an increase of 9&nbsp;percent on a constant currency basis, compared to the same period of the prior year. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Gross margin in the third quarter was 58.3&nbsp;percent, higher than the prior year&#146;s quarter gross margin of 57.3&nbsp;percent. The improvement in gross
margin compared to prior year&#146;s quarter was due to manufacturing and procurement efficiencies and an incremental contribution from the Brightree acquisition. </P>

<p Style='page-break-before:always'>
<HR  SIZE="3" style="COLOR:#999999" WIDTH="100%" ALIGN="CENTER">

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Income from operations for the quarter was $107.4&nbsp;million, a 3&nbsp;percent increase compared with the
quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2016. <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Non-GAAP</FONT> income from operations for the quarter was $126.7&nbsp;million, a 13&nbsp;percent increase compared to the same period of the prior year. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Selling, general and administrative expenses were $137.9&nbsp;million, a 16&nbsp;percent increase over the same period in the prior year, also a
16&nbsp;percent increase on a constant currency basis. SG&amp;A expenses increased to 26.8&nbsp;percent of revenue in the quarter, compared with 26.3&nbsp;percent reported in the quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2016.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Research and development expenses were $35.1&nbsp;million, or 6.8&nbsp;percent of revenue. R&amp;D expenses increased by 25&nbsp;percent compared with the
same period last year, or a 21&nbsp;percent increase on a constant currency basis. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Amortization of acquired intangible assets was $11.4&nbsp;million
during the quarter, an increase of $6.8&nbsp;million compared with the same period last year. The increase in amortization of acquired intangible assets was primarily due to the amortization expense associated with the Brightree acquisition.
Stock-based compensation costs incurred during the quarter of $11.5&nbsp;million consisted of expenses associated with employee equity grants, and our employee stock purchase plan. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Net income for the quarter was $87.8&nbsp;million, a 3&nbsp;percent decrease compared to the same period of the prior year.
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Non-GAAP</FONT> net income was $100.7&nbsp;million, a 3&nbsp;percent increase compared to the prior year. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Non-GAAP</FONT> measures adjust for amortization of acquired intangibles, acquisition related expenses and restructuring expenses. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">GAAP diluted earnings per share for the quarter decreased 3&nbsp;percent to $0.62. <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Non-GAAP</FONT> diluted earnings per share
of $0.71 were 3 percent higher compared with the same period of the prior year. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Cash flow from operations for the quarter was $67.6&nbsp;million compared
to net income in the current quarter of $87.8&nbsp;million. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>Dividend program </I></B></P>
