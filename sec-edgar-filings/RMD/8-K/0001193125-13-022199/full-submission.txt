 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>RESMED INC. ANNOUNCES RECORD FINANCIAL RESULTS FOR THE </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>QUARTER ENDED AND SIX MONTHS ENDED DECEMBER 31, 2012 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SAN DIEGO, California, January&nbsp;24, 2013 &#150; ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced record revenue and income for the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2012. Revenue for the quarter ended
December&nbsp;31, 2012 was $376.5 million, a 13% increase (a 14% increase on a constant currency basis) over the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2011. For the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2012, net income was $77.9 million, an increase of 24%
compared to the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2011. Diluted earnings per share for the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2012 were $0.53, an increase of 26% compared to the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2011. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SG&amp;A expenses were $107.8 million for the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2012, an increase of 7% (an 8% increase on a constant currency basis) over
the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2011. The increase in SG&amp;A was primarily due to expenses necessary to support sales growth. SG&amp;A costs were 28.6% of revenue in the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2012, compared to 30.2% in the quarter
ended December&nbsp;31, 2011. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">R&amp;D expenses were $30.3 million for the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2012, or 8.1% of revenue. R&amp;D
expenses increased by 11% (also an 11% increase on a constant currency basis) compared to the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2011. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The
company amortized acquired intangibles of $2.5 million ($1.9 million, net of tax) during the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2012. Stock-based compensation costs incurred during the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2012 of $9.5 million ($6.5 million,
net of tax) consisted of expenses associated with stock options, restricted stock units, and our employee stock purchase plan. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">For the six
months ended December&nbsp;31, 2012, revenue was $716.3 million, an increase of 11% over the six months ended December&nbsp;31, 2011 (a 13% increase on a constant currency basis). For the six months ended December&nbsp;31, 2012, net income was
