 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="right"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B>ResMed Inc. Announces Results for the Second Quarter of Fiscal Year 2016 </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Revenue increased 7% to $454.5 million; up 13% on a constant currency basis </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.64; non-GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.69 </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Operating cash flow of $147.4 million in the second quarter </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Company repurchased 700,000 shares in the second quarter </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL"><B>San Diego, January&nbsp;21, 2016</B> &#150; ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced results for its quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2015. Revenue for the quarter was
$454.5 million, a 7 percent increase compared with the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2014; this is a 13 percent increase on a constant currency basis. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">&#147;We
remain focused on delivering strong long-term growth from our sleep-disordered breathing and respiratory care businesses.&nbsp;Our team continued to deliver double-digit constant currency growth this quarter,&#148; said Mick Farrell, ResMed&#146;s
chief executive officer. &#147;Our Americas region was a significant contributor this quarter, as our customers continued to show their strong preference for our offerings, including Air Solutions cloud-based software, AirSense devices, and AirFit
patient interface systems. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">&#147;We have expanded our respiratory care business into the field of long-term oxygen therapy. Last week we announced a definitive
agreement to acquire Inova Labs in Austin, Texas. This will add portable oxygen concentrators, complementing our non-invasive ventilators, and providing yet another ResMed therapy option for patients with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, or
COPD.&#148;</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Farrell&nbsp;continued, &#147;In the 15 months since we launched our Air Solutions platform, ResMed has transformed into a tech-driven medical device
company.&nbsp;As the global leader of connected care for medical devices, we are liberating healthcare data, improving outcomes, and unlocking value by providing actionable information for patients, physicians, providers and&nbsp;payors, literally
every morning.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL"><B><I>Analysis of second quarter results </I></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">In the
second quarter of fiscal year 2016, revenue in the Americas was $269.5 million, a 17 percent increase over the prior year&#146;s quarter. Revenue in combined EMEA and APAC was $185.0 million, a 4 percent decrease compared with the quarter ended
December&nbsp;31, 2014; this is a 7 percent increase on a constant currency basis. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Gross margin in the second quarter was 58.6 percent, but this included a
one-time benefit relating to the release of $2.4 million in accrued expenses associated with the SERVE-HF field safety notice. Excluding this benefit, gross margin for Q2 FY16 was 58.1 percent; this is lower than the prior year&#146;s quarter,
mainly due to declines in average selling prices and an unfavorable geographic and product mix. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Income from operations for the quarter was $108.0 million, a 1
percent decrease compared with the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2014. Non-GAAP income from operations for the quarter was $116.9 million, a 5 percent increase compared with the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2014. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Selling, general and administrative expenses were $118.2 million for the second quarter of fiscal year 2016, a 4 percent decrease over the quarter ended
December&nbsp;31, 2014; this is a 4 percent increase on a constant currency basis. SG&amp;A expenses improved to 26.0 percent of revenue in the quarter, compared with 29.0 percent in the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2014. </P>

<p Style='page-break-before:always'>
<HR  SIZE="3" style="COLOR:#999999" WIDTH="100%" ALIGN="CENTER">

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Research and development expenses were $29.0 million for the second quarter of fiscal year 2016, or 6.4 percent of revenue.
R&amp;D expenses decreased by 1 percent compared with the quarter ended December&nbsp;31, 2014; this is a 14 percent increase on a constant currency basis. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">Amortization of acquired intangible assets was $4.4 million during the quarter. Stock-based compensation costs incurred during the quarter of $11.5 million consisted of
