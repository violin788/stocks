</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:30pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B>ResMed Inc. Announces Results for the Fourth Quarter of Fiscal Year 2017 </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:10pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Revenue increased 7% to $556.7&nbsp;million; up 8% on a constant currency basis </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:10pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>GAAP diluted earnings per share of $0.71; <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-GAAP</FONT> diluted earnings per share of $0.77 </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:10pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Operating cash flow of $140.3&nbsp;million in the fourth quarter </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:10pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Quarterly dividend increased by 6% to $0.35 per share </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL"><B>SAN DIEGO, August</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;1, 2017</B> &#150; ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced results for its quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2017. Revenue for the

6&nbsp;percent increase. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:10pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:ARIAL">&#147;We finished the year with solid constant currency revenue growth, fuelled by sales of our devices, masks, and <FONT
