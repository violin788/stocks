</B></P></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><I>Appointment of Jan De Witte to Board of Directors </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On May&nbsp;16, 2019, ResMed Inc. appointed Jan De Witte to serve on its board of directors, with a term expiring at ResMed&#146;s 2019 annual meeting of
stockholders, or until Mr.&nbsp;De Witte&#146;s earlier death, disability, resignation or removal. Mr.&nbsp;De Witte is the chief executive officer and member of the board of directors of Barco NV. Mr.&nbsp;De Witte will serve on ResMed&#146;s audit
committee. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr.&nbsp;De Witte was not selected to serve on ResMed&#146;s board of directors under any arrangement or understanding between Mr.&nbsp;De
Witte and any other person. ResMed is not aware of any transactions with Mr.&nbsp;De Witte that would require disclosure under Item 404(a) of Regulation <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">S-K.</FONT> </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In accordance with ResMed&#146;s customary practice, ResMed is entering into its standard form of indemnification agreement with Mr.&nbsp;De Witte, which will
require ResMed to indemnify him against certain liabilities that may arise as result of his status or service as a director. The description of Mr.&nbsp;De Witte&#146;s indemnification agreement is qualified in its entirety by the full text of
ResMed&#146;s standard form of indemnification agreement, which is incorporated here by reference to Exhibit 10.1 to ResMed&#146;s current report on Form <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">8-K</FONT> filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission
on June&nbsp;24, 2009. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As a <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-executive</FONT> director, Mr.&nbsp;De Witte will participate in ResMed&#146;s director
compensation program on a pro rata basis, as described under the caption &#147;Director Compensation&#151;2018&#148; in ResMed&#146;s proxy statement filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on October&nbsp;3, 2018. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><I>Expected Retirement of John Wareham from Board of Directors </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Also on May&nbsp;15, 2019, John Wareham, a member of ResMed&#146;s board of directors, announced that he intends to retire from the board of directors,
effective at ResMed&#146;s 2019 annual meeting of stockholders, which is anticipated to occur in November 2019. Mr.&nbsp;Wareham&#146;s intention to retire is not due to any disagreement with ResMed on any matter relating to ResMed&#146;s
operations, policies or practices. </P>
