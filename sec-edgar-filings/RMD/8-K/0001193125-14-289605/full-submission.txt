 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="right"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></P> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>RESMED Inc. Announces Results for the Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year 2014 </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Fourth quarter revenue of $415 million </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Fourth quarter GAAP earnings per share of $0.61 and non-GAAP earnings per share of $0.64 </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Operating cash flow of $115.6 million in the fourth quarter </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>Quarterly dividend increased by 12% to $0.28 per share </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>SAN DIEGO, July&nbsp;31, 2014</B> &#150; ResMed Inc. (NYSE: RMD) today announced results for its fourth quarter and for the fiscal year ended June&nbsp;30,
2014. Revenue for the quarter was $415.2 million, flat compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013 (a 1 percent decrease on a constant currency basis). Net income was $87.7 million, an increase of 20 percent compared to the quarter ended
June&nbsp;30, 2013. Diluted earnings per share for the quarter were $0.61, an increase of 22 percent compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The results for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2014 were impacted by a $6.3 million ($4.2 million, net of tax) expense for restructuring charges relating to
employee termination benefits associated with our reorganization of our commercial and research and development teams. Non-GAAP net income for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2014 was $92.0 million, a 1 percent increase compared to the quarter ended
June&nbsp;30, 2013 (non-GAAP measures exclude the impact of the restructuring expenses in the current quarter and the charge for Sydney University education, research and settlement expenses in the prior year quarter). Non-GAAP diluted earnings per
share for the quarter were $0.64, a 3 percent increase compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;Our fourth quarter results were lower than
anticipated, the result of softer sales in the Americas, partially offset by good growth in our international markets,&#148; said ResMed Chief Executive Officer Mick Farrell. &#147;We continue to benefit from our globally diversified business, with
constant currency revenue growth of 5 percent in our combined Europe and Asia-Pacific businesses.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Highlighting ResMed&#146;s new products, Farrell
continued, &#147;We see good adoption of our <B>Air</B>Fit<B>&#153; </B>P10 nasal pillows system which is currently the market leader among new patients, and although it is early, we are seeing encouraging traction for our <B>Air</B>Fit<B>&#153;
</B>N10 nasal mask and <B>Air</B>Fit<B>&#153; </B>F10 full-face mask which were launched in the fourth quarter around the world. On the flow generator side in the fourth quarter, we launched the <B>Astral&#153;</B> platform, our new generation of
life support ventilators, and are seeing strong uptake in Europe and Asia as sales begin to ramp. During the fourth quarter, we received FDA clearance for the Astral platform in the U.S. and anticipate a U.S. launch by the end of the calendar
year.&#148; </P>

<p Style='page-break-before:always'>
<HR  SIZE="3" style="COLOR:#999999" WIDTH="100%" ALIGN="CENTER">

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Farrell concluded, &#147;We remain focused on capital management, as we continued to repurchase our shares during
the quarter. And today, we announced an increase in our quarterly dividend &#150; the second increase since we began paying a dividend in September 2012. We are executing against our Three Horizons strategy and expect to return to constant currency
top-line growth as we ramp up our new product offerings over fiscal year 2015. These ResMed solutions are emblematic of our goal to improve patient outcomes and quality of life, while reducing costs for global healthcare systems. In tougher times,
we remain committed to disciplined management of our expenses while appropriately funding our long-term opportunities. We remain excited about the long-term prospects of our business, with ongoing growth opportunities in treating sleep-disordered
breathing, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, and cardiorespiratory diseases.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>Analysis of fourth quarter and fiscal year 2014 results
</I></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In the fourth quarter of fiscal year 2014, revenue outside the Americas was $200.3 million, a 9 percent increase over the prior year&#146;s
quarter (5 percent on a constant currency basis). Revenue in the Americas was $214.9 million, a 7 percent decrease compared to $230.3 million in the prior year&#146;s quarter. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Gross margin in the fourth quarter was 62.9 percent, 20 basis points higher than the same period in the prior year, due to favorable foreign currency
movements, manufacturing improvements, and favorable geographic mix, which were partially offset by declines in average selling prices. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Selling, general
and administrative expenses were $122.2 million for the quarter, a 6 percent increase (also a 6 percent increase on a constant currency basis) over the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013. SG&amp;A costs were 29.4 percent of revenue in the quarter,
compared to 27.8 percent in the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013, primarily due to higher legal expenses associated with patent litigation. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Research and
development expenses were $31.8 million for the quarter, or 7.7 percent of revenue. R&amp;D expenses increased by 1 percent (a 3 percent increase on a constant currency basis) compared to the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2013. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Operating profit for the fourth quarter was $98.5 million and cash flow from operations was $115.6 million. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">ResMed amortized acquired intangibles of $2.4 million ($1.8 million, net of tax) during the quarter. Stock-based compensation costs incurred during the
quarter of $10.8 million ($7.6 million, net of tax) consisted of expenses associated with stock options, restricted stock units, and the company&#146;s employee stock purchase plan. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">For the 12 months ended June&nbsp;30, 2014, revenue was $1,555.0 million, a 3 percent increase over the 12 months ended June&nbsp;30, 2013 (a 2 percent
increase on a constant currency basis). For the 12 months, net income was $345.3 million, a 12 percent increase compared to the 12 months ended June&nbsp;30, 2013. Diluted earnings per share for the 12 months were $2.39 per diluted share, a 14
percent increase compared to the 12 months ended June&nbsp;30, 2013. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B><I>Share repurchase program </I></B> </P>
