<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 1.01</font></b><b><font size="1" style="font-size:8.5pt;font-weight:bold;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font></b><b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Entry into a Material Definitive

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The
Compensation Committee of the Registrant&#146;s Board of Directors periodically
reviews the compensation paid to non-executive directors for service on the
Board.&#160; The Committee is assisted in its
review by a compensation consultant, Hewitt Associates,&nbsp;Inc. and the
objective is to provide a compensation program for Registrant&#146;s non-executive
directors which is competitive with programs offered by companies similarly
situated to the Registrant.&#160; Following
its most recent review, the Committee determined, subject to Board approval,
that an increase in the compensation paid to non-executive directors was
necessary in order for the Registrant&#146;s compensation program to remain
competitive.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On
November&nbsp;22, 2005, the Registrant&#146;s Board of Directors approved an
increase in the compensation paid to non-executive directors based on the
recommendation of the Compensation Committee.&#160;
Beginning in 2006, each non-executive director will be awarded 1,000
restricted stock units under Registrant&#146;s 2004 Long-Term Incentive Plan on an
annual basis.&#160; The restricted stock units
will vest if the director serves on the Registrant&#146;s Board of Directors for at
least one year following the date the restricted stock units are granted.&#160; After the restricted stock units vest, an
equal number of shares of Common Stock will be issued to each non-executive
director.&#160; A copy of the Registrant&#146;s
Long-Term Incentive Plan has been previously filed with the Commission.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:black;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">As
previously reported in filings with the Commission, other elements of the
compensation program for Registrant&#146;s non-executive directors include an annual
retainer consisting of $25,000 paid in cash and $20,000 paid in shares of
Common Stock of the Registrant and a fee of $1,500 for each Board meeting
attended.&#160; Members of Board Committees,
who are not chairs of a Committee, also receive $1,200 for each Committee
meeting they attend and an additional annual retainer of $2,500.&#160; Directors who serve as Committee Chairs
receive $1,500 for each Committee meeting attended and an additional annual
retainer of $10,000.&#160; Under the Directors&#146;
Non-Qualified Stock Option Plan, non-executive directors are granted options
each year for 2,500 shares of Common Stock and 2,500 shares of Common Stock
Non-Voting.</font></p>

<p align="left" style="color:black;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 5.02&#160; Departure of Directors
