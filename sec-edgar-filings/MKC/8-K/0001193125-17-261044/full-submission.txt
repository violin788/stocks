<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets. </B></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On August&nbsp;17, 2017,
McCormick&nbsp;&amp; Company, Incorporated, a Maryland corporation (&#147;McCormick&#148;), pursuant to the previously announced Stock Purchase Agreement (the &#147;Agreement&#148;) by and among McCormick, The R.T. French&#146;s Food Group Limited,
a private limited company incorporated in England and Wales (&#147;French&#146;s Seller&#148;), Reckitt Benckiser LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (&#147;Tiger&#146;s Milk Seller&#148; and, together with French&#146;s Seller, the
&#147;Sellers&#148;), and Reckitt Benckiser Group plc (&#147;Sellers&#146; Parent&#148;), acquired 100% of the outstanding equity interests of each of (i)&nbsp;The French&#146;s Food Company LLC, a Delaware limited liability company
(&#147;French&#146;s US&#148;), (ii)&nbsp;The French&#146;s Food Company, Inc., a Canadian corporation (&#147;French&#146;s Canada&#148;), (iii)&nbsp;The R.T. French&#146;s Food Company Limited, a private limited company incorporated in England and
Wales (&#147;French&#146;s UK&#148;), and (iv)&nbsp;Tiger&#146;s Milk LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (&#147;Tiger&#146;s Milk,&#148; and together with French&#146;s US, French&#146;s Canada and French&#146;s UK, the &#147;Acquired
Business&#148;), which is referred to herein as the &#147;Acquisition.&#148; Antitrust clearance for the Acquisition was obtained from the Federal Trade Commission on August&nbsp;14, 2017. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The consideration for the Acquisition totaled approximately $4.2&nbsp;billion in cash, and was funded with (a)&nbsp;borrowings under
McCormick&#146;s $1.5&nbsp;billion Term Loan Agreement (the &#147;Term Loan Agreement&#148;), dated August&nbsp;7, 2017 among McCormick, Bank of America, N.A., as administrative agent, and each lender party thereto, (b)&nbsp;amounts received from
the offering of $2.5&nbsp;billion aggregate principal amount of McCormick&#146;s senior unsecured notes, which closed on August&nbsp;11, 2017, and (c)&nbsp;amounts received from the offering of McCormick&#146;s common stock <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-voting,</FONT> which closed on August&nbsp;11, 2017. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The foregoing description of the Acquisition and the
Agreement does not purport to be complete and is subject to, and qualified in its entirety by reference to, the full text of the Agreement, which was filed as Exhibit 2.1 to McCormick&#146;s Current Report on
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Form&nbsp;8-K</FONT> filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the &#147;Commission&#148;) on July&nbsp;19, 2017, and is incorporated herein by reference. The Agreement has been filed to provide investors
and security holders with information regarding its terms. It is not intended to provide any other information about the Acquired Business, Sellers or McCormick. The Agreement contains representations, warranties and covenants of the parties thereto
made to and solely for the benefit of each other, and such representations, warranties and covenants may be subject to materiality and other qualifiers applicable to the contracting parties that differ from those that may be viewed as material to
investors. The assertions embodied in those representations, warranties and covenants are qualified by information in confidential disclosure schedules that the Sellers delivered in connection with the execution of the Agreement and were made only
as of the date of the Agreement. Accordingly, investors and security holders should not rely on the representations, warranties and covenants as characterizations of the actual state of facts. Moreover, information concerning the subject matter of

<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>McCormick Completes Acquisition of Reckitt Benckiser&#146;s Food Division </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">SPARKS, Md., &#150; August&nbsp;17, 2017 &#150; McCormick&nbsp;&amp; Company Inc. (NYSE: MKC), a global leader in flavor, today announced that it has
completed its acquisition of Reckitt Benckiser&#146;s Food Division (&#147;RB Foods&#148;) from Reckitt Benckiser Group plc (&#147;RB&#148;), which was previously announced on July&nbsp;18, 2017. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The brands acquired, including Frank&#146;s RedHot<SUP STYLE="font-size:85%; vertical-align:top">&reg;</SUP>, French&#146;s<SUP
STYLE="font-size:85%; vertical-align:top">&reg;</SUP> and Cattlemen&#146;s<SUP STYLE="font-size:85%; vertical-align:top">&reg;</SUP> are high quality assets and a natural strategic fit with McCormick&#146;s robust global branded flavor portfolio.
McCormick funded the purchase price of approximately $4.2&nbsp;billion, subject to certain customary purchase price adjustments, through a combination of new debt, comprised of senior unsecured notes and
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">pre-payable</FONT> term loans, and equity. The notes offering and equity offering were completed on August&nbsp;11, 2017. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We are thrilled to acquire Frank&#146;s RedHot, French&#146;s and other iconic, market-leading brands, as we complete a transaction that has been at the
top of our strategic list for over a decade and will generate significant shareholder value,&#148; said Lawrence E. Kurzius, Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer. &#147;McCormick is the perfect home for brands like Frank&#146;s RedHot and
French&#146;s as their simple, clean ingredients make them liquid spice. The addition of these highly complementary brands will allow McCormick to continue to take advantage of the growing trend toward spicy, flavorful eating with natural,
high-quality ingredients and advances our vision to Bring the Joy of Flavor to Life. The talented employees of RB Foods have built a great business, and we look forward to working with them to achieve continued success.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">McCormick expects to update its 2017 annual guidance, inclusive of the acquisition, on its third quarter earnings call on September&nbsp;28, 2017 at 8:00 a.m.
ET. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Forward-looking Information </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Certain information
