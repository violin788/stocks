<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">McCORMICK

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">SPARKS, MD, OCTOBER&nbsp;3 &#160;&#151; John P. Bilbrey, Senior Vice President of
The Hershey Company and President of Hershey International, has been appointed
to the Board of Directors of McCormick&nbsp;&amp; Company, Incorporated,
effective November&nbsp;22, 2005. &#160;In
this capacity, Mr.&nbsp;Bilbrey is responsible for the leadership and executive
management of all of Hershey&#146;s international operations.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Prior to joining Hershey, Mr.&nbsp;Bilbrey
served in a number of senior management roles, including Senior Vice President
of Mission Foods and President and Chief Executive Officer of Danone Waters of
North America.&#160; He also had more than 20
years of international consumer sales and marketing experience at Procter&nbsp;&amp;
Gamble, which included several overseas assignments.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Commented Robert J. Lawless,
Chairman, President&nbsp;&amp; CEO of McCormick, &#147;We are very pleased with this
appointment to our Board.&#160; Mr.&nbsp;Bilbrey&#146;s
extensive sales and marketing experience with international consumer products,
coupled with his executive management responsibilities, will be very important
to McCormick&#146;s future growth in the global marketplace.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">McCormick is the international
manufacturer and distributor of flavor products, including spices, herbs,
seasonings, flavorings and extracts, and other specialty foods.</font></p>
