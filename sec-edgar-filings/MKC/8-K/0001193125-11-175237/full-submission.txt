<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Seasonings and Mustards in Poland </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SPARKS, MD., June&nbsp;28 - McCormick&nbsp;&amp; Company, Incorporated (NYSE:MKC), a global leader in flavor, today announced that it has signed an agreement to purchase 100% of the shares of Kamis S.A.
Kamis is a privately held company based in Poland with distribution into Russia and other parts of Central and Eastern Europe. The completion of the agreement is expected to occur by September 2011 subject to regulatory approval. </FONT></P>

<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"> <P ALIGN="left"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>The addition of the Kamis business extends McCormick&#146;s strong presence in Western Europe into Poland and other markets in Central and Eastern
Europe. </B></FONT></P></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Alan Wilson, Chairman, President&nbsp;&amp; CEO of McCormick stated, &#147;This acquisition is
an excellent opportunity for McCormick to expand its portfolio of leading brands in Europe. Kamis is a brand leader in Poland where seasonings, mustard and other flavor products are in high demand. More than a third of consumers in Poland purchase
seasonings at least every two weeks. Across its entire portfolio, Kamis is growing sales at a mid-single digit rate. The management and employees of Kamis have built a strong business and we look forward to working with them to develop and launch
new products in other categories, accelerate penetration of other Central and Eastern European markets and expand in the food service channel.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Malcolm Swift, President McCormick Europe, Middle East and Africa added, &#147;This acquisition complements McCormick&#146;s strong brands in the U.K., France and other parts of Western Europe, as well as
its recently announced joint venture in Turkey. The agreement to acquire Kamis is in line with McCormick&#146;s growth strategy in Europe, Middle East and Africa. In addition, there is a strong fit between McCormick, a recognized flavor leader in
the region, and Kamis which has a solid reputation for high quality products and professionalism in Central and Eastern Europe.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Kamis is a leading consumer brand in Poland with approximately a 45% share of the spice and seasoning category and 30% share of the mustard category. Founded in
</FONT></P>
 <p STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">1 </FONT></P>

1991, the business has expanded beyond Poland with distribution subsidiaries in Russia, Romania and Ukraine, and exports into a number of other Central and Eastern Europe countries. In addition,
Kamis has expanded its business beyond spices, seasonings and mustards into a number of other flavor products including vinegars, sauces and marinades. By marketing under several brands and as a supplier of private label, Kamis offers consumers
everything from premium items to value-priced products. Customers include traditional grocery, supermarkets, hypermarkets and discount retailers, with a smaller portion of sales to restaurants, other food service operators and packaged food
