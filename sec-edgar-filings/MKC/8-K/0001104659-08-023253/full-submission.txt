<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; <b>Change in Certifying
Accountant Applies Only to Certain of Registrant&#146;s Benefit Plans: </b>&#160;On April&nbsp;2, 2008, the Audit Committee of
the Board of Directors of the Registrant approved the appointment of SB&nbsp;&amp;
Company, LLC and dismissed Ernst&nbsp;&amp; Young LLP as the auditor of the
following benefit plans of Registrant and certain of Registrant&#146;s subsidiary
companies: (i)&nbsp;the McCormick 401(k)&nbsp;Retirement Plan, (ii)&nbsp;the
Zatarain&#146;s Partnership L.P. 401(k)&nbsp;Savings Plan, and (iii)&nbsp;the Mojave
Foods Corporation 401(k)&nbsp;Retirement Plan.&#160;
SB&nbsp;&amp; Company, LLC will audit the aforementioned plans (the &#147;Plans&#148;)
for the Registrant&#146;s fiscal years ending November&nbsp;30, 2007, November&nbsp;30,
2008, and November&nbsp;30, 2009.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric ideograph-other;text-indent:.5in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">No
Dissatisfaction with Services: </font></b><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The change in accountants did not result from any
dissatisfaction with the quality of professional services rendered by Ernst&nbsp;&amp;
Young LLP.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric ideograph-other;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric ideograph-other;text-indent:.5in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">No
Adverse Opinion or Disagreement: </font></b><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The reports of Ernst&nbsp;&amp; Young LLP on the Plans&#146;
financial statements for the two most recent fiscal years ended prior to the
fiscal year ended November&nbsp;30, 2007, did not contain an adverse opinion or
disclaimer of opinion, nor were the reports qualified or modified as to
uncertainty, audit scope, or accounting principles.&#160; As well, there were no disagreements with
Ernst&nbsp;&amp; Young LLP on any matter of accounting principles or practices,
financial statement disclosures, or auditing scope or procedure with respect to
the Plans during the two most recent fiscal years ended prior to the fiscal
year ended November&nbsp;30, 2007, or at any time thereafter.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric ideograph-other;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric ideograph-other;text-indent:.5in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">No Prior
Consultation with New Accountant: </font></b><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">During the two most recent fiscal years ended prior to
the fiscal year ended November&nbsp;30, 2007, and prior to the appointment by
the Audit Committee of the Board of Directors of SB&nbsp;&amp; Company, LLC as
auditor of the Plans, neither the Plans nor anyone acting on behalf of the
Plans consulted SB&nbsp;&amp; Company, LLC about&#160; the application of accounting principles to a
specified transaction, the type of audit opinion that might be rendered on the
Plans&#146; financial statements, or any matter that was either the subject of a &#147;disagreement&#148;
or a &#147;reportable event&#148; (as described in Regulation SK, Item 304(a)(1)&nbsp;(iv)&nbsp;and
(v), respectively). Consequently, no written report or oral advice was provided
to the Plans by SB&nbsp;&amp; Company, LLC which that firm concluded was an
important factor considered by the Plans in reaching a decision as to an
accounting, auditing or financial reporting issue.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:ideograph-numeric ideograph-other;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

requested that Ernst&nbsp;&amp; Young LLP furnish a letter addressed to the
Securities and Exchange Commission stating that Ernst&nbsp;&amp; Young LLP
agrees with the above statements. A copy of Ernst&nbsp;&amp; Young&#146;s letter,
dated April&nbsp;8, 2008, is filed as Exhibit&nbsp;16 to this Form&nbsp;8-K.</font></p>
