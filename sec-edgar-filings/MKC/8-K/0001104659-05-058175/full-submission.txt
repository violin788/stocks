<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 2.05.</font></b><b><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;font-weight:bold;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font></b><b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Costs Associated with Exit or
Disposal Activities.</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On November&nbsp;22, 2005 the Board of Directors of
the Company approved a restructuring plan which is intended to simplify the
Company&#146;s business, improve operations, and facilitate a strategic
reorientation of the Company&#146;s industrial business. The plan includes the
consolidation of manufacturing facilities, rationalization of distribution
facilities, elimination of administrative redundancies, new initiatives in the
Company&#146;s go-to-market strategies, and inventory and SKU reductions.&nbsp; The
Company anticipates that these actions will occur over the next three years and
related charges will be recorded as phases of the plan are implemented.&nbsp;
The cumulative pre-tax impact is expected to be in the range of $130-$150 million,
which is estimated to include approximately $85 -100 million of pre-tax cash
flow. Of these charges, approximately 60% will consist of severance and other
personnel costs, 15% will consist of asset write-offs and 35% will consist of
other exit costs. Some costs will be recorded in 2005; however, the largest
portion of the cost is expected to be recorded in 2006 with lesser amounts in
2007 and 2008. &nbsp; It is anticipated that cost savings related to these
actions will be achieved beginning in 2006.&nbsp; In 2008, annual pre-tax
savings are expected to reach approximately $50 million.</font></p>
