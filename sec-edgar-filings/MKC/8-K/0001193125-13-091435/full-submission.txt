STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>McCormick Announces Mark Timbie&#146;s Plan to Retire </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">SPARKS, MD, MARCH 5,
2013&#150;McCormick&nbsp;&amp; Company&#146;s (NYSE:MKC), a global leader in flavor, today announced that Mark Timbie, President-Consumer Foods Americas&nbsp;&amp; Chief Administrative Officer, will be retiring at the end of June 2013. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Mr.&nbsp;Timbie has been a key leader for McCormick over the last 16 years. Besides serving as a member of the company&#146;s Management Committee,
Mark&#146;s career at McCormick has been in leadership roles for our U.S. Consumer business as well as President of McCormick Canada, President of McCormick&#146;s International business and President North American Consumer Foods. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Mr.&nbsp;Timbie joined McCormick as a very experienced CPG executive in 1996 as Vice President for Sales&nbsp;&amp; Marketing for the McCormick/Schilling
Division. He served as Board Advisor to the Sales Group in the late 1990s and was named Vice President&nbsp;&amp; General Manager for Perimeter Products and Consumer Marketing in 2000. He was promoted to President of McCormick Canada one year later.
</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">His dedication to the company continued as he led the organization through a very successful period of international expansion where he took
on various roles including President &#150; International Consumer Products Group. In this role, Mark was responsible for McCormick&#146;s consumer businesses in Europe, the Middle East, Africa, Asia Pacific, Canada, Mexico and Latin America as well
as McCormick&#146;s Global Export Department. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;I have had the privilege of working closely with Mark over the last decade, and I value
the contributions he has made to McCormick. His dedication and commitment serve as a textbook example for all us to follow,&#148; said Alan Wilson, McCormick Chairman, President and CEO. &#147;He is an important member of our senior team and will be
missed.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Mr.&nbsp;Timbie served on the Board of Directors for Tasty Baking Company and currently serves on the Board of Directors of the
Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of Baltimore. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>About McCormick </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">McCormick&nbsp;&amp; Company, Incorporated is a global leader in flavor. With $4 billion in annual sales, the company manufactures, markets and distributes spices, seasoning mixes, condiments and other
flavorful products to the entire food industry &#150; retail outlets, food manufacturers and foodservice businesses. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Every day, no matter
where or what you eat, you can enjoy food flavored by McCormick. <I>McCormick Brings Passion to Flavor&#153;</I>. </FONT></P>
