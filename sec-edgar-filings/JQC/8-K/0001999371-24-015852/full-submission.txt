<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white"><B>Item&nbsp;8.01. Other Events.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">Coale Mechlin has been named a portfolio manager
of Nuveen Credit Strategies Income Fund (the &ldquo;Fund&rdquo;), effective December 30, 2024.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">Coale&nbsp;Mechlin is a portfolio manager
on Nuveen&rsquo;s Leveraged Finance investment team, focusing on the platform&rsquo;s bank loan strategies. His responsibilities include
supporting the day-to-day oversight and monitoring of the platform&rsquo;s bank loan strategies.&nbsp;He&nbsp;joined Nuveen in 2017 as
a research analyst covering the Technology sector. Prior to joining Nuveen, he was a high yield/senior loan capital markets associate
for J.P. Morgan and an analyst for the UBS Investment Bank.&nbsp;He&nbsp;graduated with a B.A. in Economics from Wesleyan University.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">There have been no changes in Fund&rsquo;s
investment objectives or policies.</P>
