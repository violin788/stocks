 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify"><B>Item&nbsp;8.01. Other Events. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">On December&nbsp;1, 2020, the Board of Trustees of Nuveen Credit Strategies Income Fund (NYSE: JQC), a registered <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">closed-end</FONT> management investment company (the &#147;Fund&#148;), announced the approval of a new <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">sub-advisory</FONT> agreement between Nuveen Fund Advisors, LLC (&#147;Nuveen Fund
Advisors&#148;), the Fund&#146;s investment adviser, and Nuveen Asset Management, LLC (&#147;NAM&#148;). The new <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">sub-advisory</FONT> agreement was approved in connection with the forthcoming merger of the Fund&#146;s
current <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">sub-adviser,</FONT> Symphony Asset Management LLC (&#147;Symphony&#148;), with and into NAM, which is currently scheduled to close on December&nbsp;31, 2020. Symphony and NAM are both affiliates of Nuveen
Fund Advisors and all are subsidiaries of Nuveen, LLC. The new <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">sub-advisory</FONT> agreement will become effective upon the close of the merger. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:11pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">The terms of the new <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">sub-advisory</FONT> agreement with NAM are substantially identical to the current <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">sub-advisory</FONT> agreement with Symphony. NAM will provide the same level of services to the Fund, with the Symphony personnel who currently serve as portfolio managers of the Fund continuing to do so as NAM personnel.
In addition, this change will not impact the Fund&#146;s fees, investment objectives or policies. </P> <P STYLE="font-size:12pt; margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
