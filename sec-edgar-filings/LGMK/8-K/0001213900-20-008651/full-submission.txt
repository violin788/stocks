<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Nxt-ID, Inc. Announces Investor Webcast to Discuss the Financial

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: justify; margin: 0pt 0">Oxford, Connecticut, March 31, 2020 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) &mdash;
Nxt-ID, Inc. (NASDAQ:&nbsp;<U>NXTD</U>) (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;), a provider of healthcare devices and services, announces results
for the year and three months ended December 31, 2019.</P>

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Financial Highlights and results from the year and fourth quarter
included:</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" STYLE="width: 100%; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top">
