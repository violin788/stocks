<P STYLE="text-align: justify; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">/EIN News/ -- Oxford, Connecticut, Aug. 14, 2020 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE)
-- Nxt-ID, Inc.&nbsp;(NASDAQ:&nbsp;NXTD) (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;), a provider of healthcare devices and services, announces
results for the six and three months ended June 30, 2020.</P>

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 5pt">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Financial Highlights and results from the second quarter included:</P>

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 5pt">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%" STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top">

$6.2 million and $2.5 million, respectively, down significantly in both periods as compared to the same 2019 periods.</FONT></TD></TR></TABLE>

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 5pt"></FONT></P>

2019 periods.</FONT></TD></TR></TABLE>

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif"></P>

compared to $1.9 million and $1.0 million for the same 2019 periods.</FONT></TD></TR></TABLE>

ended June 30, 2020.</FONT></TD></TR></TABLE>

<P STYLE="margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 5pt">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&ldquo;2020 thus far has been very challenging and dynamic for
the Company as it has been for the rest of the world. We had made significant progress in terms of right sizing our business and
paying down our term debt facility and then COVID-19 struck the world. Like many other companies, our second quarter 2020 operating
results were significantly impacted by COVID-19. In spite of the reduction in our revenues, we were very close to breakeven for
the first six months ended June 30, 2020&rdquo; said Vin Miceli, Chief Executive Officer of Nxt-ID. &ldquo;We continue to make
significant progress with regards to our new product development efforts and we remain very optimistic about our future prospects
in a post COVID-19 environment.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 5pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Concluded Miceli: &ldquo;I look forward to explaining the quarter&rsquo;s
results,&nbsp;updating our shareholders and providing some context regarding our Company&rsquo;s path forward.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="text-align: justify; font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>About Nxt-ID, Inc.</B><BR>
Nxt-ID, Inc.&nbsp;(NASDAQ: NXTD) provides technology products and services for healthcare applications. The Company has extensive
experience in access control, biometric and behavior-metric identity verification, security and privacy, encryption and data protection,
payments, miniaturization and sensor technologies. Through its subsidiary, LogicMark LLC, Nxt-ID, Inc. is a manufacturer and distributor
of non-monitored and monitored personal emergency response systems sold through dealers/distributors and the United States Department
of Veterans Affairs. Learn more about Nxt-ID at&nbsp;www.nxt-id.com. For Nxt-ID, Inc. corporate information contact:&nbsp;info@nxt-id.com</P>
