<P STYLE="font: 18pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 10pt"><B>Direct Digital
Holdings Reports First Quarter 2022 Financial Results</B></FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B><I>First Quarter 2022 Revenue Up 100% Year-Over-Year
to $11.4 Million</I></B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>Houston, May 12, 2022</B> -- Direct Digital
Holdings, Inc. (Nasdaq: DRCT) (&ldquo;Direct Digital&rdquo;), a leading advertising and marketing technology platform, announced financial
results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2022.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Mark Walker
said, &ldquo;we are pleased to report record revenue for the first quarter of 2022, which demonstrates the increasing value of Direct
Digital&rsquo;s world-class buy- and sell-side advertising platform for middle-market clients. Our quarterly growth was primarily driven
by our sell-side advertising segment, and we are excited about the prospect of maintaining this momentum throughout 2022 by continuing
to innovate our programmatic advertising offerings for the middle market segment, enhance our publisher partner engagement and monetization

and settlement of seller notes and earnout liability, loss on early extinguishment of debt, and loss on early redemption of non-participating
preferred units, (&ldquo;Adjusted EBITDA&rdquo;), a non-GAAP measure, is useful in evaluating our operating performance. The most directly
comparable GAAP measure to Adjusted EBITDA is net loss.</P>
