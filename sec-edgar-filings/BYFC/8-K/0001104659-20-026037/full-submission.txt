<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>Broadway Financial Corporation Announces
Results for Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2019</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">LOS ANGELES, CA &ndash; (BUSINESS WIRE)
 &ndash; February 27, 2020 &ndash; Broadway Financial Corporation (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;) (NASDAQ Capital Market: BYFC), parent
company of Broadway Federal Bank, f.s.b. (the &ldquo;Bank&rdquo;), today reported a net loss of&nbsp;$69 thousand, or less than
$0.01 per share, for the fourth quarter of 2019 compared to net income of&nbsp;$275 thousand, or $0.01 per diluted share, for the
fourth quarter of 2018.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">During the fourth quarter of 2019, the
Bank reported increases in net interest income of $336 thousand, service charges on deposits of $25 thousand, and miscellaneous
fee income of $34 thousand compared to the fourth quarter of 2018. Results for the fourth quarter of 2019 were impacted, however,
by a loan loss provision of $294 thousand, resulting from growth in the Bank&rsquo;s loan portfolio, whereas results for the fourth
quarter of 2018 included a loan loss provision recapture of $254 thousand. Also, during the fourth quarter of 2019, the Company
did not sell any loans and therefore did not report any gain on sale of loans, whereas during the fourth quarter of 2018, the Bank
recorded a gain on sale of loans of $50 thousand. In addition, certain non-recurring professional expenses contributed to an increase
of $45 thousand in non-interest expense in the fourth quarter of 2019 compared to the fourth quarter of 2018.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt
