<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><FONT STYLE="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, Serif; font-size: 10pt"><B>&nbsp;</B></FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><FONT STYLE="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, Serif; font-size: 10pt">Bensenville,
IL &ndash; December 18, 2020&nbsp;&ndash; Rubicon Technology, Inc. (NASDAQ: RBCN) announced today that it has entered into an
amendment to extend its Section 382 Rights Agreement (the &ldquo;Rights Agreement&rdquo;). The Rights Agreement was entered into
in an effort to preserve stockholder value by protecting against a possible limitation on the Company&rsquo;s ability to use its
net operating loss carry-forwards, which for U.S. federal income tax purposes were estimated at approximately $188.1 million as
of December 31, 2019.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><FONT STYLE="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, Serif; font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

Rights Agreement was originally entered into in December 2017 and was scheduled to expire on December 18, 2020. The amendment,
which has been unanimously approved by the Board of Directors and was approved by stockholders at the Company&rsquo;s 2020 Annual
Meeting, extends the final expiration date of the Rights Agreement to December 18, 2023. <FONT STYLE="background-color: white">Except
for the extension of the final expiration date, the Rights Agreement otherwise remains unmodified. </FONT></FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><FONT STYLE="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, Serif; font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;</FONT></P>

the Securities and Exchange Commission o</FONT>n December 18, 2017. Additional information regarding the amendment of the Rights
Agreement&nbsp;is included in the Current Report on Form 8-K and the Registration Statement on Form 8-A/A filed today by the Company

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><FONT STYLE="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, Serif; font-size: 10pt">Rubicon

custom sapphire products with superior quality and precision. Rubicon is ISO 9001 certified and ITAR registered.</FONT></P>
