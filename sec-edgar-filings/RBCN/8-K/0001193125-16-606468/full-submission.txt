meet customer needs and specifications. Its largest sapphire product lines are cores, polished wafers, and patterned sapphire substrate (&#147;PSS&#148;) wafers. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The PSS product line, introduced in 2013, consists of patterned sapphire wafers of various sizes. LED manufacturers commonly etch a pattern
