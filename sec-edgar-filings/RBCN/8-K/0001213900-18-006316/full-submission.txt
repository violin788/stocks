<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">As further described under
Item 5.07 below, the annual meeting of stockholders (the &ldquo;Annual Meeting&rdquo;) of Rubicon Technology, Inc. (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;)
was held on May 10, 2018. At this meeting, the Company&rsquo;s stockholders approved, by an affirmative vote of the majority of
the Company&rsquo;s outstanding shares of common stock an amendment (the &ldquo;Amendment&rdquo;) to the Company&rsquo;s Certificate
of Incorporation (as amended the &ldquo;Certificate of Incorporation&rdquo;) to reduce the number of authorized shares of preferred
stock allowed under the Certificate of Incorporation from 5,000,000 to 1,000,000. The Board previously approved the Amendment and
recommended that it be submitted to the Company&rsquo;s stockholders for approval. On May 10, 2018, following the Annual Meeting,
the Board determined to effect the preferred stock reduction and approved the corresponding final form of the Certificate of Amendment
containing the Amendment, and the Company filed the Certificate of Amendment with the Secretary of State of the State of Delaware.
The Amendment is effective as of 12:01 a.m. (Delaware time) on May 15, 2018.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">Rubicon Technology, Inc.,
a corporation organized and existing under and by virtue of the laws of the State of Delaware (the &ldquo;Corporation&rdquo;),
does hereby certify as follows:</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">1.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The
name of the Corporation is Rubicon Technology, Inc.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">2.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;At
a meeting of the Board of Directors of the Corporation (the &ldquo;Board&rdquo;), resolutions were duly adopted setting forth a
proposed amendment (the &ldquo;Amendment&rdquo;) of the Eighth Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation of the Corporation
(the &ldquo;Certificate of Incorporation&rdquo;), declaring said amendment to be advisable and calling a meeting of the stockholders
of the Corporation for consideration thereof. The Amendment is set forth in paragraph 3 below.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">3.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The
Certificate of Incorporation be, and hereby is, amended by deleting Article 4, Capital Stock, in its entirety, and substituting
in lieu thereof, a new Article 4 as follows:</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center"><B>ARTICLE 4</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center"><B>CAPITAL STOCK</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 1in">The total number of shares
of capital stock which the Corporation shall have the authority to issue is 9,200,000 shares which is divided into two classes
as follows: 1,000,000 shares of Preferred Stock (&ldquo;Preferred Stock&rdquo;) with a par value of $.001 per share, and 8,200,000
shares of Common Stock (&ldquo;Common Stock&rdquo;) with a par value of $.001 per share.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 1in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Pursuant
to a resolution of the Board, a meeting of the stockholders of the Corporation was duly called and held upon notice in accordance
with Section 222 of the DGCL at which meeting the necessary number of shares as required by statute were voted in favor of the
Amendment.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">5.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The
Amendment was duly adopted in accordance with the provisions of Section 242 of the DGCL.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

accordance with Section 103(d) of the DGCL, the Amendment shall become effective on the date and at the time set forth below:</P>
