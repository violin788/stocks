<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">On June 23, 2020, Rubicon Sapphire Technology
(Malaysia) SDN. BHD. (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;), a wholly owned subsidiary of Rubicon Technology, Inc. (&ldquo;RTI&rdquo;), completed
the previously announced sale to Computime (Malaysia) SDN. BHD (the &ldquo;Purchaser&rdquo;) of its sixty (60)-year leasehold land
held under PN 11295, Lot 10010 (previously H.S.(D) 57673, PT 4627), Mukim 01, Daerah Seberang Perai Tengah, Negeri Pulau Pinang,
Malaysia measuring 12,383 square metres in area together with all the buildings erected thereon bearing assessment address No.
3065, Tingkat Perusahaan 4A, Kawasan Perusahaaan Perai, 13600 Perai, Penang (the &ldquo;Property&rdquo;) pursuant to the terms
and conditions of the Sale and Purchase Agreement (the &ldquo;Agreement&rdquo;), dated as of December 19, 2019.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">The full text of the Agreement was filed as
Exhibit 10.1 to RTI&rsquo;s Current Report on Form 8-K filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on December 19, 2019 and
is incorporated herein by reference.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">The gross sale price for the Property was Ringgit
Malaysia 20,750,000 (approximately $4.8 million based upon today&rsquo;s exchange rate which is subject to significant fluctuation
on a daily basis). The Company realized net proceeds of approximately Ringgit Malaysia 20,000,000 (approximately $4.6 million based
upon today&rsquo;s exchange rate which is subject to significant fluctuation on a daily basis) after the payment of consent fees,
real estate taxes, brokerage and legal fees, transfer and withholding taxes and other expenses.</P>
