<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement. </B></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On September&nbsp;9, 2016, Rubicon
Technology, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) voluntarily terminated its Loan and Security Agreement with Silicon Valley Bank (the &#147;Lender&#148;) dated January&nbsp;2, 2013, as amended on August&nbsp;6, 2015 (as amended, the &#147;Loan
Agreement&#148;). Prior to its termination, the Loan Agreement provided the Company with a revolving line of credit which is described under the heading &#147;Credit Facility&#148; in Note 4 to the financial statements contained in Item&nbsp;1 of
Part I of the Company&#146;s Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2016, and which description is incorporated herein by reference. Termination of the Loan Agreement was evidenced by a Pay-Off Letter executed by the
Lender and the Company (the &#147;Pay-Off Letter&#148;). The Loan Agreement was terminated because it no longer served the Company&#146;s needs and in order for the Company to save costs otherwise incurred in connection with maintaining the line of
credit. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The pay-off amount in connection with the termination of the Loan Agreement was $2,754.73 and primarily consisted of the unused
revolving line facility fee; as of September&nbsp;9, 2016, no principal was outstanding under the revolving line of credit. The Company did not incur any early termination penalties in connection with the termination. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Pursuant to the Pay-Off Letter, upon payment of the pay-off amount, all obligations under the Loan Agreement were paid and discharged in full,
all unfunded commitments by the Lender to make credit extensions to the Company under the Loan Agreement were terminated, all security interests granted to or held by the Lender under the Loan Agreement were released, and all guaranties supporting
the Loan Agreement were released. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The foregoing description of the Pay-Off Letter does not purport to be complete and is qualified in its
entirety by reference to the full text of the Pay-Off Letter, which is filed as Exhibit 10.1 hereto, and is incorporated herein by reference. </P> <P STYLE="font-size:18pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
