<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Ventyx Biosciences Reports Third Quarter Financial Results and Provides Business Update </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Wholly-owned pipeline with three clinical-stage programs targeting significant inflammatory and immunology disease markets </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Dosing initiated in a multiple-ascending dose (MAD) Phase 1 trial for VTX958, an oral, selective tyrosine kinase type 2 (TYK2) inhibitor,
and a Phase 1 trial for VTX2735, an oral <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">Nod-like</FONT> receptor protein 3 (NLRP3) inhibitor </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Completed upsized initial public offering (IPO) of common stock, raising approximately $174.3&nbsp;million in gross proceeds, including full
exercise of underwriters&#146; option to purchase additional shares </I></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">ENCINITAS, Calif., November 17, 2021 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) &#150;
Ventyx Biosciences, Inc. (Nasdaq: VTYX), (&#147;Ventyx&#148; or the &#147;Company&#148;), a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company focused on advancing new therapies for millions of patients living with inflammatory diseases and autoimmune
disorders, today announced financial results for the quarter ended September&nbsp;30, 2021. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="justify">&#147;I am proud of our team&#146;s
accomplishments as we work to build a leading immunology company that has three clinical stage programs,&#148; said Raju Mohan, Chief Executive Officer of Ventyx. &#147;By selectively modulating key immune targets we have been able to generate
differentiated drug candidates that have the potential to improve and expand treatment options for the millions of patients suffering from inflammatory diseases and autoimmune disorders. We believe our capital position, supplemented by funds raised
