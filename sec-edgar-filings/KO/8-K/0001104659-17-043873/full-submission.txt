<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On July&nbsp;5, 2017, the Company completed a cash tender offer and consent solicitation (the &#147;Offer and Solicitation&#148;) for two series of outstanding debt securities (the &#147;Extended CCR Notes&#148;) issued by its wholly owned subsidiary Coca-Cola Refreshments USA,&nbsp;Inc. (&#147;CCR&#148;). The Offer and Solicitation was made pursuant to an offer to purchase and consent solicitation statement (the &#147;Original Statement&#148;), dated May&nbsp;22, 2017, as amended on June&nbsp;6, 2017, and as further amended on June&nbsp;21, 2017 (as amended, the &#147;Amended Statement&#148;). The Company received the requisite consents on one series of Extended CCR Notes, the 8.500% Debentures due February 1, 2022 (the &#147;8.500% CCR Debentures&#148;).</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In connectio</font>n with the Offer and Solicitation, the Company and CCR entered into the Third Supplemental Indenture (the &#147;Third Supplemental Indenture&#148;), dated the date hereof, among the Company, CCR and Deutsche Bank Trust Company Americas, as trustee (the &#147;Trustee&#148;), with respect to the 8.500% CCR Debentures. The Third Supplemental Indenture effects certain amendments to the Indenture dated as of July&nbsp;30, 1991 (the &#147;Base Indenture&#148;), as amended by a First Supplemental Indenture dated as of January&nbsp;29, 1992 (the &#147;First Supplemental Indenture&#148;), between CCR and the Trustee.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Additionally, the Third Supplemental Indenture provides for full and unconditional guarantees of the 8.500% CCR Debentures, issued by the Company (the &#147;Company Guarantees&#148;). The Company Guarantees will be unsecured and unsubordinated obligations of the Company and will rank equally in right of payment will all of the Company&#146;s other unsecured and unsubordinated debt (including any unsecured and unsubordinated guarantees of the Company&#146;s subsidiaries&#146; indebtedness).</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Base Indenture, the First Supplemental Indenture and the Third Supplemental Indenture are filed as exhibits to this Current Report on Form&nbsp;8-K and are incorporated herein by reference.</font></p>
