<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On June&nbsp;22, 2017, the Company completed a cash tender offer and consent solicitation (the &#147;Offer and Solicitation&#148;) for nine series of outstanding debt securities (the &#147;CCR Notes&#148;) issued by its wholly owned subsidiary Coca-Cola Refreshments USA,&nbsp;Inc. (&#147;CCR&#148;). The Offer and Solicitation was made pursuant to a offer to purchase and consent solicitation statement (the &#147;Original Statement&#148;), dated May&nbsp;22, 2017, as amended on June&nbsp;6, 2017 (as amended, the &#147;Amended Statement&#148;). The CCR Notes include the 8.000% Debentures due September&nbsp;15, 2022, 6.750% Debentures due September&nbsp;15, 2023, 7.000% Debentures due October&nbsp;1, 2026, 6.950% Debentures due 2026, 6.750% Debentures due 2028, 6.700% Debentures due 2036, 5.710% Medium-Term Notes, 6.750% Debentures due 2038 and 7.000% Debentures due 2098.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In connectio</font>n with the Offer and Solicitation, the Company and CCR entered into the Second Supplemental Indenture (the &#147;Second Supplemental Indenture&#148;), dated the date hereof, among the Company, CCR and Deutsche Bank Trust Company Americas, as trustee (the &#147;Trustee&#148;), with respect to the Closing CCR Notes. The Second Supplemental Indenture effects certain amendments to the Indenture dated as of July&nbsp;30. 1991 (the &#147;Base Indenture&#148;), as amended by a First Supplemental Indenture dated as of January&nbsp;29, 1992 (the &#147;First Supplemental Indenture&#148;), between CCR and the Trustee.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Additionally, the Second Supplemental Indenture provides for full and unconditional guarantees of the Closing CCR Notes, issued by the Company (the &#147;Company Guarantees&#148;). The Company Guarantees will be unsecured and unsubordinated obligations of the Company and will rank equally in right of payment with all of the Company&#146;s other unsecured and unsubordinated debt (including any unsecured and unsubordinated guarantees of the Company&#146;s subsidiaries&#146; indebtedness).</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Base Indenture, the First Supplemental Indenture and the Second Supplemental Indenture are filed as exhibits to this Current Report on Form&nbsp;8-K and are incorporated herein by reference.</font></p>
