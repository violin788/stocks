announced on December 9, 2019, and that the Company will release its third quarter 2019 financial performance for the period ended

<TD STYLE="width: 0"></TD><TD STYLE="width: 1in"><B>Item 3.01.</B></TD><TD><B>Notice of Delisting or Failure to Satisfy a Continued Listing Rule or Standard; Transfer of Listing.</B></TD></TR></TABLE>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">As reported by the
Company in its Form 12b-25 Notification of Late Filing with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (the &ldquo;<U>SEC</U>&rdquo;)
on December 16, 2019, the Company was unable to file its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q (the &quot;<U>Form 10-Q</U>&quot;) for the
quarter ended October 31, 2019 within the prescribed time period.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">On December 24, 2019,
the Company received a notification letter from the Listing Qualifications Department of The Nasdaq Stock Market LLC (&quot;<U>NASDAQ</U>&quot;)
stating that NASDAQ has determined that the Company is not in compliance with NASDAQ Listing Rule 5250(c)(1) due to the Company&rsquo;s
failure to timely file its Form 10-Q for the quarter ended October 31, 2019 with the SEC.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The NASDAQ notification
letter notes that the Company is required to submit a plan to regain compliance with NASDAQ's filing requirements for continued
listing within sixty (60) calendar days of the date of the NASDAQ notification letter. Upon acceptance of the Company&rsquo;s compliance
plan, NASDAQ is permitted to grant an extension of up to one hundred eighty (180) days from the Form 10-Q's filing date for the
Company to regain compliance with NASDAQ's filing requirements for continued listing.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The NASDAQ notification
letter has no immediate effect on the listing or trading of the Company&rsquo;s common stock on The NASDAQ Capital Market.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The Company anticipates
that it will file its Form 10-Q with the SEC on or about January 6, 2020, and thereby regain compliance with the NASDAQ continued
listing requirements eliminating the need for the Company to submit a formal plan to regain compliance.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">A copy of the press
release, dated January 2, 2020, disclosing the Company&rsquo;s receipt of the NASDAQ notification letter is attached hereto as
Exhibit 99.2.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white"><B>Streamline Health<SUP>&reg;</SUP>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white"><B>Company Determined No Need To Amend
Previously Filed Financial Statements To Resolve Amortization Issues Announced December 9, 2019</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white"><FONT STYLE="font-family: Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><B>ATLANTA,
GA, January 2, 2020&nbsp;/&nbsp;</B></FONT><U>Streamline Health Solutions, Inc.</U>&nbsp;(NASDAQ:STRM), provider of integrated
solutions, technology-enabled services and analytics supporting revenue cycle optimization for healthcare enterprises today announced
that it will release its third quarter 2019 financial performance for the period that ended&nbsp;October 31, 2019, on&nbsp;Wednesday,
January 8, 2020&nbsp;after the close of the financial markets.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">As previously disclosed, the Company determined that certain
corrections were required for capitalized software development costs and related amortization with respect to previously reported
periods.&nbsp; Two corrections were required, categorized as follows:</P>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%" STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top">
<TD STYLE="width: 0.25in"></TD><TD STYLE="width: 0.5in">(i)</TD><TD>software enhancements that were generally released and not amortized in a timely manner, and</TD></TR></TABLE>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%" STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top">
<TD STYLE="width: 0.25in"></TD><TD STYLE="width: 0.5in">(ii)</TD><TD>amortization expense was calculated using an incorrect method.</TD></TR></TABLE>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">The first resulted from an administrative error, while the second
resulted from a misapplication of U.S. Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP).&nbsp; These corrections off-set each other
in the Company&rsquo;s previously issued financial statements.&nbsp; Additionally, these differences self-corrected in the Company&rsquo;s
financial statements through the periods from Fiscal Year 2016 through Fiscal Q3 2019.&nbsp; The Company evaluated the materiality
of the effect of these adjustments and determined it was not necessary to restate its previously issued financial statements.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">The Company intends to disclose these immaterial corrections
through an out of period adjustment in the Company&rsquo;s financial statements for the quarter ended October 31, 2019. The corrections
related primarily to Fiscal Years 2016 and 2017 with a cumulative under-amortization of $678,000 that is eliminated through over-amortization
in the following periods:</P>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%" STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top">
<TD STYLE="width: 0.25in"></TD><TD STYLE="width: 0.5in">(i)</TD><TD>$146,000 for Fiscal Year 2018,</TD></TR></TABLE>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%" STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top">
<TD STYLE="width: 0.25in"></TD><TD STYLE="width: 0.5in">(ii)</TD><TD>$153,000 for Fiscal Q1 2019,</TD></TR></TABLE>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%" STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top">
<TD STYLE="width: 0.25in"></TD><TD STYLE="width: 0.5in">(iii)</TD><TD>$165,000 for Fiscal Q2 2019,</TD></TR></TABLE>

<TABLE CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%" STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin-top: 0pt; margin-bottom: 0pt"><TR STYLE="vertical-align: top">
<TD STYLE="width: 0.25in"></TD><TD STYLE="width: 0.5in">(iv)</TD><TD>and $214,000 for Fiscal Q3 2019.</TD></TR></TABLE>

31, 2019. As of the end of Fiscal Q3 2019, the Company will have no more corrections necessary to accurately reflect the capitalized
software development asset, and related accumulated amortization. Further details regarding the corrections will be contained in
the Company&rsquo;s Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended October 31, 2019.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</P>
