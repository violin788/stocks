Capital Market under the symbol &#8220;STRM.&#8221; To regain compliance, the closing bid price of the Company&#8217;s common stock must

listing requirements for The Nasdaq Capital Market, with the exception of the Bid Price Requirement. If the Company meets these requirements,
the Company will have an additional 180 calendar days to comply in order for the Company&#8217;s common stock to remain listed on The
Nasdaq Capital Market. If the Company is not eligible for the second compliance period, then the Staff will provide notice that the Company&#8217;s
securities will be subject to delisting, which determination may be appealed to the Nasdaq Hearings Panel.</span></p>
