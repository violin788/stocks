<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">On June 20, 2021, the Board of Directors (the
 &#8220;<I>Board</I>&#8221;) of Skillsoft Corp., a Delaware corporation (the &#8220;<I>Company</I>&#8221;), approved by unanimous written

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Following such change, the date of the Company&#8217;s next fiscal
