<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On August&nbsp;7, 2015, the board of directors of Illinois Tool Works Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) elected Christopher O&#146;Herlihy, age 51, as a Vice
Chairman of the Company, to hold office until the May 2016 meeting of the Board of Directors or until his successor is elected. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr. O&#146;Herlihy joined
the Company in 1989 and prior to this promotion served as Executive Vice President of the Food Equipment segment since October, 2010. Prior to becoming an executive officer, he also served as a Group President for both the Polymers &amp; Fluids and
Food Equipment segments. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr.&nbsp;O&#146;Herlihy will receive an increase in annual base salary from $504,926 to $610,000 per year, effective as of
August&nbsp;7, 2015, and an increase in his annual incentive award target from 80% to 90% of year-end base salary. </P>
