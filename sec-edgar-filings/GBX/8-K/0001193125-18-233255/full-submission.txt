<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Lake Oswego, Oregon, July</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;31, 2018 </B>&#150; Gunderson Marine LLC (&#147;Gunderson Marine&#148;), a wholly-owned
subsidiary of The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (NYSE:GBX), announced today that it has signed a binding contract with Overseas Shipholding Group, Inc. (&#147;OSG&#148;), a provider of energy transportation services for crude oil and petroleum products
in the U.S. Flag markets, for the construction of one 204,000 barrel capacity oil and chemical tank barge for dual mode ITB service pursuant to U.S. Coast Guard NVIC <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">2-81,</FONT> Change 1, for anticipated delivery to
OSG during the second quarter of calendar 2020. This barge will be constructed at Gunderson&#146;s Portland, Oregon building facilities and will be built to comply with MARPOL Annex VI Regulation 13 Tier III standards regarding nitrogen oxide

be registered under the U.S. Flag with a coastwise endorsement, allowing it to be employed in Jones Act trades. The barge will be constructed to pair with existing tugs within OSG&#146;s current fleet for operation as an articulated tug barge or ATB
unit. The agreement with Gunderson Marine also provides OSG with an option to construct a second sister barge which would have a scheduled delivery date during the 4th quarter of calendar 2020.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </P>

Gunderson Marine is the only shipyard in the western United States with successful experience in building ATBs of the type we will deliver to OSG.&#148;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;Gunderson is a great place to work and our agreement with OSG fortifies a strong baseload of business over the next few years,&#148;
