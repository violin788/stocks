Rights Agreement) of any common stock of the Company or any securities convertible into common
stock of the Company by reason of acting as an initial purchaser in a private offering
contemplating resales under Rule&nbsp;144A under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended. The foregoing
description of the Fourth Amendment is qualified by the full text of the Fourth Amendment, a copy
of which is attached hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;4.1 and incorporated by reference herein.
