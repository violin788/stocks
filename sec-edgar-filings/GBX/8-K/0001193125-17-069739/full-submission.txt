<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>(b) Departure of Certain Officers. </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On
March&nbsp;2, 2017 (the &#147;Effective Date&#148;), The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) entered into a Separation and Consulting Agreement with Victoria McManus, the Company&#146;s Executive Vice President and Chief Strategic
Officer. Pursuant to the Separation and Consulting Agreement, Ms.&nbsp;McManus resigned as an employee of the Company on the Effective Date and will transition to a consulting role which shall continue until terminated by either party upon thirty
days written notice (the &#147;Consulting Term&#148;). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>(e) Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On March&nbsp;1, 2017, the Company&#146;s Compensation Committee approved the terms of a Separation and Consulting Agreement with
Ms.&nbsp;McManus pursuant to which Ms.&nbsp;McManus will provide consulting services to the Company during the Consulting Term to work on special projects as assigned by the Company&#146;s Chairman and Chief Executive Officer. Pursuant to the terms
of the Separation and Consulting Agreement (i)&nbsp;Ms.&nbsp;McManus will continue to be paid her current base salary during the Consulting Term; (ii)&nbsp;consulting service will count as employment service with respect to the requirement for
Ms.&nbsp;McManus to repay her hiring bonus received in fiscal 2016 if not employed through October 2017; and (iii)&nbsp;all outstanding RSUs held by Ms.&nbsp;McManus on the Effective Date will continue to vest according to their terms during the
Consulting Term, subject to forfeiture in accordance with the terms of the applicable RSU agreement upon termination of the Consulting Term, unless otherwise agreed in writing at the time of termination of the Consulting Term and approved by the
Company&#146;s Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors; provided, that, with respect to the 5,000 time-based vesting RSUs scheduled to vest in March 2017 and the 6,067 time-based vesting RSUs scheduled to vest in October 2017 (together, the
&#147;<U>2017 RSUs</U>&#148;), in the event the Company terminates the Consulting Term prior to vesting for any reason other than &#147;Cause&#148; (as defined below), the vesting of the 2017 RSUs shall fully accelerate. &#147;Cause&#148; shall
mean: the conviction of Ms.&nbsp;McManus (including a plea of nolo contendere) of a felony or gross misdemeanor under federal or state law which is materially and demonstrably injurious to the Company or which impairs Ms.&nbsp;McManus&#146;s ability
to perform substantially her duties for the Company. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">During the Consulting Term, Ms.&nbsp;McManus will continue to receive certain
benefits she received as an employee of the Company, including medical, dental and vision benefits. A <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-solicitation</FONT> period will be in effect through the end of the Consulting Term and for a period of two
years after the end of the Consulting Term. </P>

<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>SEPARATION AND CONSULTING AGREEMENT </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">This Separation and Consulting Agreement (&#147;Agreement&#148;) is made effective as of March&nbsp;2, 2017 (&#147;Effective Date&#148;) by
and between The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (The &#147;Company&#148;) and Victoria McManus (&#147;McManus&#148;). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>RECITAL </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Company and McManus desire to enter into this Agreement, setting forth the terms and conditions relating to McManus&#146; transition from
her current position with the Company and her engagement as a consultant to the Company following the cessation of her employment with the Company. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>AGREEMENT </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">NOW,
THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements hereinafter set forth, the Company and McManus agree as follows: </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>ARTICLE 1 </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Employment
and Separation </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">1.1&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<U>Employment Status</U>. As of the Effective Date, McManus shall resign from her current
position as Executive Vice President and Chief Strategic Officer of the Company and shall also resign any other roles as an officer and any other position of any and all subsidiaries of the Company. As of the Effective Date, McManus will cease to be
an employee of the Company. Except as provided in Section&nbsp;4.1, this Agreement amends, supersedes and replaces in all respects that certain Offer Letter between McManus and the Company, dated as of October&nbsp;12, 2015 (the &#147;McManus Offer
Letter&#148;), and the McManus Offer Letter is of no further force or effect. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">1.2&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<U>Retention of Hiring
Bonus</U>. McManus received a <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">one-time</FONT> hiring bonus in 2015. The Company and McManus agree that for purposes of conditions related to such hiring bonus that termination of employment does not cause a required
