<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Fifth sequential quarterly increase in orders </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Orders across a broad range of railcar types </I></P>

<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Lake Oswego, Oregon, August</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;26, 2021 &#150;</B>The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (NYSE: GBX) (&#147;Greenbrier&#148;), a
leading international supplier of equipment and services to global freight transportation markets, today announced new orders totaling 5,500 units valued at more than $530&nbsp;million received thus far during its fourth fiscal quarter that began on
June&nbsp;1, 2021. This increases Greenbrier&#146;s backlog and provides further visibility entering fiscal 2022. Orders announced today are from a diverse mix of customers and comprise a broad range of railcar types, including intermodal, boxcars,
tanks, covered hoppers and gondolas. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">William A. Furman, Chairman and CEO said, &#147;Greenbrier continues to see momentum in our markets
as we navigate the economic recovery and COVID variants. Our commercial and leasing teams have performed exceptionally in this environment. Greenbrier&#146;s proven ability to adjust production capacity in response to growing demand positions us to
actively secure new orders, strengthening our backlog. We expect this order trend to continue into fiscal 2022.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Certain orders
referenced in this release are subject to customary documentation and completion of terms. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>About Greenbrier </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Greenbrier, headquartered in Lake Oswego, Oregon, is a leading international supplier of equipment and services to global freight
transportation markets. Through its wholly-owned subsidiaries and joint ventures, Greenbrier designs, builds and markets freight railcars and marine barges in North&nbsp;America, Europe and Brazil. We are a leading provider of freight railcar wheel
services, parts, repair, refurbishment and retrofitting services in North America through our wheels, repair&nbsp;&amp; parts business unit. Greenbrier manages 445,000 railcars and offers railcar management, regulatory compliance services and
leasing services to railroads and other railcars owners in North America. GBX Leasing (GBXL) is a special purpose subsidiary that owns and manages a portfolio of leased railcars that originate primarily from Greenbrier&#146;s manufacturing
operations. Together, GBXL and Greenbrier own a lease fleet of 8,700 railcars. Learn more about Greenbrier at www.gbrx.com. </P>
