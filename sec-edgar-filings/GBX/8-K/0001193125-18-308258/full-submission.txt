</I></B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>~ Joint venture to establish a new multi-modal business centered on rail ~ </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>~ Objectives include improved livability through jobs and enhanced freight movement ~ </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>~ Supports objectives of Kingdom&#146;s Vision 2030 plan ~ </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Lake Oswego, OR, October</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;26, 2018</B> &#151; The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (NYSE:GBX) and Saudi Railway Company
(&#147;SAR&#148;) announced today that they have signed an agreement under which the parties will invest and generate investments totaling 1&nbsp;billion Saudi riyals (USD $270 million) in the Saudi rail industry. Greenbrier and SAR intend to
establish a joint venture company (&#147;JV&#148;) in Saudi Arabia to execute railway projects and supply railcars for profitable growth of the Saudi freight rail market. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Together through the JV, Greenbrier and SAR will establish a new multi-modal business centered on creating and maximizing existing and new
rail routes for freight movement throughout the Kingdom and, ultimately, the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) region. The JV will invest in assets and infrastructure required to expand profitable rail service offerings to the Saudi market. Based on
achieving identified milestones, Greenbrier will provide the JV up to $100&nbsp;million USD (370&nbsp;million Saudi riyals) in new railcars, lift equipment and other terminal investment necessary to place railcars in revenue service, and will
operate intermodal and other freight terminals. SAR will provide the JV locomotives, rail access and service schedules to facilitate line haul services. Using its investment syndication model, Greenbrier will facilitate raising an additional
$170&nbsp;million USD (630&nbsp;million Saudi riyals) in collaboration with SAR and international public and private investment communities. The JV will operate similar to the model of TTX in North America on car supply, and can expand to serve GCC
nations. Greenbrier will have a first right to manufacture and provide railcars for the JV&#146;s railcar pool and establish a Saudi-based manufacturing/assembly presence. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Pooling brings several benefits to SAR including technology and knowledge transfer, advanced railcar designs and efficient network service
design. The JV will promote supply chain efficiencies by increasing local content, reducing the capital burden on SAR, lowering SAR&#146;s operating costs and improving the railroad&#146;s competitiveness with highway transport. Freight traffic in
Saudi Arabia is predominantly transported by truck or by pipeline. The JV will help SAR meet its customers&#146; needs by providing well-maintained railcars in an efficient, network system for general freight traffic. </P>
