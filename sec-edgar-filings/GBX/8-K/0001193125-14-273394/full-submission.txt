<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>~ Largest independent railcar repair network of 38 locations now open for business ~ </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>~ Includes 14 certified tank car shops to meet surging demand for recertification, retrofit and repair ~ </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Lake Oswego, Oregon and Pittsburg, Kansas, July&nbsp;21, 2014</B> &#150; The&nbsp;Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (NYSE:GBX) and Watco Companies
LLC announced today that they have completed formation of their 50/50 railcar repair joint venture creating the largest independent railcar repair shop network in North America. The new joint venture, called GBW Railcar Services, LLC (GBW), owns and
operates the combined network of 38 railcar repair, refurbishment and maintenance shops of Greenbrier and Watco. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">GBW&#146;s network of
facilities located across North America features 14 tank car repair shops certified by the Association of American Railroads as required by federal regulations. GBW is unique in its ability to service almost the entire range of railcar types in
North America. The scale of GBW&#146;s shop network combined with the breadth of its service offerings provides a Customer reach to GBW that is unrivaled by any independent railcar repair shop in North America. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;GBW will be ready to deliver unparalleled quality, value and service to our Customers,&#148; said Watco CEO Rick Webb. &#147;We have
been investing in our shops, and we will continue to invest in the GBW shops to match our Customers&#146; expectations.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">GBW creates
premier capacity to service, recertify and retrofit North America&#146;s tank car fleet and offers a range of best in class repair and maintenance services for other general freight cars. For example, current requirements for federally-mandated tank
car recertification are running at a rate of approximately 22,000 tank cars per year. Recertification is expected to increase by 25% from current </P>
