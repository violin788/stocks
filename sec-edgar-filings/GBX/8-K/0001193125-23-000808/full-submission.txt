</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Greenbrier acquires 100% interest in GBX Leasing joint venture </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Acquires interest of The Longwood Group in portfolio of leased railcars </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>Stable lease fleet cash flows balance exposure to fluctuations in new railcar market </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Lake Oswego, Oregon, January</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;3, 2023 &#150;</B> The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (NYSE: GBX) (&#147;Greenbrier&#148;), a
leading international supplier of equipment and services to global freight transportation markets, today announced it acquired a 100% interest in GBX Leasing (&#147;GBXL&#148;), its railcar leasing joint venture with The Longwood Group
(&#147;Longwood&#148;). This furthers Greenbrier&#146;s strategy to grow its lease fleet and expand its services offering. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Greenbrier
acquired Longwood&#146;s minority interest in GBXL&#146;s portfolio of leased railcars, manufactured primarily by Greenbrier. GBXL now operates as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Greenbrier. Since its inception in early 2021, GBXL has delivered stable
and <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">tax-advantaged</FONT> cash flows designed in part to complement the more cyclical revenues derived from Greenbrier&#146;s new railcar manufacturing. In turn, GBXL&#146;s leased railcar portfolio led to its
first-ever issuance of railcar asset-backed securities (&#147;ABS&#148;) in February 2022. Driven by high investor interest, the ABS notes were fully subscribed at issuance at a blended interest rate of 2.9%. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Greenbrier CEO and President Lorie Tekorius stated, &#147;Greenbrier has enjoyed a productive and beneficial relationship with Longwood in the
development of our leasing business. I would like to thank Longwood and its CEO, D. Stephen Menzies, for offering strategic investment guidance and market assistance during GBXL&#146;s nearly two years of operations. The formation of GBXL has
plainly and demonstrably expanded Greenbrier&#146;s value proposition for its customers and shareholders.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>About Greenbrier </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Greenbrier, headquartered in Lake Oswego, Oregon, is a leading international supplier of equipment and services to global freight transportation markets.
Through its wholly-owned subsidiaries and joint ventures, Greenbrier designs, builds and markets freight railcars and marine barges in North America, Europe and Brazil. We are a leading provider of freight railcar wheel services, parts, maintenance
and retrofitting services in North America through our maintenance services business unit. Greenbrier manages 408,000 railcars and offers railcar management, regulatory compliance services and leasing services to railroads and other railcars owners
in North America. GBX Leasing (GBXL) is a special purpose subsidiary that owns and manages a portfolio of leased railcars that originate primarily from Greenbrier&#146;s manufacturing operations. GBXL and Greenbrier own a lease fleet of
approximately 12,200 railcars. Learn more about Greenbrier at <U>www.gbrx.com</U>. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>-More-</I> </P>
