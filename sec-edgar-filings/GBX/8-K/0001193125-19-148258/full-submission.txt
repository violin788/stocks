<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"> <P STYLE=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman; " ALIGN="left"><B>Regulation FD Disclosure </B></P></TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (&#147;Greenbrier&#148;) recently experienced unofficial labor disruptions at the Romanian manufacturing operations of its
subsidiary Greenbrier-Astra Rail, B.V. Workers have walked out at the manufacturing facility located in Arad, Romania protesting wage levels and working conditions. The &#147;wildcat strike&#148; affected approximately 1,200 workers, and is not
sanctioned by the local union. Negotiations with the union and other parties are ongoing, and after 3 days, most workers have returned to work. Greenbrier does not currently have an estimate of the potential impact on financial results or ongoing
operations. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The information under this Item 7.01 shall not be deemed &#147;filed&#148; for the purposes of Section&nbsp;18 of the Securities Exchange Act
of 1934, as amended (the &#147;Exchange Act&#148;), or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section. Such information shall not be incorporated by reference into any registration statement or other document filed under the Securities Act or
the Exchange Act, except as expressly set forth by specific reference in such a filing. </P>
