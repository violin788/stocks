and all outstanding awards pursuant to the Company&#146;s equity compensation arrangements, amounts to approximately 525,000 shares. Mr.&nbsp;Furman has advised the Company that he intends to sell 250,000 shares in the current stock trading window,
or pursuant to a Rule <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">10b5-1</FONT> trading plan, or a combination thereof. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The information under this Item 7.01 shall
