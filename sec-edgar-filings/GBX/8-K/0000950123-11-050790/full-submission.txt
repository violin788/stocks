<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">As
previously announced, in accordance with the Indenture dated as of May&nbsp;11, 2005 among The
Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (&#147;Greenbrier&#148;), the guarantors party thereto and U.S. Bank National
Association, as trustee (the &#147;Trustee&#148;) (as amended or supplemented, the &#147;Notes Indenture&#148;),
on April&nbsp;13, 2011 Greenbrier issued a notice to redeem on May&nbsp;16, 2011 (the &#147;Redemption Date&#148;) any
and all of its 8 3/8% Senior Notes due 2015 (the &#147;Notes&#148;)
that then remained outstanding for a price
of 102.792% of the principal amount of such remaining Notes, plus accrued and unpaid interest to,
but not including, the Redemption Date (the &#147;Redemption Payment&#148;). In accordance with Section
11.01 of the Notes Indenture, on May&nbsp;16, 2011, Greenbrier irrevocably deposited the Redemption
Payment for all remaining outstanding Notes with the Trustee and took all other actions necessary
under the Notes Indenture to satisfy and discharge the Notes and the Notes Indenture. Pursuant to
the terms of the Notes Indenture, upon Greenbrier&#146;s irrevocable deposit of the Redemption Payment
with the Trustee and the taking of such actions, the Notes and the Notes Indenture are deemed to
have been satisfied in full and discharged and the Notes Indenture ceases to be of any further
effect.</DIV>
