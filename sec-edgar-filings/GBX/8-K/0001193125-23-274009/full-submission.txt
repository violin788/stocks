<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>First-ever Inclusion, Diversity, Equity, Access and Leadership (IDEAL) summit convened </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>ISO 14001 certifications achieved at two additional facilities&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Lake Oswego, Oregon, November</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;9, 2023</B> &#150; The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (NYSE:GBX) (&#147;Greenbrier&#148;)
announced the release of its 2023 Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) report, <I>On Track Together</I>. Greenbrier continues to progress on its ambitious ESG agenda. The 2023 report reviews Greenbrier&#146;s 2021 materiality assessment to
address stakeholders&#146; evolving values and priorities. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Greenbrier&#146;s alignment with the Task Force on Climate-related Financial
Disclosures (TCFD) framework continues with the company&#146;s internal analysis to identify gaps in its approach. <I>On Track Together</I> also describes the state of the company&#146;s assessment of climate-related physical and transition risks.
</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The report highlights new targets and key achievements from fiscal 2023 in accordance with its five core ESG pillars:
(1)&nbsp;Safety&nbsp;&amp; Quality, (2)&nbsp;People, (3) Environmental Sustainability, (4)&nbsp;Governance&nbsp;&amp; Ethics, and (5)&nbsp;Communities. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Safety&nbsp;&amp; Quality </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In fiscal
2023, one railcar Manufacturing facility and eight Maintenance Services locations achieved at least one year without a recordable incident, another year of solid safety results. In addition, the company introduced a continuous improvement safety
initiative, Dynamic Hand Safety. This program prioritizes the prevention of hand injuries, Greenbrier&#146;s most prevalent safety incident. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>People
</B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Greenbrier reiterated its dedication to its workforce, including advancing its Inclusion, Diversity, Equity, Access and Leadership
(IDEAL) commitment. Two new Employee Resource Groups were introduced, for a total of eight. Greenbrier&#146;s first-ever IDEAL Summit brought leaders across the company together to identify successes, define improvement areas and set future
objectives for DEI at the company. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Environment </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Greenbrier has established a greenhouse gas reduction goal to reduce Scope 2 GHG emissions intensity by 20% per unit of output by 2027, based
on its 2022 baseline. In fiscal 2023, a pilot solar power project began at Greenbrier Tlaxcala in Mexico. The production from the solar panel installation has a projected average energy savings of just over 67,000
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">kW-hours</FONT> per month. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>-More- </I></P>
