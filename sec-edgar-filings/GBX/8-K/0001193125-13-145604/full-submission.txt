STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>(e)</B> <B>Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><U>Adoption of Umbrella
Performance-Based Plan For Executive Officers </U></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">On April&nbsp;2, 2013 the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors
of The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) approved the adoption of The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. Umbrella Performance-Based Plan for Executive Officers (the &#147;Umbrella Plan&#148;). The purpose of the Umbrella Plan is to enable
the Company to grant performance-based incentive awards to executive officers that qualify as &#147;performance-based compensation&#148; under Internal Revenue Code &#167;162(m) and applicable regulations (&#147;&#167;162(m)&#148;), including both
short-term cash incentive awards made under the Company&#146;s annual bonus program for executive officers, and long-term equity-based incentive awards made under the Company&#146;s 2010 Amended and Restated Stock Incentive Plan. The Umbrella Plan
sets forth the business criteria that the Compensation Committee may use for performance-based awards, and other provisions intended to enable performance-based awards to qualify as such under &#167;162(m). The Company intends to submit the Umbrella
Plan to shareholders for approval at the next annual meeting of shareholders, as required in order for performance-based incentive awards to qualify as &#147;performance-based compensation&#148; under &#167;162(m). Awards that qualify as
&#147;performance-based compensation&#148; are not subject to the $1 million federal income tax deduction limit that otherwise applies under &#167;162(m) to compensation paid to the Company&#146;s chief executive officer and three other most highly
compensated executive officers (other than its chief financial officer). </FONT></P>
