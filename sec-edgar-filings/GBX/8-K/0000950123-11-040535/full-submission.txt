<DIV align="center" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><B>Greenbrier announces expiration and final results of tender offer for 8 3/8% Senior Notes due 2015</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt; text-indent: 4%"><B>Lake Oswego, Oregon, April&nbsp;27, 2011 </B>&#151; The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. &#091;NYSE:GBX&#093; announced
today that its previously announced cash tender offer and consent solicitation (the &#147;Tender Offer&#148;)
for any and all of its outstanding 8 3/8% Senior Notes due 2015 (the &#147;Notes&#148;) expired today at 8:00
a.m., New York City time (the &#147;Expiration Date&#148;).
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt; text-indent: 4%">On April&nbsp;13, 2011 (the &#147;Early Settlement Date&#148;), Greenbrier announced that it had purchased
$90,599,000, or approximately 38.55%, of the aggregate principal amount of the Notes, representing
all of the Notes that were validly tendered and not validly withdrawn pursuant to the Tender Offer
at or prior to 5:00 p.m., New York City time, on April&nbsp;12, 2011 (the &#147;Consent Payment Deadline&#148;).
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt; text-indent: 4%">An additional $203,000 principal amount of the Notes was validly tendered after the Consent
Payment Deadline and on or prior to the Expiration Date pursuant to the Tender Offer, resulting in
a total of $90,802,000 principal amount of Notes tendered pursuant to the Tender Offer, or
approximately 38.64% of the aggregate principal amount of the Notes outstanding at the time the
Tender Offer was commenced. Greenbrier accepted for purchase and payment all such additional Notes
today for a price of $1,021.67 per $1,000 principal amount of Notes, plus any accrued and unpaid
interest up to, but not including, April&nbsp;27, 2011.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt; text-indent: 4%">As previously announced, on the Early Settlement Date, Greenbrier issued, pursuant to the
indenture governing the Notes, a notice to redeem on May&nbsp;16, 2011 (the &#147;Redemption Date&#148;) any and
all of the Notes that then remain outstanding. The redemption price for the redeemed Notes will be
102.792% of the principal amount of such remaining Notes, plus accrued and unpaid interest to, but
not including, the Redemption Date.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt; text-indent: 4%">BofA Merrill Lynch acted as Dealer Manager and Solicitation Agent for the Tender Offer. D. F.
King &#038; Co., Inc. acted as the Information Agent and Depositary for the Tender Offer.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt; text-indent: 4%">This press release shall not constitute an offer to purchase, a solicitation of an offer to
sell, or a solicitation of tenders or consents with respect to, any Notes. No offer, solicitation
or purchase will be made in any jurisdiction in which such an offer, solicitation or purchase would
be unlawful.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt"><B>About Greenbrier Companies</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 10pt; text-indent: 4%">Greenbrier, headquartered in Lake Oswego, Oregon, is a leading supplier of transportation
equipment and services to the railroad industry. Greenbrier builds new railroad freight cars in its
three manufacturing facilities in the U.S. and Mexico and marine barges at its U.S. facility. It
also repairs and refurbishes freight cars and provides wheels and railcar parts at 37 locations
across North America. Greenbrier builds new railroad freight cars and refurbishes freight cars for
the European market through both its operations in Poland and various subcontractor facilities
throughout Europe.
</DIV>
