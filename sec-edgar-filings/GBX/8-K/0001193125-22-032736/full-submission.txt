<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Lake Oswego, Oregon, February</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;9, 2022 &#150;</B>The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (NYSE: GBX) (&#147;Greenbrier&#148;), a
leading international supplier of equipment and services to global freight transportation markets, today announced the completion of its first offering of railcar asset-backed securities (&#147;ABS&#148;) and long-term financing for GBX Leasing
(&#147;GBXL&#148;), a joint venture between Greenbrier and The Longwood Group. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">GBXL&#146;s wholly owned subsidiary GBX Leasing <FONT

with a blended interest rate of 2.9%. The Notes have a weighted average life of approximately six years. The transaction is secured by nearly 4,500 railcars with a fair market value of $413&nbsp;million and their associated operating leases. The
railcar securitization will be consolidated on Greenbrier&#146;s balance sheet but is <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-recourse</FONT> to Greenbrier. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Greenbrier CEO and Chairman William A. Furman stated, &#147;The strong response from investors produced excellent terms for GBXL&#146;s first
asset-backed securitization. The quality of this transaction demonstrates the respect and confidence engendered by Greenbrier&#146;s leasing capabilities and the current strength of railcar securitizations in the debt market. All debt associated
with GBXL&#146;s leasing warehouse will be extinguished with the proceeds of this financing. This positions GBXL for robust fleet growth through 2022, creating scale for our leasing strategy going forward. Stable cash flows from leasing bolster
Greenbrier&#146;s integrated business model, allowing us to more fully capitalize on growing momentum in the North American freight railcar market.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>About Greenbrier </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Greenbrier,
headquartered in Lake Oswego, Oregon, is a leading international supplier of equipment and services to global freight transportation markets. Through its wholly owned subsidiaries and joint </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">ventures, Greenbrier designs, builds and markets freight railcars and marine barges in North America, Europe and Brazil. We are a leading
