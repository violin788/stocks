<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>(e) Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><U>2016 Bonus Program </U></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The 2016 Executive
Officer Bonus Program (the &#147;Bonus Program&#148;) of The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;), originally adopted on October&nbsp;29, 2015, was amended on November&nbsp;10, 2015. The Bonus Program provides that the Company&#146;s
executive officers, including its named executive officers, may earn bonuses based on Company and/or business unit financial performance during fiscal 2016. No bonuses will be paid unless the Company achieves at least the threshold level of
performance against its corporate-level adjusted EBITDA goal. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">One factor of the Bonus Program was substituted as it applies to named
executive officer William Glenn, Senior Vice President and Chief Commercial Officer. As substituted, 20% of Mr.&nbsp;Glenn&#146;s bonus opportunity is now based on EBIT for Greenbrier Leasing Company LLC (&#147;GLC&#148;) rather than return on
invested capital for GLC. </P>
