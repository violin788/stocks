<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">(e)&nbsp;In March&nbsp;2009, the Company implemented certain wage reductions in response to economic
conditions then-existing. These wage reductions range from 7.5% to 50% of annual base compensation
for its executive officers, depending on their annual base compensation range. Effective December
1, 2010, the Company will restore 50% of the annual base compensation reductions for its executive
officers, other than the Chief Executive Officer, that were implemented March&nbsp;1, 2009.</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Also effective December&nbsp;1, 2010, the Company will restore 3.5% of the annual base compensation
for William A. Furman, President and Chief Executive Officer of the Company, which is comparable to
the wage restoration percentage amount being implemented for the Company&#146;s hourly employees.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Executives who have formal employment contracts with the Company are being requested to amend
such agreements to reflect the above adjustments.
