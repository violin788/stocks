<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Lake Oswego, Oregon, April&nbsp;6, 2015</B> &#150; The Greenbrier Companies (NYSE: GBX) announced today the election of Kelly M. Williams
to the Company&#146;s Board of Directors. The election of Ms.&nbsp;Williams increases Greenbrier&#146;s Board of Directors to nine members, seven of which are independent. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Ms.&nbsp;Williams&#146; credentials include over 15 years&#146; experience in the private equity industry. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Ms.&nbsp;Williams is President of GCM Grosvenor Private Markets, and a member of its Investment Committee and Management Committee. GCM is one
of the world&#146;s leading managers of private markets fund investment and co-investment programs, with over $22 billion in assets under management. Prior to joining GCM Grosvenor, Ms.&nbsp;Williams was a Managing Director and the Group Head of the
Customized Fund Investment Group of Credit Suisse&#146;s private equity group. Prior to joining Credit Suisse, she was an executive director of Prudential&#146;s private equity group, where she co-founded the Customized Fund Investment Group in
1999. Prior to joining Prudential, Ms.&nbsp;Williams was an Associate with Milbank, Tweed, Hadley&nbsp;&amp; McCloy LLP, where she specialized in global project finance. She graduated magna cum laude from Union College in 1986 with a Bachelor of
Arts degree in Political Science and Mathematics and received her Juris Doctor from New York University School of Law in 1989. She is a member of the New York bar and is active in many professional and charitable organizations. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;We are delighted to have Kelly Williams join Greenbrier&#146;s Board,&#148; said William A. Furman, chairman and chief executive officer
of Greenbrier. &#147;Kelly is an experienced and highly successful leader in the private equity industry, with significant involvement spanning many businesses and industries. She will give Greenbrier insight into new opportunities for expansion, as
well as areas of focus for investors. Her expertise and experience, with particular emphasis on investments and access to capital, will be extremely helpful to the Company.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Greenbrier, (<U>www.gbrx.com</U>), headquartered in Lake Oswego, Oregon, is a leading supplier of transportation equipment and services to the
railroad industry. We build new railroad freight cars in our 4 manufacturing facilities in the U.S. and Mexico and marine barges at our U.S. manufacturing facility. Greenbrier also sells reconditioned wheel sets and provides wheel services at 10
