except as described below. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Half of each RSU award is subject to time-based vesting. Time-based RSUs will vest in equal annual
installments over a three-year period on the first, second and third anniversaries of the date of the grant, provided the recipient remains employed with the Company through the vesting date. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Half of each RSU award is subject to performance-based vesting. Performance-based RSUs will vest, in whole or in part, contingent upon
achievement of the Company&#146;s goals relating to cumulative adjusted EBITDA and adjusted return on equity (&#147;ROE&#148;), measured during the period March&nbsp;1, 2014 &#150; August&nbsp;31, 2016 (the &#147;Measurement Period&#148;). The
performance goals are equally weighted, such that vesting of 50% of the performance-based RSUs is dependent upon performance against the cumulative adjusted EBITDA goal and vesting of 50% of the performance-based RSUs is dependent upon performance
against the adjusted ROE goal. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Vesting of RSUs related to each performance goal will be considered independently. Threshold, target and
stretch performance levels are established for the cumulative adjusted EBITDA and adjusted ROE goals based on the Company&#146;s financial plan. For the cumulative adjusted EBITDA goal, threshold performance is set at 65% of plan EBITDA; target
performance is set at 85% of plan EBITDA; and stretch performance is set at 105% of plan EBITDA. The threshold performance level for adjusted ROE is 11.6%; target performance is 14.1%; and stretch performance is 19.0%. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">If the Company achieves the threshold level of performance on a goal, then 50% of the performance-based RSUs tied to that goal will vest. If
the Company achieves the target level of performance on a goal, 100% of the performance-based RSUs tied to that goal will vest. If the Company achieves a stretch goal, then the recipient will be entitled to receive two shares of Common Stock for
each performance-based RSU tied to that goal, rather than one share. RSU </P>

Recipient one share of Common Stock for each vested RSU, upon the later of (i)&nbsp;vesting in accordance with this Agreement, or (ii)&nbsp;the distribution date or dates elected by the Recipient, if the Recipient elects to defer receipt of the

the Performance-Based RSUs shall vest based upon achievement of Adjusted EBITDA goals (the &#147;<B>Adjusted EBITDA Performance RSUs</B>&#148;), and up to 50% of the Performance-Based RSUs shall vest based upon achievement of Return on Equity
(&#147;<B>ROE</B>&#148;) goals (the &#147;<B>ROE Performance RSUs</B>&#148;), during the Measurement Period, as set forth in subsections 2(c)(i) and (ii), below: </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:4%; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B></B>(i) <B>Adjusted EBITDA Performance RSUs. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:8%; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">(1) 100% of the Adjusted EBITDA Performance RSUs (50% of the total number of Performance-Based RSUs) will vest on the Vesting
Date if the Company&#146;s Adjusted EBITDA equals the Adjusted EBITDA Target Level. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:8%; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">(2) 50% of the Adjusted EBITDA
Performance RSUs (25% of the total number of Performance-Based RSUs) will vest on the Vesting Date if the Company&#146;s Adjusted EBITDA equals the Adjusted EBITDA Threshold Level. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:8%; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">(3) If the Company&#146;s Adjusted EBITDA is greater than the Threshold Level but less than the Target Level, vesting of the
Adjusted EBITDA Performance RSUs will be interpolated between 50% and 100%. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:8%; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">(4) If the Company&#146;s Adjusted EBITDA is
less than the Threshold Level, none of the Adjusted EBITDA Performance RSUs will vest. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:4%; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B></B>(ii) <B>ROE Performance

Vesting Date if the Company achieves its ROE Target Level. </P>

RSUs will be settled for 200% of the number of shares underlying the Adjusted EBITDA Performance RSUs. If the Company&#146;s Adjusted EBITDA during the Measurement Period exceeds the Adjusted EBITDA Target Level but is below the Adjusted EBITDA
Stretch Level, the number of shares for which the Adjusted EBITDA Performance RSUs will be settled will be interpolated between 100% and 200% of the number of shares underlying the Adjusted EBITDA Performance RSUs at the Target level, based on the
