</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Adrian Downes Named Greenbrier Chief Financial Officer </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>~ Permanent appointment follows nine-month service as Acting CFO ~ </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B><I>~ <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">30-year</FONT> accounting and finance veteran will serve as Principal Financial Officer ~ </I></B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Lake Oswego, Oregon, June</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;3, 2019</B> &#150;&nbsp;The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (NYSE: GBX) today announced that its
Senior Vice President and Acting Chief Financial Officer, Adrian Downes, has been appointed Chief Financial Officer. In addition to continuing his duties managing Greenbrier&#146;s accounting, tax, internal audit and IT functions, Downes will also
serve as the Company&#146;s Principal Financial and Accounting Officer. He continues to report to Lorie Tekorius, Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Downes has more than 30 years of accounting and finance experience in a variety of industries, both with publicly held and privately held
companies. Before joining Greenbrier in 2013 as its Chief Accounting Officer, he was Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer for Knowledge Universe, a private-equity owned early childhood education provider. Downes previously held
various senior financial executive positions of progressively increasing responsibility for Fortune 200 publicly held organizations including SUPERVALU, Albertson&#146;s, Gap, Inc. and Pacific Telesis. He began his career in public accounting at
PricewaterhouseCoopers, initially based at their Dublin, Ireland office before transferring to San Francisco. He obtained his Certified Public Accountant certification in California. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">William A. Furman, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, stated, &#147;Adrian&#146;s successful tenure for the past nine months as Acting CFO,
in addition to other assignments over the past six years requiring judgment and expertise, prepares him well to serve as Greenbrier&#146;s next CFO. Adrian&#146;s promotion to CFO is the latest in a succession of executive advancements in recent
years, demonstrating the depth of Greenbrier&#146;s talent base. Adrian&#146;s appointment as CFO allows the other members of our executive team to better focus their energies on executing Greenbrier&#146;s strategic objectives. Greenbrier&#146;s
strategy is to develop a talent pipeline and grow at scale, while also strengthening our North American market base and expanding internationally.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Lorie Tekorius, Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer, said, &#147;I am pleased Adrian has accepted this position adding to his
leadership within our senior management team. For the past six years, he has driven continuous improvement in Greenbrier&#146;s international accounting, information technology, internal audit and tax planning functions. We look forward to the
additional contributions Adrian will make as CFO.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>- More - </I></P>
