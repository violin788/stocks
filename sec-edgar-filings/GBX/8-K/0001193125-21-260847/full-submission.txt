<td align="left" style="vertical-align:top"> <p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement. </p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On August&#160;27, 2021 (the &#8220;Effective Date&#8221;), The Greenbrier Companies, Inc. (&#8220;Greenbrier&#8221;) entered into a Second Amendment to Fourth Amended and Restated Credit Agreement (the &#8220;Second Amendment&#8221;), with Bank of America, N.A. (&#8220;BofA&#8221;), as Administrative Agent (the &#8220;Facility Agent&#8221;), the Guarantors (as defined therein) party thereto, and the Lenders (as defined therein) party thereto, which amends and restates that certain Fourth Amended and Restated Credit Agreement, dated as of September&#160;26, 2018 (as amended by that certain First Amendment to Fourth Amended and Restated Credit Agreement, Guarantor Joinder and Amendment to Certain Collateral Documents, dated as of June&#160;3, 2019, the &#8220;Original Credit Facility&#8221;, as amended by the Second Amendment, the &#8220;Amended Credit Facility&#8221;), by and among Greenbrier, the Facility Agent, and the lending institutions party thereto from time to time. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In addition, on the Effective Date, Greenbrier Leasing Company LLC (&#8220;GLC&#8221;), a wholly-owned subsidiary of Greenbrier, entered into a First Amendment to Amended and Restated Credit Agreement (the &#8220;First Amendment&#8221;), with BofA, as Administrative Agent (the &#8220;Term Agent&#8221;), and the Lenders (as defined therein) party thereto, which amends and restates that certain Amended and Restated Credit Agreement, dated as of September&#160;26, 2018 (the &#8220;Original Term Facility&#8221;, as amended by the First Amendment, the &#8220;Amended Term Facility&#8221;) by and among GLC, the Term Agent, and the lending institutions party thereto from time to time. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Amended Credit Facility and the Amended Term Facility are described further below. </p> <p style="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><span style="text-decoration:underline">The Amended Credit Facility </span></p> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Amended Credit Facility continues to allow Greenbrier to borrow, on a revolving basis, up to $600.0&#160;million based on availability under a revised borrowing base formula. As of the Effective Date, approximately $160.0&#160;million was outstanding under the revolving credit facility. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Under the Amended Credit Facility, Greenbrier increased the term loans (&#8220;Greenbrier Term Loans&#8221;) incurred thereunder such that, as of the Effective Date, $291.89&#160;million was outstanding under the term credit facility.
