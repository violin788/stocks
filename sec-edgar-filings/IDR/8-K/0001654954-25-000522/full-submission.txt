(the &#8220;Company&#8221;) approved a one-time stock option grant pursuant to the Company&#8217;s 2023 Equity Incentive Compensation Plan (the &#8220;Plan&#8221;) to employees, executive officers and directors of the Company, to further incentivize individuals to enhance stockholder value over the long-term, align individual interests with that of the stockholders&#8217; interests, and to retain such employees, officers, and directors.</p><p style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: times new roman; MARGIN: 0px; TEXT-INDENT: 0.5in; text-align:justify;">&#160;</p><p style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-FAMILY: times new roman; MARGIN: 0px; TEXT-INDENT: 0.5in; text-align:justify;">The Committee and the Board approved granting a total of 425,000 options to employees, directors, and officers.
