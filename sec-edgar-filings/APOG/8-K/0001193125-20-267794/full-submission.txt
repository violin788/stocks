(the &#8220;Company&#8221;) took several proactive measures to manage the Company&#8217;s cost structure and cash flow in response to uncertainty created by the <span style="white-space:nowrap">COVID-19</span> pandemic, which included temporary salary reductions for the Company&#8217;s named executive officers. The temporary reductions included a base salary reduction of 25% for Joseph F. Puishys, Chief Executive Officer and President, and 20% for certain other named executive officers. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In light of current business conditions, (i)&#160;on October&#160;6, 2020, the Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors (the &#8220;Board&#8221;) approved restoration of base salaries for the Company&#8217;s named executive officers (other than Mr.&#160;Puishys), effective October&#160;25, 2020, and recommended that the Board restore Mr.&#160;Puishys base salary, and (ii)&#160;on October&#160;7, 2020, the Board approved restoration of Mr.&#160;Puishys base salary. </p> <p style="font-size:18pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&#160;</p>
