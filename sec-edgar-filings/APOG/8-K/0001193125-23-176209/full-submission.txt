<td style="width:11%;vertical-align:top" align="left"><span style="font-weight:bold">Item&#160;5.02</span></td>
<td align="left" style="vertical-align:top"> <p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangement of Certain Officers </p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold">(e) Compensatory Arrangement of Certain Officers </p> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold">Change in Control Agreement. </p> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On June&#160;22, 2023, Apogee Enterprises, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) entered into a <span style="white-space:nowrap"><span style="white-space:nowrap">change-in-control</span></span> agreement (the &#8220;Change in Control Agreement&#8221;) with Matthew J. Osberg, the Company&#8217;s Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Change in Control Agreement is a &#8220;double trigger&#8221; agreement. It provides that, in the event of a change in control of the Company, Mr.&#160;Osberg will have specific rights and receive specified benefits if Mr.&#160;Osberg&#8217;s employment is terminated without &#8220;cause&#8221; (as defined in the Change in Control Agreement) or Mr.&#160;Osberg voluntarily terminates his employment for &#8220;good reason&#8221; (as defined in the Change in Control Agreement) within two years after the change in control (the 10th business day following such employment termination date is referred to herein as the &#8220;Employment Termination Date&#8221;). In these circumstances, Mr.&#160;Osberg will receive a severance payment equal to two times his annual salary and target cash incentive bonus for the fiscal year (as calculated under the terms of the Change in Control Agreement). In addition, all options and restricted stock awards held by Mr.&#160;Osberg that have not vested by the Employment Termination Date will be immediately vested on such date. Following the Employment Termination Date, the Change in Control Agreement provides that, for a <span style="white-space:nowrap">24-month</span> period following a change in control, the Company will continue to provide medical and dental insurance coverage for Mr.&#160;Osberg and his dependents or will reimburse Mr.&#160;Osberg for the cost of obtaining substantially similar benefits.
