Jewell and Longman pursuant, and subject, to the terms of the Company&#8217;s 2019 Stock Incentive Plan and the customary forms of award agreements. </p> <p style="font-size:18pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&#160;</p>

<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Apogee Enterprises Announces Leadership Transition </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>MINNEAPOLIS, MN, October</B><B></B><B>&nbsp;18, 2023 &#150; Apogee Enterprises, Inc. (Nasdaq: APOG)</B> today announced a transition in its segment
leadership. Effective immediately, Brent C. Jewell will assume the role of President of Apogee&#146;s Architectural Glass Segment. Brent will succeed Nick C. Longman, who has concurrently been named President of Apogee&#146;s Architectural Framing
Systems Segment. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;This leadership transition comes two years into the execution of Apogee&#146;s new enterprise strategy, over which time the Glass
and Framing Systems Segments have achieved significant operational and financial improvements,&#148; said Ty R. Silberhorn, Chief Executive Officer. &#147;This transition will align Brent and Nick&#146;s leadership strengths with the business needs
of the two segments as we continue to advance our strategy. The move will also provide both leaders the opportunity to further their professional development, strengthening the company&#146;s talent bench.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr.&nbsp;Silberhorn continued, &#147;I would like to recognize Brent and Nick for the tremendous success that Framing Systems and Glass have achieved under
their leadership. This change will provide renewed focus and energy as we build on this record of success, driving continued profitable growth in the years ahead.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Brent C. Jewell </B>has served as President of Apogee&#146;s Architectural Framing Systems Segment since August 2019. He joined Apogee in 2018 as Senior
Vice President, Business Development and Strategy. In that role he led efforts to develop Apogee&#146;s business strategy and was responsible for implementing increased cross-business collaboration to drive
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">top-line</FONT> growth. Before joining Apogee, Brent spent seven years at Valspar, a global leader in the coatings industry, where he held senior level positions in their General Industrial Americas and North America
Wood Coatings divisions. Before Valspar, Brent held senior level positions in sales, marketing and general management at NewPage, a leading manufacturer of coated printing and specialty papers. </P>
