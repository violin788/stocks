<td align="left" style="vertical-align:top"> <p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangement of Certain Officers </p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On March&#160;2, 2021, Bernard P. Aldrich informed the Board that he has decided to retire from the Board of Directors (the &#8220;Board&#8221;) of Apogee&#160;Enterprises, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) and will not stand for <span style="white-space:nowrap">re-election</span> at the 2021 Annual Meeting of Shareholders (the &#8220;Annual Meeting&#8221;). Mr.&#160;Aldrich will continue to serve on the Board until the Annual Meeting. Mr.&#160;Aldrich&#8217;s retirement is not as a result of a disagreement with the Company relating to the Company&#8217;s operations, policies or practices. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">With the retirement of Mr.&#160;Aldrich (a member of the Board&#8217;s Class&#160;II, which Class&#160;will be standing for election at the 2021 Annual Meeting), the Board took the opportunity to realign its Board Classes to ensure that each Class&#160;would have an equal number of members, as contemplated by the Company&#8217;s Restated Articles of Incorporation. To facilitate the Class&#160;realignment, on March&#160;3, 2021, Christina M. Alvord resigned from the Board as a Class&#160;III director (term expiring in 2022), and was <span style="white-space:nowrap">re-elected</span> by the Board to serve as a Class&#160;II director effective as of March&#160;3, 2021. Ms.&#160;Alvord will continue to serve on the Audit Committee and Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee of the Board. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Ms.&#160;Alvord, 53, has served as a member of the Board since January 2020, when she was elected as a Class&#160;III director. </p>
