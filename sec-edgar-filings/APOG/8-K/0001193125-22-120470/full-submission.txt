In the event the executive officer&#8217;s employment is terminated after the Performance Period by reason of death, Disability or Retirement, the executive officer or the executive officer&#8217;s estate, as applicable, will be entitled to receive, if not yet paid, the Performance Award. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Performance Period will end on the date of the Change in Control, and the award will be adjusted by the Committee in its sole discretion.
