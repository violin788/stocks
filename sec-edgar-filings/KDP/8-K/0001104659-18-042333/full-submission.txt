<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Dr Pepper Snapple Group Sets Record Date for Special Dividend Contemplated by Keurig Transaction and Provides Estimated Earnings and Profits</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">PLANO, Texas, June&nbsp;26, 2018 &#151; Dr Pepper Snapple Group,&nbsp;Inc. (NYSE: DPS) (the &#147;Company&#148; or &#147;DPS&#148;) today announced that its Board of Directors has conditionally set July&nbsp;6, 2018 as the record date for the special dividend (the &#147;Special Dividend&#148;) of $103.75 per share on the Company&#146;s common stock contemplated by the Agreement and Plan of Merger among the Company, a special purpose merger subsidiary of the Company and Maple Parent Holdings Corp (&#147;Maple Parent&#148;), the indirect parent of Keurig Green Mountain,&nbsp;Inc.&#160; Under the terms of the agreement, Maple Parent will merge with the merger subsidiary and become a wholly owned subsidiary of the Company.&#160; </font>As previously announced, following the closing of the merger DPS will be renamed Keurig Dr Pepper Inc. and trade under the symbol &#147;KDP&#148; on the New York Stock Exchange.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Completion of the transaction remains subject to stockholder approvals that will be sought at our Annual Meeting scheduled for June&nbsp;29, 2018, and other customary closing conditions.&#160; Subject to the foregoing, the closing is currently scheduled for July&nbsp;9, 2018.&#160; It is anticipated that the Board will declare the Special Dividend, conditioned upon the completion of the transaction, to be payable in U.S. dollars at 12:01 A.M.&nbsp;EDT on July&nbsp;10, 2018 to stockholders of record on the July&nbsp;6, 2018 record date, the trading day immediately prior to the anticipated closing date.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">For U.S. federal income tax purposes, the Special Dividend is expected to be characterized as a distribution pursuant to Section&nbsp;301(a)&nbsp;of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986.&#160; Assuming this characterization applies, as a result, the Special Dividend will be considered a dividend for U.S. federal income tax purposes to the extent of the accumulated earnings and profits (&#147;E&amp;P&#148;) of DPS and Maple Parent through the end of the 2018 taxable year.&#160; The portion of the Special Dividend that is not characterized as a dividend for tax purposes will be applied against and reduce tax basis, with any excess treated as gain from the sale or exchange of property.&nbsp; Based on DPS&#146;s and Maple Parent&#146;s estimate of their respective E&amp;P, it is currently estimated that the applicable E&amp;P will be $29.50 per share, and that such amount will be used for purposes of determining any dividend withholding on payments to Non-U.S. Holders, as more fully described in DPS&#146;s definitive proxy statement filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (&#147;SEC&#148;) on May&nbsp;29, 2018.&#160; DPS stockholders should review the Company&#146;s annual meeting proxy statement which provides additional details regarding the U.S. federal income tax treatment, and consult their own tax advisors regarding the particular tax consequences of receipt of the Special Dividend pursuant to the Merger Agreement in light of their particular circumstances.</font></p>
