 <p style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold">Item&#160;5.02.&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </p> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On November&#160;9, 2022, the Board of Directors (the &#8220;Board&#8221;) of Keurig Dr Pepper Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) accepted the resignation of Ozan Dokmecioglu as President and Chief Executive Officer and as a director of the Company, effective immediately, and the Board appointed Robert Gamgort, the Company&#8217;s current Executive Chairman and former President and Chief Executive Officer, to serve as President and Chief Executive Officer. Mr.&#160;Gamgort will continue to serve as Executive Chairman. Accordingly, the Board reduced its size to eleven (11)&#160;members. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr.&#160;Gamgort, 60, has served as Executive Chairman of the Company since January 2019 and served as President and Chief Executive Officer of the Company from July 2018 to July 2022. Prior to the merger between Keurig Green Mountain, Inc. (&#8220;KGM&#8221;) and Dr Pepper Snapple Group, Inc. that formed the Company in 2018, Mr.&#160;Gamgort served as Chief Executive Officer of KGM, beginning in 2016. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr.&#160;Gamgort has no family relationship with any directors or executive officers of the Company, nor are there any arrangements or understandings between Mr.&#160;Gamgort and any other persons pursuant to which he was selected as an officer of the Company. There are no related party transactions between Mr.&#160;Gamgort and the Company reportable under Item 5.02 of Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">8-K</span> and Item 404(a) of Regulation <span style="white-space:nowrap">S-K.</span> </p>
