<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>DR PEPPER SNAPPLE GROUP RAISES QUARTERLY DIVIDEND 10.4% AND AUTHORIZES ADDITIONAL $1 BILLION SHARE<BR>
REPURCHASE PROGRAM</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Plano, TX, Feb. 11, 2016 &#150; Dr Pepper Snapple Group, Inc. (NYSE: DPS) today announced that its Board
of Directors declared a quarterly dividend of $0.53 per share on the company&#146;s common stock &#150; a
10.4% increase in the dividend rate. The dividend is payable in U.S. dollars on April&nbsp;5, 2016, to
shareholders of record on March&nbsp;15, 2016.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">In addition, the company announced that its Board of Directors authorized the repurchase of an
additional $1&nbsp;billion of the company&#146;s common stock. In total, the company has now authorized $5
billion of share repurchases. Through the third quarter of 2015, the company repurchased $3.2
billion of its common stock.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>About Dr Pepper Snapple Group</B>
<BR>
Dr Pepper Snapple Group (NYSE: DPS) is a leading producer of flavored beverages in North America
and the Caribbean. Our success is fueled by more than 50 brands that are synonymous with
refreshment, fun and flavor. We have 6 of the top 10 non-cola soft drinks, and 13 of our 14 leading
brands are No.&nbsp;1 or No.&nbsp;2 in their flavor categories. In addition to our flagship Dr Pepper and
Snapple brands, our portfolio includes 7UP, A&W, Canada Dry, Clamato, Crush, Hawaiian Punch,
Mott&#146;s, Mr & Mrs T mixers, Pe&#241;afiel, Rose&#146;s, Schweppes, Squirt and Sunkist soda. To learn more
about our iconic brands and Plano, Texas-based company, please visit www.DrPepperSnapple.com. For
our latest news and updates, follow us at www.Facebook.com/DrPepperSnapple or
www.Twitter.com/DrPepperSnapple.
