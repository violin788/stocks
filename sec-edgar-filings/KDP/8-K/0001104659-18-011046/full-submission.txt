(&quot;Keurig&quot;) announced that they will host an investor event at Keurig&#146;s office in Burlington, Massachusetts, on March 20, 2018. Bob Gamgort, Chief Executive Officer of Keurig, Larry Young, President and Chief Executive Officer of Dr Pepper Snapple, and other executive leaders will provide a business overview and articulate the strategy and significant growth potential for Keurig Dr Pepper (&quot;KDP&quot;) upon consummation of the previously announced proposed merger of Dr Pepper Snapple and Maple Parent Holdings Corp., the parent of Keurig.</font></p>
