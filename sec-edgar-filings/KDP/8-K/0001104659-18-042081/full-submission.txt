<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman"><font style="font-size:10.0pt;">On June&nbsp;25, 2018, Dr Pepper Snapple Group,&nbsp;Inc. (&#147;DPS&#148; or the &#147;Company&#148;) and Maple Parent Holdings Corp. (&#147;Maple&#148;) announced the anticipated resignations of certain Company executives, including Martin M. Ellen, Executive Vice President&nbsp;&amp; Chief Financial Officer and James J. Johnston, President - Beverage Concentrates &amp; Latin America Beverages, upon (and subject to) completion of the merger contemplated by the Agreement and Plan of Merger (the &#147;Merger Agreement&#148;) among the Company, a special purpose merger subsidiary of the Company (&#147;Merger Sub&#148;) and Maple, the indirect parent of Keurig Green Mountain,&nbsp;Inc. </font>In addition, upon (and subject to) completion of the merger, Larry D. Young, President and Chief Executive Officer, will retire from the Company but remain on the Board of Directors.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">ITEM 8.01. Other Events</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On June&nbsp;25, 2018, the Company and Maple issued a joint press release announcing the above resignations of certain Company executives and the anticipated executive leadership team of the Company to be appointed upon (and subject to) completion of the Merger.&#160; Under the terms of the Merger Agreement, the Merger Sub will merge into Maple, with Maple becoming a wholly owned subsidiary of the Company.&#160; As previously announced, concurrently with the closing of the merger, DPS will be renamed &#147;Keurig Dr Pepper Inc.&#148; and, following the consummation of the merger, will trade under the symbol &#147;KDP&#148; on the New York Stock Exchange</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">A copy of the press release described above is filed as an exhibit to this Current Report on Form&nbsp;8-K and is incorporated by reference herein.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In addition, the Merger Agreement provides that following the completion of the merger, the Company&#146;s Board of Directors will be comprised of twelve members, including two directors designated by Mondel&#275;z International Holdings LLC (&#147;Mondel&#275;z LLC&#148;), a minority investor in Maple.&#160; The Company&#146;s definitive proxy statement, as filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on May&nbsp;29, 2018, identifies the two Mondel&#275;z LLC designees as Dirk Van de Put, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Mondel&#275;z International,&nbsp;Inc. and Brian Gladden, Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Mondel&#275;z International,&nbsp;Inc.&#160; Mondel&#275;z LLC has informed the Company and Maple that due to Mr.&nbsp;Gladden&#146;s planned departure from Mondel&#275;z International,&nbsp;Inc., it will designate Gerhard W. Pleuhs, Executive Vice President and General Counsel of Mondel&#275;z International,&nbsp;Inc., in place of Mr.&nbsp;Gladden.</font></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Leadership Team Announced for Keurig Dr Pepper</font></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">BURLINGTON, MA and PLANO, TX (June&nbsp;25, 2018) &#151; Dr Pepper Snapple Group,&nbsp;Inc. (NYSE: DPS) and Keurig Green Mountain,&nbsp;Inc. (&#147;Keurig&#148;) today jointly announced the leadership team for the new Keurig Dr Pepper (&#147;KDP&#148;), effective upon the expected July&nbsp;9, 2018 close of the merger transaction.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;Our new leadership team represents the very best talent across both great companies,&#148; stated Bob Gamgort, future CEO of Keurig Dr Pepper.&#160; &#147;With proven track records of delivering strong results,&nbsp;I am pleased to have these experienced executives join me in creating a new challenger in the beverage industry.&#160; Our combined selling and distribution capabilities, along with our portfolio of hot and cold beverages, will enable us to reach consumers anytime and anywhere they shop for a beverage.&#148;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Upon the successful close of the merger, the following executives will comprise the KDP executive leadership team:</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Jim Baldwin</font></b> is appointed Chief Legal Officer&nbsp;&amp; General Counsel. He joined Dr Pepper Snapple in 1997 as Assistant General Counsel and was most recently Executive Vice President and General Counsel.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Rodger Collins </font></b>is appointed President Direct Store Delivery, with responsibility for all company-owned DSD operations.&#160; He joined Dr Pepper Snapple in 2006 through its acquisition of&nbsp;Dr&nbsp;Pepper/Seven-Up Bottling Group. He has been with the Bottling Group and its predecessor companies since 1978 and was most recently President of Packaged Beverages.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Fernando Cortes </font></b>is appointed Chief Supply Chain Officer.&#160; He joined Dr Pepper Snapple in 2003 and was most recently Executive Vice President of Supply Chain.</p>
