<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On January&nbsp;31, 2017 (the &#147;Closing Date&#148;), Dr Pepper Snapple Group,&nbsp;Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) completed its previously announced acquisition of Bai Brands LLC (&#147;Bai&#148;), pursuant to the terms of the Agreement and Plan of Merger, dated as of November&nbsp;21, 2016 (the &#147;Original Merger Agreement&#148;), and as amended on January&nbsp;31, 2017 (&#147;Amendment No.&nbsp;1&#148; and, together with the Original Merger Agreement as amended by Amendment No.&nbsp;1, the &#147;Merger Agreement&#148;), by and among the Company, Superfruit Merger Sub, LLC (&#147;Merger Sub&#148;), Bai and Fortis Advisors LLC, in its capacity as the Member Representative. Capitalized terms used herein have the meanings set forth in the Merger Agreement.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On the Closing Date, pursuant to the terms of the Merger Agreement, Merger Sub merged with and into Bai (the &#147;Merger&#148;), with Bai surviving the Merger as a wholly-owned indirect subsidiary of the Company (the &#147;Effective Time&#148;).</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">At the Effective Time, each Class&nbsp;A Voting Common Unit, Class&nbsp;B Voting Common Unit, Non-Voting Common Unit and Profits Interest Unit held by the members of Bai (the &#147;Members&#148;) (together the &#147;Bai Units&#148;) (including all Bai Units to be issued upon the vesting of Restricted Units held by the Members and all Bai Units to be issued upon the exercise (including the deemed exercise) of Warrants held by the Members) were cancelled and exchanged for an aggregate of USD $1.7 billion in cash, as adjusted and subject to the reduction of certain expenses set forth in the Merger Agreement (the &#147;Merger Consideration&#148;).&#160; A portion of the Merger Consideration is being held in escrow to secure the indemnification obligations of the Members.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">As previously disclosed, on&nbsp;December&nbsp;14, 2016, the Company entered into the Seventh&nbsp;Supplemental Indenture by and among the Company, Well Fargo Bank, N.A. (&#147;Wells Fargo&#148;), as trustee, and the guarantors party thereto, to the Indenture, dated as of December&nbsp;15, 2009, by and among the Company, Wells Fargo and the guarantors party thereto, in connection with the issuance&nbsp;of USD $1.55 billion&nbsp;of senior unsecured notes consisting of&nbsp;$250 million&nbsp;aggregate principal amount of&nbsp;2.530%&nbsp;Senior Notes due&nbsp;2021&nbsp;(the &#147;2021&nbsp;Notes&#148;), $500 million&nbsp;aggregate principal amount of&nbsp;3.130%&nbsp;Senior Notes due&nbsp;2023&nbsp;(the &#147;2023&nbsp;Notes&#148;), $400 million&nbsp;aggregate principal amount of&nbsp;3.430%&nbsp;Senior Notes due&nbsp;2027&nbsp;(the &#147;2027&nbsp;Notes&#148;) and $400 million&nbsp;aggregate principal amount of&nbsp;4.420%&nbsp;Senior Notes due&nbsp;2046&nbsp;(the &#147;2046&nbsp;Notes&#148; and, together with the&nbsp;2021&nbsp;Notes, the&nbsp;2023&nbsp;Notes and the&nbsp;2027&nbsp;Notes, the &#147;Notes&#148;). The Notes were issued in an underwritten offering registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended. The Company used the net proceeds from the issuance of the Notes, together with working capital, to fund the Merger Consideration.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The foregoing description of the Merger&nbsp;Agreement&nbsp;does not purport to be complete and is subject to, and qualified in its entirety by, the full text of the Original Merger&nbsp;Agreement&nbsp;(filed as Exhibit&nbsp;2.1 to the Company&#146;s Current Report on Form&nbsp;8-K filed November&nbsp;23, 2016 and incorporated by reference herein) and Amendment No.&nbsp;1, which is filed as Exhibit&nbsp;2.2 to this Current Report on Form&nbsp;8-K and is incorporated herein by reference.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The acquisition for $1.7 billion in cash includes a tax benefit of approximately $400 million on a net present value basis.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Dr Pepper Snapple Group (NYSE: DPS) is a leading producer of flavored beverages in North America and the Caribbean.
