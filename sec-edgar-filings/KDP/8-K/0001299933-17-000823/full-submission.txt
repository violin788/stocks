<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>DR PEPPER SNAPPLE GROUP DECLARES QUARTERLY DIVIDEND</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Plano, TX, August&nbsp;10, 2017 &#150; Dr Pepper Snapple Group, Inc. (NYSE: DPS) today announced that its
Board of Directors declared a quarterly dividend of $0.58 per share on the company&#146;s common stock.
The dividend is payable in U.S. dollars on October&nbsp;5, 2017, to shareholders of record on September
14, 2017.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>About Dr Pepper Snapple Group</B>
<BR>
Dr Pepper Snapple Group (NYSE: DPS) is a leading producer of flavored beverages in North America
and the Caribbean. Our success is fueled by more than 50 brands that are synonymous with
refreshment, fun and flavor. We have seven of the top 10 non-cola soft drinks, and nine of our 10
leading brands are No.&nbsp;1 or No.&nbsp;2 in their flavor categories. In addition to our flagship Dr Pepper
and Snapple brands, our portfolio includes 7UP, A&W, Bai, Canada Dry, Clamato, Crush, Hawaiian
Punch, IBC, Mott&#146;s, Mr & Mrs T mixers, Pe&#241;afiel, Rose&#146;s, Schweppes, Squirt and Sunkist soda. To
learn more about our iconic brands and Plano, Texas-based company, please visit
www.DrPepperSnapple.com. For our latest news and updates, follow us at
www.Facebook.com/DrPepperSnapple or www.Twitter.com/DrPepperSnapple.
