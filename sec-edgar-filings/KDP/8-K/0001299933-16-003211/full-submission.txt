<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>DR PEPPER SNAPPLE GROUP DECLARES QUARTERLY DIVIDEND</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Plano, TX, November&nbsp;16, 2016 &#150; Dr Pepper Snapple Group, Inc. (NYSE: DPS) today announced that its
Board of Directors declared a quarterly dividend of $0.53 per share on the company&#146;s common stock.
The dividend is payable in U.S. dollars on January&nbsp;5, 2017, to shareholders of record on December
13, 2016.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>About Dr Pepper Snapple Group</B>
<BR>
Dr Pepper Snapple Group (NYSE: DPS) is a leading producer of flavored beverages in North America
and the Caribbean. Our success is fueled by more than 50 brands that are synonymous with
refreshment, fun and flavor. We have six of the top 10 non-cola soft drinks, and 13 of our 14
leading brands are No.&nbsp;1 or No.&nbsp;2 in their flavor categories. In addition to our flagship Dr Pepper
and Snapple brands, our portfolio includes 7UP, A&W, Canada Dry, Clamato, Crush, Hawaiian Punch,
Mott&#146;s, Mr & Mrs T mixers, Pe&#241;afiel, Rose&#146;s, Schweppes, Squirt and Sunkist soda. To learn more
about our iconic brands and Plano, Texas-based company, please visit www.DrPepperSnapple.com. For
our latest news and updates, follow us at www.Facebook.com/DrPepperSnapple or
www.Twitter.com/DrPepperSnapple.
