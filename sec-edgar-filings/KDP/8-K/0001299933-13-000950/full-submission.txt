<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><FONT style="font-size: 14pt"><B>DR PEPPER SNAPPLE GROUP TO PRESENT AT SANFORD C. BERNSTEIN STRATEGIC DECISIONS CONFERENCE 2013</B></FONT>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 14pt"><FONT style="font-size: 12pt">PLANO, Texas &#150; May&nbsp;21, 2013 &#150; Dr Pepper Snapple Group, Inc. (NYSE: DPS) today announced that
Larry Young, president and CEO, and Marty Ellen, chief financial officer, will present at the
Sanford C. Bernstein Strategic Decisions Conference 2013 in New York on Thursday, May&nbsp;30, at 9:00
a.m. EDT.
</FONT>

<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">The audio will be accessible live through the DPS website at <U>http://www.drpeppersnapple.com</U>
and will be archived for replay for a period of 14&nbsp;days.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>About Dr Pepper Snapple Group</B>
<BR>
Dr Pepper Snapple Group (NYSE: DPS) is a leading producer of flavored beverages in North America
and the Caribbean. Our success is fueled by more than 50 brands that are synonymous with
refreshment, fun and flavor. We have 6 of the top 10 non-cola soft drinks, and 13 of our 14 leading
brands are No.&nbsp;1 or No.&nbsp;2 in their flavor categories. In addition to our flagship Dr Pepper and
Snapple brands, our portfolio includes 7UP, A&W, Canada Dry, Clamato, Crush, Hawaiian Punch,
Mott&#146;s, Mr & Mrs T mixers, Pe&#241;afiel, Rose&#146;s, Schweppes, Squirt and Sunkist soda. To learn more
about our iconic brands and Plano, Texas-based company, please visit www.DrPepperSnapple.com. For
our latest news and updates, follow us at www.Facebook.com/DrPepperSnapple or
www.Twitter.com/DrPepperSnapple.
