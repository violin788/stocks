<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 3.02</font></b>&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; <b>Unregistered Sales of Equity Securities</b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On September&nbsp;27, 2018, Core Nutrition, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (&#147;Core&#148;), Keurig Dr Pepper Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;), Cranberry Merger Sub, LLC, a direct wholly owned subsidiary of the Company (&#147;Merger Sub&#148;), and Paul Nadel, as representative of the equityholders of Core entered into an Agreement and Plan of Merger (the &#147;Merger Agreement&#148;), pursuant to which, among other things, and subject to the satisfaction or waiver of specified conditions, Merger Sub will be merged with and into Core with Core surviving the merger as a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Company (the &#147;Transaction&#148;).</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">In the Transaction, each outstanding unit of equity interest held by the members of Core, subject to certain exceptions, will be converted into the right to receive the applicable per-unit portion of the merger consideration. The merger consideration, which represents an enterprise value of $525 million and is subject to customary post-closing working capital and other adjustments (the &#147;Final Transaction Value&#148;), will be funded substantially with shares of common stock of the Company, par value $0.01 per share (&#147;Company Common Stock&#148;) subject to certain adjustments paid in cash. The number of shares of Company Common Stock issued will be based on the Final Transaction Value and&#160; the volume weighted average of the closing prices of the Company Common Stock for the five (5)&nbsp;consecutive trading days ending on, and including, the second (2nd) trading day prior to the closing of the Transaction.&#160; Assuming a volume weighted average price of $23.44, the closing price of Company Common Stock on September&nbsp;26, 2018, and assuming for illustrative purposes only there are no further adjustments for working capital and other adjustments, the Company would issue approximately 19,347,270 shares of Company Common Stock as the stock portion of the merger consideration. The exact number of shares of Company Common Stock issued may be higher or lower.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">All shares of Company Common Stock to be issued in the Transaction will be issued to former equityholders of Core, pursuant to an exemption from registration under Section&nbsp;4(a)(2)&nbsp;of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, and Rule&nbsp;506 of Regulation D promulgated thereunder. Each former equityholder of Core receiving Company Common Stock will make cecrtain representations and warranties to the Company regarding, among other things, whether it is an accredited investor and its investment intent.</font></p>

<p align="right" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:right;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Exhibit 99.1<a name="Exhibit99_1_031623"></a></font></b></p>
<p align="right" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:right;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman"><img width="254" height="72" src="g360401mmi001.jpg"></font></p>
<p align="right" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:right;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><u><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE</font></u></b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p align="center" style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Keurig Dr Pepper to Acquire Core</font></b><b><font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;font-weight:bold;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font></b><b>, a Premium Enhanced Beverage Company</b></p>
<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">Acquisition represents the Company&#146;s latest move in the evolution of its allied brands portfolio</font></i></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">BURLINGTON, MA and PLANO, TX (September&nbsp;27, 2018) &#151; Keurig Dr Pepper (NYSE: KDP) today announced that it has entered into a definitive agreement to acquire CORE</font><font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;Nutrition LLC at a value of $525 million, or approximately $435 million net of anticipated tax benefits.&#160; Founded in 2015, the rapidly growing Core<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;Nutrition portfolio includes CORE<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;Hydration, a premium, nutrient-enhanced bottled water, and CORE<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;Organic, USDA-certified organic enhanced fruit hydration, both currently distributed by KDP.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The acquisition of CORE</font><font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;Nutrition will be funded with KDP common stock, net of KDP&#146;s current equity investment and certain other contractual adjustments.&#160; The Company expects the transaction to be neutral to KDP&#146;s adjusted diluted earnings per share&#160; in 2019 and accretive thereafter.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">CORE</font><font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;has been a KDP allied brand since 2016, with KDP currently managing approximately two-thirds of the brand&#146;s U.S. distribution.&#160; Net sales of all CORE<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;beverages have grown at an average annualized rate of approximately 115% over the past three years, with current retail sales(1)&nbsp;across both measured and unmeasured channels estimated in excess of $200 million in the latest 52 weeks.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Commenting on the announcement, KDP CEO Bob Gamgort stated, &#147;CORE</font><font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;has been a valued and successful allied brand partner, and we are pleased that this on-trend beverage will become part of our owned KDP family of brands.&#160; Our sales and distribution capabilities have helped fuel significant growth for the CORE<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;portfolio. Through this transaction we intend to realize the full growth potential for the business.&#148;</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Lance Collins, founder of CORE</font><font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;commented, &#147;We created CORE<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;to meet the increasing desires for innovative, enhanced water offerings with functional benefits for today&#146;s modern consumer.&#160; KDP has been an outstanding partner for CORE<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;and I am certain that, under its ownership, CORE<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;will continue to see tremendous long-term success.&#160; I am a strong supporter of the KDP strategy and business model and am looking forward to being a shareholder in the company.&#148;</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Following the closing of the acquisition, both CORE</font><font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;Hydration and CORE<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;Organic will continue to be distributed primarily through KDP&#146;s company-owned direct store delivery network and independent distributor network, while certain select existing CORE<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;Nutrition distribution partners are expected to maintain their channel coverage.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The transaction is subject to customary closing conditions and is expected to close by the end of 2018.&#160; Evercore acted as financial advisor to KDP and Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher&nbsp;&amp; Flom LLP served as legal counsel.&#160; Gibson, Dunn&nbsp;&amp; Crutcher LLP served as legal counsel for CORE</font><font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;Nutrition and Whipstitch Capital provided a fairness opinion.</p>
