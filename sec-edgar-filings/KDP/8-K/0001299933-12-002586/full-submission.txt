<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>DR PEPPER SNAPPLE GROUP DECLARES QUARTERLY DIVIDEND</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Plano, TX, Nov. 16, 2012 &#150; Dr Pepper Snapple Group, Inc. (NYSE: DPS) today announced that its
Board of Directors declared a quarterly dividend of $0.34 per share on the company&#146;s common stock.
The dividend is payable in U.S. dollars on Jan. 4, 2013, to shareholders of record on Dec. 17,
2012.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>About Dr Pepper Snapple</B>
<BR>
Dr Pepper Snapple Group (NYSE: DPS) is a leading producer of flavored beverages in North America
and the Caribbean. Our success is fueled by more than 50 brands that are synonymous with
refreshment, fun and flavor. We have 6 of the top 10 non-cola soft drinks, and 11 of our 14 leading
brands are No.&nbsp;1 in their flavor categories. In addition to our flagship Dr Pepper and Snapple
brands, our portfolio includes 7UP, A&W, Canada Dry, Clamato, Crush, Hawaiian Punch, Mott&#146;s, Mr &
Mrs T mixers, Pe&#241;afiel, Rose&#146;s, Schweppes, Squirt and Sunkist soda. To learn more about our iconic
brands and Plano, Texas-based company, please visit DrPepperSnapple.com. For our latest news and
updates, follow us at Facebook.com/DrPepperSnapple or Twitter.com/DrPepperSnapple.
