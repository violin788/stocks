	Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.

<P ALIGN="LEFT">
<FONT SIZE="2">
On September 22, 2016, Dr Pepper Snapple Group, Inc. (the "Company") issued a press release announcing that Jos&#233; Guti&#233;rrez has joined the Company's Board of Directors (the "Board") effective today. Mr. Guti&#233;rrez was designated as an independent director and will stand for re-election at the annual stockholder's meeting occuring on May 18, 2017. Mr. Guti&#233;rrez will serve on the Corporate Governance and Nominating Committee. <br><br>Mr. Guti&#233;rrez, 55, currently serves as senior executive vice president and chief of staff to the chairman & CEO of AT&T. Over the course of 25 years at AT&T, Mr. Guti&#233;rrez has held several senior executive positions, including president and CEO of Southwestern Bell (AT&T Southwest), president of AT&T Global Enterprise Solutions, president of AT&T Wholesale and Access Solutions, president and CEO of AT&T Advertising Solutions and president of Industry Markets. Mr. Guti&#233;rrez currently serves on the board of directors of Denny's Corporation. He is also a member of the Strategic Development Board at the University of Missouri&#x2019;s Trulaske College of Business, a member of the Missouri 100 Advisory Board and co-chairman of the National Campaign at the University of Missouri. He is vice chairman of the Thompson Foundation for Autism and Neurodevelopmental Disorders, a member of the board of directors of the Cotton Bowl, a former board member of the World Affairs Council of Dallas/Forth Worth and a former trustee of the Dallas Museum of Art.<br><br>As a non-management member of the Company's Board of Directors, Mr. Guti&#233;rrez will receive an annual retainer of $100,000 and an award for restricted stock units equal to $130,000 in remuneration for his services. Mr. Guti&#233;rrez will receive a pro-rated retainer of $50,000 and an award of restricted stock units equal to $65,000 in remuneration for his services in 2016. <br><br>There are no arrangements or understandings known to the Company between Mr. Guti&#233;rrez and any other person pursuant to which he was elected as a director. There are no transactions in which Mr. Guti&#233;rrez has an interest requiring disclosures pursuant to Item 404(a) of Regulation S-K of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended. <br><br>A copy of the press release is attached hereto as Exhbit 99.1 and incorporated herein by reference.

<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><FONT style="font-size: 12pt"><B>JOS&#201; GUTI&#201;RREZ JOINS DR PEPPER SNAPPLE GROUP BOARD OF DIRECTORS</B></FONT>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">PLANO, Texas (Sept. 22, 2016) &#150; Dr Pepper Snapple Group, Inc. (NYSE: DPS) today announced the
appointment of Jos&#233; Guti&#233;rrez to its board of directors.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><img src="e43003-162667581258821bbe_2.jpg">Mr. Guti&#233;rrez, 55, serves as senior executive vice
president and chief of staff to the chairman and CEO of AT&T. Prior to assuming his current role in
2015, he held several senior executive positions at AT&T, including president and CEO of
Southwestern Bell (AT&T Southwest), president of AT&T Global Enterprise Solutions, president of
AT&T Wholesale and Access Solutions, president and CEO of AT&T Advertising Solutions and president
of Industry Markets.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">&#147;Over the course of 25&nbsp;years at AT&T, Jos&#233; has built a highly accomplished career leading large,
diverse teams serving business and residential customers across the U.S. and around the world,&#148;
said Wayne R. Sanders, DPS chairman. &#147;With this experience, along with his passion for customer
service, he is a great fit for the board of a company like ours where customer centricity is a core
value.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Mr.&nbsp;Guti&#233;rrez serves on the board of directors of Denny&#146;s Corporation. He is also a member of the
Strategic Development Board at the University of Missouri&#146;s Trulaske College of Business, a member
of the Missouri 100 Advisory Board and co-chairman of the National Campaign at the University of
Missouri. He is vice chairman of the Thompson Foundation for Autism and Neurodevelopmental
Disorders, a member of the board of directors of the Cotton Bowl, a former board member of the
World Affairs Council of Dallas/Fort Worth and a former trustee of the Dallas Museum of Art.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">He earned a master&#146;s degree in management from the Kellogg Graduate School of Management at
Northwestern University, as well as bachelor&#146;s and master&#146;s degrees in accountancy from the
University of Missouri. He also attended the Colegio Universitario de Estudios Financieros in
Madrid, Spain.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>About Dr Pepper Snapple Group</B>
<BR>
Dr Pepper Snapple Group (NYSE: DPS) is a leading producer of flavored beverages in North America
and the Caribbean. Our success is fueled by more than 50 brands that are synonymous with
refreshment, fun and flavor. We have six of the top 10 non-cola soft drinks, and 13 of our 14
leading brands are No.&nbsp;1 or No.&nbsp;2 in their flavor categories. In addition to our flagship Dr Pepper
and Snapple brands, our portfolio includes 7UP, A&W, Canada Dry, Clamato, Crush, Hawaiian Punch,
Mott&#146;s, Mr & Mrs T mixers, Pe&#241;afiel, Rose&#146;s, Schweppes, Squirt and Sunkist soda. To learn more
about our iconic brands and Plano, Texas-based company, please visit DrPepperSnapple.com. For our
latest news and updates, follow us at Facebook.com/DrPepperSnapple or Twitter.com/DrPepperSnapple.
