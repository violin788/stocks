<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>DR PEPPER SNAPPLE GROUP TO ACQUIRE BAI BRANDS, LLC</B>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B><I>Bai is the fastest growing brand in the enhanced water category</I></B>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 12pt"><B><I>Solid innovation pipeline that extends to other categories</I></B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">PLANO, TX and HAMILTON, NJ, November&nbsp;22, 2016 &#150; Dr Pepper Snapple Group, Inc. (NYSE: DPS) today
announced that it has reached an agreement to acquire Bai Brands, LLC (&#147;Bai&#148; or the &#147;Company&#148;), and
its complete portfolio of high-growth premium antioxidant infused beverages. The cash purchase
price of $1.7&nbsp;billion includes a tax benefit of approximately $400&nbsp;million on a net present value
basis and will be financed through new unsecured notes and short term commercial paper. We expect
to maintain our strong investment grade credit profile and have no plans to change our existing
shareholder dividends and share repurchase distributions.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Bai provides a strong platform to incubate and grow better-for-you beverages throughout the
non-carbonated and carbonated beverage sectors. It is expected to generate approximately $425
million in net sales in 2017 and add an incremental $132&nbsp;million to our current net sales
expectation for 2017. The transaction is expected to be approximately $0.03 dilutive to reported
diluted EPS in 2017 driven by planned increases in marketing investments behind the brand and
increased interest expense associated with the financing of the purchase price. The transaction is
expected to be accretive to reported diluted EPS in 2018.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">&#147;We&#146;re excited to welcome Bai into our family of great brands,&#148; said Larry Young, DPS President and
CEO. &#147;In a relatively short time, Bai has carved out a leadership position in the enhanced water
category and has now extended that success into other fast-growing and profitable categories. We&#146;re
equally impressed with their innovation pipeline, which will continue to meet the needs of
consumers seeking great tasting, low-calorie beverages with natural flavors and no artificial
sweeteners.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Young continued, &#147;Bai has contributed greatly to our allied brand lineup since we began
distributing it broadly in 2013. Adding it to the broad range of choices and options in our
company-owned portfolio is a natural next step. Moving forward, we will empower Bai&#146;s management
team to continue the breakthrough and disruptive branding and innovation that have revolutionized
their categories and work with them to put the brand in front of more consumers in more places.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Bai is one of the fastest growing beverage brands, offering a family of premium better-for-you
beverages. The Company&#146;s product portfolio spans across several high-growth beverage categories
including enhanced water, carbonated flavored water, coconut water and premium ready-to-drink teas.
With its Bai, Bai Bubbles, Cocofusion and other innovative brands, Bai is positioned for expanding
growth in key beverage segments. These highly profitable categories are projected to continue to
grow worldwide for the foreseeable future. The acquisition of Bai will further enable us to meet
growing consumer demand for better-for-you beverages.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Bai will operate within the Packaged Beverages segment and continue to be led by founder Ben Weiss.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">&#147;Over the past seven years, Bai has proven to be an agent of change in a marketplace that is
rapidly evolving,&#148; said Weiss. &#147;We&#146;ve worked tirelessly to challenge the notion that better-for-you
beverages can&#146;t taste good. On our journey, we found a strong ally in DPS, an ally who embraced
our mission to change the way the world drinks. Now, it only makes sense to continue our quest
together. We are thrilled to join the DPS family and create a new path forward with infinite
possibilities.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">The transaction, which is subject to customary closing conditions, is expected to close in the
first quarter of 2017. The boards of both companies have approved the transaction.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Credit Suisse Securities (USA)&nbsp;LLC is serving as exclusive financial advisor to Dr Pepper Snapple
Group and Morgan, Lewis & Bockius LLP is acting as legal counsel. J.P. Morgan Securities LLC is
serving as exclusive financial advisor to Bai and Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom LLP is
acting as legal counsel.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>Conference Call</B>
<BR>
At 7:30 a.m. (CST)&nbsp;today, Dr Pepper Snapple Group will host a conference call with investors to
discuss the Bai acquisition. The conference call and slide presentation will be accessible live
through DPS&#146;s website at http://www.drpeppersnapple.com and will be archived for replay for a
period of 14&nbsp;days.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt"><B>About Dr Pepper Snapple Group</B>
<BR>
Dr Pepper Snapple Group (NYSE: DPS) is a leading producer of flavored beverages in North America
and the Caribbean. Our success is fueled by more than 50 brands that are synonymous with
refreshment, fun and flavor. We have six of the top 10 non-cola soft drinks, and 13 of our 14
leading brands are No.&nbsp;1 or No.&nbsp;2 in their flavor categories. In addition to our flagship Dr Pepper
and Snapple brands, our portfolio includes 7UP, A&W, Canada Dry, Clamato, Crush, Hawaiian Punch,
Mott&#146;s, Mr & Mrs T mixers, Pe&#241;afiel, Rose&#146;s, Schweppes, Squirt and Sunkist soda. To learn more
about our iconic brands and Plano, Texas-based company, please visit
