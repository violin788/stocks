<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><FONT style="font-size: 11pt"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of

</FONT>

<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt">(b)


<P align="left" style="font-size: 11pt">On May&nbsp;18, 2017 Joyce Roche retired as a director of Dr Pepper Snapple Group, Inc. Ms.&nbsp;Roche was an
independent director and did not stand for re-election at the Annual Meeting of Stockholders held
on May&nbsp;18, 2017 because she reached mandatory retirement age under the Corporate Governance
Guidelines.
