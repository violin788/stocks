<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">On August&#160;1, 2022, HealthEquity,&#160;Inc. (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration: underline">Company</span>&#8221;)
announced that Edward R. (Ted) Bloomberg, the Company&#8217;s Chief Operating Officer, ceased serving as the Company&#8217;s Chief
Operating Officer, effective immediately. Mr.&#160;Bloomberg will serve as a Special Advisor to the Company for a limited period
of time, in which role he will assist in transitioning his responsibilities as determined by Jon Kessler, the Company&#8217;s
President and Chief Executive Officer. The Company also announced that Mr.&#160;Kessler will assume the duties and responsibilities
of the Company&#8217;s principal operating officer in connection with Mr.&#160;Bloomberg&#8217;s resignation.</p>
