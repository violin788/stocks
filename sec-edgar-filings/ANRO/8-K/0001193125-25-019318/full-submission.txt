<td style="vertical-align:top;text-align:left"> <p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement </p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On February&#160;3, 2025, Alto Neuroscience, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) entered into a Sales Agreement (the &#8220;Agreement&#8221;) with Leerink Partners LLC (the &#8220;Agent&#8221;), pursuant to which the Company from time to time may offer and sell shares (the &#8220;ATM Shares&#8221;) of its common stock, par value $0.0001 per share (&#8220;Common Stock&#8221;), through or to the Agent (the &#8220;ATM Offering&#8221;). The ATM Shares will be offered and sold pursuant to a Registration Statement on Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">S-3</span> filed by the Company on February&#160;3, 2025 (the &#8220;Registration Statement&#8221;) and the ATM Offering prospectus that forms a part of such Registration Statement, following such time, if ever, as the Registration Statement is declared effective by the Securities and Exchange Commission.
