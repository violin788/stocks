<p style="Background-color:#FFFFFF;margin-bottom:0pt;margin-top:0pt;text-indent:0%;font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align:justify;Background-color:#FFFFFF;margin-bottom:0pt;margin-top:0pt;text-indent:0%;color:#0A0A0A;font-family:Cambria;font-size:11pt;font-weight:normal;font-style:normal;text-transform:none;font-variant: normal;"><a name="_Hlk39725199"></a>The Phase 1b study of GB004 was a multi-center, randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled study which enrolled 34 patients with active mild-to-moderate ulcerative colitis. Patients were randomized 2:1 to receive either a 120mg once-daily dose of a solution formulation of GB004 (n=23) or placebo (n=11). The primary objective of the study was to evaluate the safety and tolerability of GB004 administered over 28 days. Pharmacokinetics were evaluated as a secondary objective, while exploratory objectives included measurements of pharmacodynamics and clinical outcomes. Histology, endoscopic improvement, and mucosal healing were evaluated individually in two segments of the large intestine: the sigmoid colon and rectum.</p>
