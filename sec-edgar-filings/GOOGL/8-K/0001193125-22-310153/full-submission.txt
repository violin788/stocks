(&#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">Alphabet</span>&#8221;) approved a new equity award for Sundar Pichai, Chief Executive Officer of Alphabet and Google LLC (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">CEO</span>&#8221;).</p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Committee currently follows a triennial grant cycle for CEO equity awards. Mr.&#160;Pichai&#8217;s last equity award was granted in December 2019.</p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As with the 2019 award, the award consists of both performance-based and time-based equity. The vesting of a significant portion of the award will depend on Alphabet&#8217;s total shareholder return (relative to S&amp;P 100 companies) (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">TSR</span>&#8221;), and this performance-based equity may not vest at all.</p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Committee recognizes Mr.&#160;Pichai&#8217;s strong performance as CEO. The <span style="white-space:nowrap">on-target</span> value of the award is unchanged from the 2019 award. However, relative to the 2019 award, the Committee made two design changes such that more of the award&#8217;s vesting dependent on performance: (1) increased the proportion of performance stock units (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">PSUs</span>&#8221;) to 60% from 43%, and (2) increased the performance requirement for <span style="white-space:nowrap">on-target</span> PSU payout to 55th percentile from 50th percentile of TSR. These changes further align Mr.&#160;Pichai&#8217;s compensation to long-term shareholder value creation and Alphabet&#8217;s stock performance relative to the S&amp;P 100 over the applicable performance periods.</p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">For this triennial award, Mr.&#160;Pichai was granted two tranches of PSUs<span style="font-weight:bold">&#160;</span>with a target value of $63,000,000 each. The target number of PSUs was calculated by dividing the target value of the total PSU grant by the average closing price of Alphabet&#8217;s Class&#160;C capital stock during the month of November 2022 (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">Average Closing Price</span>&#8221;). The PSUs will vest, if at all, based on the TSR performance over a 2023-2024 performance period for the first tranche and over a 2023-2025 performance period for the second tranche, subject to continued employment on each applicable vesting date. Depending upon Alphabet&#8217;s TSR performance, the number of PSUs that vest in a tranche will range from <span style="white-space:nowrap">0%-200%</span> of target. Each vested PSU will entitle Mr.&#160;Pichai to receive one share of Alphabet&#8217;s Class&#160;C capital stock. Upon termination of employment by reason of death, unvested PSUs with respect to a tranche will immediately vest at target (or actual if the performance period ended prior to death). Upon termination of employment by Alphabet without cause, PSUs will vest based on actual performance but will be prorated based on the number of calendar days in the performance period that Mr.&#160;Pichai performed services and any remaining unvested PSUs will be forfeited. The PSUs are subject to the terms and conditions of Alphabet&#8217;s Amended and Restated 2021 Stock Plan and the applicable PSU agreement. The foregoing description is only a summary of the terms of the PSU award and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of Mr.&#160;Pichai&#8217;s form PSU agreement, which will be filed as an exhibit to Alphabet&#8217;s Annual Report on Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">10-K</span> for the fiscal year ending December&#160;31, 2022.</p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The triennial award also included a grant of $84,000,000 in the form of Alphabet restricted stock units (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">GSUs</span>&#8221;). The number of GSUs was calculated by dividing the GSU award amount by the Average Closing Price. These GSUs will vest over three years as follows: 1/12th will vest on March&#160;25, 2023, and an additional 1/12th will vest quarterly thereafter until fully vested, in each case subject to continued employment on each applicable vesting date. Upon vesting, each GSU will entitle Mr.&#160;Pichai to receive one share of Alphabet&#8217;s Class&#160;C capital stock. Upon termination of employment by reason of death, all unvested GSUs will immediately vest. All GSUs are subject to the terms and conditions of Alphabet&#8217;s Amended and Restated 2021 Stock Plan and the applicable GSU agreement.</p></div></div> <p style="page-break-before:always"></p> <hr style="color:#999999;height:3px;width:100%" /> <div style="text-align:center"> <div style="width:8.5in;text-align:left;margin-left: auto;margin-right: auto"> <p style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:center">SIGNATURE</p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the undersigned hereunto duly authorized.</p> <p style="font-size:12pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&#160;</p>
