<td style="width:11%;vertical-align:top" align="left"><span style="font-weight:bold">Item&#160;5.02.</span></td>
<td align="left" style="vertical-align:top"> <p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On April&#160;6, 2021, the Leadership Development and Compensation Committee of the Board of Directors (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">Board</span>&#8221;) of Alphabet Inc. (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">Alphabet</span>&#8221;) approved a new compensation arrangement for Ruth Porat, Chief Financial Officer of Alphabet and Google LLC. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Ms.&#160;Porat was granted an equity award consisting of one tranche of performance stock units (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">PSUs</span>&#8221;) with a target value of $5,000,000. The target number of PSUs was calculated by dividing the target value of $5,000,000 by<span style="font-weight:bold"> </span>the average closing price of Alphabet&#8217;s Class&#160;C capital stock during the month of March 2021 (the &#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">Average Closing Price</span>&#8221;). The PSUs will vest, if at all, based on the Total Shareholder Return (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">TSR</span>&#8221;) performance of Alphabet relative to the companies comprising the S&amp;P 100 over a 2021-2023 performance period, subject to continued employment. Depending upon performance, the number of PSUs that vest will range from <span style="white-space:nowrap">0%-200%</span> of target. Upon vesting, each PSU will entitle Ms.&#160;Porat to receive one share of Alphabet&#8217;s Class&#160;C capital stock. Upon termination of employment by reason of death, unvested PSUs will immediately vest, assuming target (or actual if the performance period ended prior to death) and be settled in shares of Alphabet&#8217;s Class&#160;C capital stock. Upon termination of employment by Alphabet without cause, PSUs will vest based on actual performance but will be prorated based on the number of calendar days in the performance period Ms.&#160;Porat performed services and any remaining unvested PSUs will be forfeited. The PSUs are subject to the terms and conditions of Alphabet&#8217;s Amended and Restated 2012 Stock Plan. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">This grant is intended to be part of a transition to a performance-based equity structure for Ms.&#160;Porat. Going forward, we anticipate that a portion of Ms.&#160;Porat&#8217;s equity compensation will be granted in the form of PSUs. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The foregoing description is only a summary of the terms of the PSU award and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Performance Stock Unit Agreement, which will be filed as an exhibit to Alphabet&#8217;s Form <span style="white-space:nowrap">10-Q.</span> </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Ms.&#160;Porat was also granted an award of $5,000,000 in the form of restricted stock units (&#8220;<span style="text-decoration:underline">GSUs</span>&#8221;). The number of GSUs comprising the grant was calculated by dividing the equity award amount by the Average Closing Price. These GSUs will vest as follows: 1/4th will vest on March&#160;25, 2022 and an additional 1/4th will vest quarterly thereafter until fully vested, in each case subject to continued employment. Upon vesting, each GSU will entitle Ms.&#160;Porat to receive one share of Alphabet&#8217;s Class&#160;C capital stock. Upon termination of employment by reason of death, all unvested GSUs will immediately vest in full and be settled in shares of Alphabet&#8217;s Class&#160;C capital stock. All GSUs are subject to the terms and conditions of Alphabet&#8217;s Amended and Restated 2012 Stock Plan. </p>
