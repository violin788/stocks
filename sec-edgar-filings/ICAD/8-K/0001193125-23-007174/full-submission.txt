<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">NASHUA, N.H. &#150; January&nbsp;11, 2023&#151;iCAD, Inc.&nbsp;(NASDAQ: ICAD),&nbsp;a global medical technology leader providing innovative cancer detection
