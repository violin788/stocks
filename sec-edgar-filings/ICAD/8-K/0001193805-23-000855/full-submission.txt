<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">On June&#160;13, 2023, iCAD, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;)
held its annual meeting of stockholders (the &#8220;Meeting&#8221;). The total number of shares entitled to vote at the Meeting was 25,260,576
and there were present at the Meeting, in person or by proxy, 19,074,480 shares, which constituted a quorum for the Meeting.</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">At the Meeting, the stockholders voted:</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0 0pt 24.45pt; background-color: white">(1) to elect Dana Brown, Michael
Klein, Dr. Rakesh Patel, Andy Sassine, and Dr. Susan Wood to the board of directors of the Company until the next annual meeting of stockholders;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0 0pt 24.45pt; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0 0pt 24.45pt; background-color: white">(2) to approve, by&#160;non-binding&#160;advisory

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0 0pt 24.45pt; background-color: white">&#160;</p>

<p style="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0 0pt 24.45pt; background-color: white">(3) to ratify the selection of
BDO USA, LLP as the Company&#8217;s independent registered public accounting firm, for the fiscal year ending December&#160;31, 2023.</p>
