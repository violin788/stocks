 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01. Entry into Material Definitive Agreement </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On June&nbsp;22, 2020, iCAD, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;), along with its wholly-owned subsidiaries Xoft, Inc. and Xoft Solutions LLC
(together with the Company, collectively, the &#147;Borrowers&#148;), entered into a First Amendment dated as of June&nbsp;16, 2020 (the &#147;First Amendment&#147;), to the Loan and Security Agreement, dated as of March&nbsp;30, 2020 (the
&#147;Loan Agreement&#148;), with Western Alliance Bank (the &#147;Lender&#148;). The First Amendment (i)&nbsp;amended the financial covenant tied to minimum consolidated revenue contained in the Loan Agreement to provide that, for the testing
periods following December&nbsp;31, 2020, the Borrowers and the Lender will work together in good faith to set future minimum consolidated revenue amounts or the Borrowers will comply with an unrestricted cash to indebtedness covenant,
(ii)&nbsp;deleted the maximum net loss financial covenant contained in the Loan Agreement, (iii)&nbsp;added an unrestricted cash to total indebtedness financial covenant to the Loan Agreement and (iv)&nbsp;stipulated that the Borrowers must be in
compliance with at least one of the foregoing financial covenants at all times. The Borrowers paid the Lender a fee of $10,000 for the First Amendment. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The foregoing description of the First Amendment is not a complete description of all terms and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of
the First Amendment, a copy of which will be filed with the Company&#146;s Quarterly Report on Form <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">10-Q</FONT> for the fiscal quarter ended June&nbsp;30, 2020. </P>
</DIV></Center>
