<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center; background-color: white"><FONT STYLE="font-size: 12pt"><B>iCAD
Pre-Announces Estimated Q4 2024 Revenue</B></FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B><I>iCAD to Participate in the
BTIG at Snowbird: </I></B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B><I>12<SUP>th</SUP> Annual MedTech,
Digital Health, Life Science &amp; Diagnostic Tools Conference</I></B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; color: #0D2C5E"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">NASHUA, NH., January 28, 2025 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE)
-- iCAD, Inc. (NASDAQ: ICAD) (&ldquo;iCAD&rdquo; or the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;), a global leader on a mission to create a world where cancer
can&rsquo;t hide by providing clinically proven AI-powered breast health solutions, today <FONT>reported select
preliminary, unaudited financial results for the fourth quarter of 2024. Based on preliminary, unaudited financial information, the Company
expects total revenue for the fourth quarter of 2024 to be between approximately $5.1 and approximately $5.3 million. Total Annual Recurring
Revenue (T-ARR) was approximately $9.8 million, up 11% year-over-year. In the fourth quarter of 2024, the Company closed 106 deals, 19
of which were cloud deals with both new and established customers.</FONT></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">iCAD also announces it will participate in the BTIG at Snowbird: 12th
Annual MedTech, Digital Health, Life Science &amp; Diagnostic Tools Conference from February 11-12, 2025, in Snowbird, Utah. Dana Brown,
Chief Executive Officer, and Eric Lonnqvist, Chief Financial Officer, of iCAD will participate in one-on-one meetings with investors at
the event. To request a meeting with iCAD, investors should contact their BTIG representative.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">iCAD&rsquo;s consolidated financial statements for the twelve months
ended December 31, 2024, are not yet available, and full audited results for the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2024 will
be announced in March 2025.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">The preliminary estimated financial information included in this press
release for the fourth quarter ended December 31, 2024 is based solely on management&rsquo;s estimates reflecting currently available
preliminary information, and remains subject to iCAD&rsquo;s consideration of subsequent events, particularly as it relates to material
estimates and assumptions used in preparing our consolidated financial statements for the twelve months ended December 31, 2024. iCAD&rsquo;s
final consolidated financial results as of and for the three months ended December 31, 2024 may differ materially from estimates and the
interim balances set forth in this release. Furthermore, the information presented herein does not include all information necessary for
an understanding of the Company&rsquo;s full fiscal year ended December 31, 2024.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>About iCAD</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">iCAD, Inc. (NASDAQ: ICAD) is a global leader&nbsp;on a mission to create
a world where cancer can&rsquo;t hide by providing clinically proven AI-powered solutions that enable medical providers to accurately
and reliably detect cancer&nbsp;earlier and improve patient outcomes. Headquartered in Nashua, N.H., iCAD&rsquo;s industry-leading ProFound
Breast Health Suite provides AI-powered&nbsp;mammography&nbsp;analysis for&nbsp;breast&nbsp;cancer detection, density assessment and risk
evaluation. The ProFound Breast Health Suite is cleared by the U.S. Food &amp; Drug Administration (FDA) and has received CE mark and
Health Canada licensing. Used by thousands of providers serving millions of patients, ProFound is available in over 50 countries. In the
last five years alone, iCAD estimates reading more than 40 million mammograms worldwide, with nearly 30% being tomosynthesis. For more
information, visit&nbsp;www.icadmed.com.</P>
