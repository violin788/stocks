<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On June&nbsp;26, 2020, iCAD, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) held its annual meeting of stockholders (the &#147;Meeting&#148;). The total number of shares
entitled to vote at the Meeting was 22,867,121 and there were present at the Meeting, in person or by proxy, 18,531,978 shares, which constituted a quorum for the Meeting. At the Meeting, the stockholders voted: </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">(1) to elect Michael Klein, Nathaniel Dalton, Dr.&nbsp;Rakesh Patel, Andy Sassine and Dr.&nbsp;Susan Wood to the board of directors of the
Company until the next annual meeting of stockholders; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">(2) to approve and adopt an amendment to the Company&#146;s Certificate of
Incorporation to increase the authorized shares of common stock from 30,000,000 to 60,000,000; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">(3) to approve, by <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-binding</FONT> advisory vote, the resolution approving named executive officer compensation; and </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; margin-left:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">(4) to
ratify the selection of BDO USA, LLP as the Company&#146;s independent registered public accounting firm, for the fiscal year ending December&nbsp;31, 2020. </P>
