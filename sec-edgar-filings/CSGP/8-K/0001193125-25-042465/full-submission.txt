Pursuant to the Merger Agreement, among other things, (i)&#160;Merger Sub I was merged with and into Matterport (the &#8220;First Merger&#8221;), with Matterport surviving the First Merger as a wholly-owned subsidiary of CoStar Group (the &#8220;Surviving Corporation&#8221;) and (ii)&#160;as the Threshold Percentage (as defined in the Merger Agreement) was at least 40%, immediately following the First Merger and as part of a single integrated transaction, the Surviving Corporation was merged with and into Merger Sub II (the &#8220;Second Merger&#8221; and, together with the First Merger, the &#8220;Mergers&#8221;), with Merger Sub II surviving the Second Merger as a wholly-owned subsidiary of CoStar Group. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Upon completion of the Mergers, pursuant to the terms and conditions of the Merger Agreement, each share of Matterport Class&#160;A common stock, par value $0.0001 per share (the &#8220;Matterport Common Stock&#8221;), issued and outstanding immediately prior to the effective time of the First Merger (the &#8220;First Effective Time&#8221;) (other than any cancelled shares) was converted into (i)&#160;a number of shares of common stock of CoStar Group, par value $0.01 per share (each, a &#8220;CoStar Share&#8221;), equal to 0.03552 (the &#8220;Exchange Ratio&#8221;) and (ii) $2.75 in cash per share, without interest.
