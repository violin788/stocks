Aehr has developed and introduced several innovative products including the FOX-P<sup style="vertical-align:super">TM</sup> families of test and burn-in systems and FOX WaferPak<sup style="vertical-align:super">TM</sup> Aligner, FOX WaferPak Contactor, FOX DiePak<sup style="vertical-align:super">&#174;</sup> Carrier and FOX DiePak Loader.
