 <p style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold">Item&#160;8.01 Other Events. </p> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As previously disclosed, on February&#160;20, 2020, <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> US, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;), SoftBank Group Corp.&#160;(&#8220;SoftBank&#8221;) and Deutsche Telekom AG entered into a letter agreement (the &#8220;Letter Agreement&#8221;) concurrently with an amendment to the business combination agreement relating to the merger of the Company and Sprint Corporation.&#160;The Letter Agreement required SoftBank to cause its applicable affiliates to surrender to the Company, for no additional consideration, 48,751,557 shares of the Company&#8217;s common stock immediately following the effective time of such merger. The Letter Agreement also requires the Company to issue to SoftBank an equivalent number of shares (the &#8220;SoftBank Specified Shares&#8221;), for no additional consideration, if the trailing <span style="white-space:nowrap"><span style="white-space:nowrap">45-trading-day</span></span> volume-weighted average price per share (&#8220;VWAP&#8221;) of the Company&#8217;s common stock on NASDAQ is equal to or greater than a certain threshold price, as adjusted in accordance with the Letter Agreement (the &#8220;Threshold Price&#8221;), at any time during the period from April&#160;1, 2022 through December&#160;31, 2025. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As of the close of trading on December 22, 2023, the <span style="white-space:nowrap">45-trading</span> day VWAP exceeded $149.35, the current Threshold Price. The Company will promptly issue the SoftBank Specified Shares to SoftBank in accordance with the Letter Agreement and in reliance on the exemption from registration provided by Section&#160;4(a)(2) of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended. </p>
