<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"> <P STYLE=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman; " ALIGN="left"><B>Entry Into a Material Definitive Agreement. </B></P></TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On December&nbsp;20, 2018, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> USA,&nbsp;Inc. <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">(&#147;T-Mobile</FONT> USA&#148;)
entered into the Thirty-Eighth Supplemental Indenture (the &#147;Supplemental Indenture&#148;) by and among <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> USA, the guarantors party thereto and Deutsche Bank Trust Company Americas (the
&#147;Trustee&#148;), which amends and supplements the Indenture, dated April&nbsp;28, 2013, by and among <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> USA, the guarantors party thereto and Deutsche Bank Trust Company Americas (as amended and
supplemented, the &#147;Indenture&#148;). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Supplemental Indenture effects certain amendments (the &#147;Indenture Amendments&#148;) to the Indenture
pertaining to <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> USA&#146;s (i)&nbsp;Senior Reset Notes due 2023 (the &#147;2023 Senior Notes&#148;), (ii) 4.000% Senior Notes due <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">2022-1</FONT> (the &#147;2022 Senior
Notes&#148;), (iii) 5.125% Senior Notes due <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">2025-1</FONT> (the &#147;2025 Senior Notes&#148;), (iv) 5.375% Senior Notes due <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">2027-1</FONT> (the &#147;2027 Senior Notes&#148;), (v)
4.500% Senior Notes due <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">2026-1</FONT> (the &#147;2026 Senior Notes&#148;) and (vi) 4.750% Senior Notes due <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">2028-1</FONT> (the &#147;2028 Senior Notes&#148;, and together with the 2022
Senior Notes, 2025 Senior Notes, 2027 Senior Notes and the 2026 Senior Notes, the &#147;Post-2017 Notes&#148;, and the Post-2017 Notes collectively with the 2023 Senior Notes, the &#147;Notes&#148;, and each series of the Notes, a
&#147;Series&#148;). Deutsche Telekom AG (&#147;DT&#148;) holds all of the outstanding Notes of each Series and consented to the Indenture Amendments. DT is <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> US, Inc.&#146;s <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">(&#147;T-Mobile&#148;)</FONT> majority stockholder and a holder of a portion of <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> USA&#146;s outstanding debt, as further described in
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile&#146;s</FONT> periodic reports with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Indenture Amendments are
being effected in connection with&nbsp;the previously announced agreement by <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> to merge (the &#147;Merger&#148;) a wholly-owned subsidiary of <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> with Sprint
Corporation (&#147;Sprint&#148;), pursuant to that certain Business Combination Agreement (the &#147;Business Combination Agreement&#148;), dated as of April&nbsp;29, 2018, among Sprint, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile,</FONT> SoftBank
Group Corp. (&#147;SoftBank&#148;), DT and the additional parties thereto (the Merger, together with the other transactions contemplated by the Business Combination Agreement, the <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">&#147;T-Mobile</FONT>
Transaction&#148;). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Indenture Amendments, among other things, amend the Indenture (i)&nbsp;pertaining to the 2023 Senior Notes to increase the amount
of secured debt under the Credit Facilities (as defined in the Indenture) ratio basket&nbsp;from the greater of (x)&nbsp;$9.0&nbsp;billion and (y)&nbsp;150% of Consolidated Cash Flow (as defined in the Indenture, as applicable to the 2023 Senior
Notes) to the greater of (x)&nbsp;$9.0&nbsp;billion and (y)&nbsp;an amount that would not cause the Secured Debt to Cash Flow Ratio (as defined in the Indenture, as applicable to the Post-2017 Notes) (calculated net of cash and cash equivalents) to
exceed 2.00x, (ii)&nbsp;pertaining to all Notes to allow certain entities related to Sprint&#146;s existing spectrum securitization notes program (the &#147;Existing Sprint Spectrum Program&#148;) to be
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">non-guarantor</FONT> Restricted Subsidiaries (as defined in the Indenture), provided that the aggregate principal amount of the spectrum notes issued and outstanding under the Existing Sprint Spectrum Program does
not exceed $7.0&nbsp;billion and provided that the principal amount of such spectrum notes shall reduce the amount available under the Credit Facilities ratio basket, (iii)&nbsp;pertaining to the 2025 Senior Notes to amend the maturity date from
April&nbsp;15, 2025 to April&nbsp;15, 2021, (iv) pertaining to the 2027 Senior Notes to amend the maturity date from April&nbsp;15, 2027 to April&nbsp;15, 2022, and (v)&nbsp;pertaining to the 2025 Senior Notes to provide that <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> USA shall be required to redeem 100% of the then-outstanding 2025 Senior Notes, at a price equal to 100% of the aggregate principal amount of the 2025 Senior Notes, plus accrued and unpaid interest on the
2025 Senior Notes redeemed to, but not including, the applicable redemption date, on any date that <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> USA redeems or prepays any other notes or debt securities (other than the other Notes) issued by <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> USA and outstanding as of April&nbsp;29, 2018. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Indenture Amendments will become effective immediately
prior to the consummation of the <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> Transaction. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Supplemental Indenture is attached hereto as
Exhibit&nbsp;4.1. The foregoing description of the Supplemental Indenture is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Supplemental Indenture, which is incorporated herein by reference. </P>
