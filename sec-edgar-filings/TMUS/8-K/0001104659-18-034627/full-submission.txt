<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item&nbsp;1.01</font></b>&#160; <b> Entry Into a Material Definitive Agreement.</b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On May&nbsp;20, 2018, T-Mobile USA,&nbsp;Inc. (the &#147;T-Mobile USA&#148;) entered into the Thirty-Seventh Supplemental Indenture (the &#147;Supplemental Indenture&#148;) by and among T-Mobile USA, the guarantors party thereto and Deutsche Bank Trust Company Americas (the &#147;Trustee&#148;), which amends and supplements the Indenture, dated April&nbsp;28, 2013, by and among T-Mobile USA, the guarantors party thereto and Deutsche Bank Trust Company Americas (as amended and supplemented, the &#147;Indenture&#148;).</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Supplemental Indenture effects certain amendments (the &#147;Indenture Amendments&#148;) to the Indenture pertaining to T-Mobile USA&#146;s (i)&nbsp;6.000% Senior Notes due 2023 (CUSIP No.&nbsp;87264AAM7) (the &#147;2023 Notes&#148;), (ii)&nbsp;6.500% Senior Notes due 2024 (CUSIP No.&nbsp;87264AAJ4) (the &#147;6.500% 2024 Notes&#148;), (iii)&nbsp;6.000% Senior Notes due 2024 (CUSIP No.&nbsp;87264AAQ8) (the &#147;6.000% 2024 Notes&#148;), (iv)&nbsp;6.375% Senior Notes due 2025 (CUSIP No.&nbsp;87264AAN5) (the &#147;2025 Notes&#148;), (v)&nbsp;6.500% Notes due 2026 (CUSIP No.&nbsp;87264AAP0) (the &#147;2026 Notes&#148; and, collectively with the 2023 Notes, the 6.500% 2024 Notes, the 6.000% 2024 Notes and the 2025 Notes, the &#147;Pre-2017 Notes&#148;), (vi)&nbsp;4.000% Senior Notes due 2022 (CUSIP No.&nbsp;87264AAR6) (the &#147;2022 Notes&#148;), (vii)&nbsp;5.125% Senior Notes due 2025 (CUSIP No.&nbsp;87264AAS4) (the &#147;5.125% 2025 Notes&#148;), (viii)&nbsp;5.375% Senior Notes due 2027 (CUSIP No.&nbsp;87264AAT2) (the &#147;2027 Notes&#148;), (ix)&nbsp;4.500% Senior Notes due 2026 (CUSIP No.&nbsp;87264AAU9) (the &#147;4.500% 2026 Notes&#148;) and (x)&nbsp;4.750% Senior Notes due 2028 (CUSIP No.&nbsp;87264AAV7) (the &#147;2028 Notes&#148;, and together with the 4.500% 2026 Notes, the &#147;2018 Notes&#148;, and the 2028 Notes together with the 2022 Notes, 5.125% 2025 Notes, 2027 Notes and the 4.500% 2026 Notes, the &#147;Post-2017 Notes&#148;, and the Post-2017 Notes together with the Pre-2017 Notes, the &#147;Notes&#148;, and each series of the Notes, a &#147;Series&#148;). Holders representing at least a majority in aggregate principal amount of each Series&nbsp;of the Notes consented to the Indenture Amendments.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Indenture Amendments are being effected in connection with </font>the previously announced agreement by T-Mobile US,&nbsp;Inc. (&#147;T-Mobile&#148;) to merge (the &#147;Merger&#148;) a wholly-owned subsidiary of T-Mobile with Sprint Corporation (&#147;Sprint&#148;), pursuant to that certain Business Combination Agreement (the &#147;Business Combination Agreement&#148;), dated as of April&nbsp;29, 2018, among Sprint, T-Mobile, SoftBank Group Corp. (&#147;SoftBank&#148;), Deutsche Telekom AG and the additional parties thereto (the Merger, together with the other transactions contemplated by the Business Combination Agreement, the &#147;T-Mobile/Sprint Transaction&#148;).</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Indenture Amendments, among other things, amend the Indenture (i)&nbsp;pertaining to the Pre-2017 Notes to increase the amount of secured debt under the Credit Facilities ratio basket&nbsp;from the greater of (x)&nbsp;$9.0 billion and (y)&nbsp;150% of Consolidated Cash Flow (as defined in the Indenture, as applicable to the Pre-2017 Notes) to the greater of (x)&nbsp;$9.0 billion and (y)&nbsp;an amount that would not cause the Secured Debt to Cash Flow Ratio (as defined in the Indenture, as applicable to the Post-2017 Notes) (calculated net of cash and cash equivalents) to exceed 2.00x and (ii)&nbsp;pertaining to all Notes to allow certain entities related to Sprint&#146;s existing spectrum securitization notes program (the &#147;Existing Sprint Spectrum Program&#148;) to be non-guarantor Restricted Subsidiaries (as defined in the Indenture), provided that the aggregate principal amount of the spectrum notes issued and outstanding under the Existing Sprint Spectrum Program does not exceed $7.0 billion and provided that the principal amount of such spectrum notes shall reduce the amount available under the Credit Facilities ratio basket.&#160; The Indenture Amendments will become effective immediately prior to the consummation of the T-Mobile Transaction.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Supplemental Indenture is attached hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;4.1. The foregoing description of the Supplemental Indenture is qualified in its entirety by reference to the full text of the Supplemental Indenture, which is incorporated herein by reference.</font></p>
