Kavanaugh was elected to the Board of Directors (the &#8220;Board&#8221;) of <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> US, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;), effective immediately. Mr.&#160;Kavanaugh was selected as a designee of Deutsche Telekom AG (&#8220;Deutsche Telekom&#8221;) pursuant to that certain Second Amended and Restated Stockholders&#8217; Agreement, dated June&#160;22, 2020, by and among the Company, Deutsche Telekom and SoftBank Group Corp. Mr.&#160;Kavanaugh was also appointed to the Audit Committee of the Board (the &#8220;Audit Committee&#8221;), effective on August&#160;1, 2023, replacing Mr.&#160;Kelvin Westbrook who has served on the Audit Committee since June&#160;16, 2023. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In connection with his election to the Board, Mr.&#160;Kavanaugh was granted an award of 1,582 restricted stock units under the Company&#8217;s 2023 Incentive Award Plan, which will vest on the <span style="white-space:nowrap">one-year</span> anniversary of the Company&#8217;s 2023 annual stockholders meeting, subject to continued Board service through the vesting date.
