 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01 &#150; Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On July&nbsp;25, 2017, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> USA, Inc. (&#147;TMUSA&#148;), a wholly-owned subsidiary of <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> US, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;), amended the terms of its $2&nbsp;billion secured term loan due January 2024 (the &#147;2024 Term Loan&#148;) from Deutsche Telekom AG (&#147;DT&#148;), its majority
stockholder, to lower the applicable margin payable on LIBOR indexed loans thereunder from 2.25% to 2.00%. The reduction of the applicable margin on the 2024 Term Loan is expected to result in an aggregate yearly interest savings of $5&nbsp;million
over the remaining life of the 2024 Term Loan. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">TMUSA was not required to pay any amendment fees or other consideration to DT in
connection with the repricing of the 2024 Term Loan. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The repricing of the 2024 Term Loan was provided under Amendment No.&nbsp;4, dated
as of July&nbsp;25, 2017 (&#147;Amendment No.&nbsp;4&#148;), to that certain Term Loan Credit Agreement, dated as of November&nbsp;9, 2015 (as amended, the &#147;Term Loan Facility&#148;) among the Company, TMUSA, Deutsche Bank AG, New York Branch,
as administrative agent and collateral agent, and DT, as the lender. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Amendment No.&nbsp;4 also modifies the 2024 Term Loan to include a
soft-call prepayment premium of 1.00% of the outstanding principal amount of the 2024 Term Loan payable to DT upon certain refinancings of such loan by TMUSA with lower priced debt prior to a date that is six months after July&nbsp;25, 2017. No
other material terms of the 2024 Term Loan or the Term Loan Facility were modified. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">DT is the Company&#146;s majority stockholder and a
holder of a portion of TMUSA&#146;s outstanding debt, as further described in the Company&#146;s periodic reports filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Amendment No.&nbsp;4 is incorporated herein by reference as an exhibit to this current report on Form
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">8-K.</FONT> The foregoing summary of Amendment No.&nbsp;4 is qualified in its entirety by reference to such exhibit. </P>
