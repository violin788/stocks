<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"> <P STYLE=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman; " ALIGN="left"><B>Regulation FD Disclosure. </B></P></TD></TR></TABLE>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On May&nbsp;20, 2019, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> US, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) and Sprint Corporation (&#147;Sprint&#148; and,
together with the &#147;Company&#148;, the &#147;Parties&#148;) filed with the Secretary of the Federal Communications Commission (the &#147;FCC&#148;) a written <I>ex parte</I> presentation (the &#147;Presentation&#148;) relating to the proposed
combination of the respective businesses (the &#147;Merger&#148;) of the Company and Sprint, pursuant to that certain Business Combination Agreement, dated as of April&nbsp;29, 2018, by and among the Company, Sprint and the other parties thereto.
</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The completion of the Merger remains subject to regulatory approvals and certain other customary closing conditions. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><I>Presentation Overview </I></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">To facilitate the FCC&#146;s
review and approval of the FCC license transfers associated with the proposed Merger, the Presentation includes proposed commitments from the Parties that provide, subject to the terms thereof, that: (1)&nbsp;the combined company following the
closing (the &#147;Closing&#148;) of the Merger (&#147;New <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile&#148;)</FONT> will build a world-leading 5G network; (2)&nbsp;rural Americans will receive robust high speed 5G broadband service; <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">(3)&nbsp;in-home</FONT> broadband competition will be enhanced; and (4)&nbsp;Boost Mobile (&#147;Boost&#148;), one of the Sprint&#146;s existing prepaid brands, will be divested to a serious and credible buyer who can
compete aggressively in prepaid services on a long-term basis. In addition, in the Presentation, the Parties reconfirm their prior commitment to make available the same or better rate plans as those offered by
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> or Sprint as of February&nbsp;4, 2019 for three years following the Merger. Finally, the Parties make commitments concerning the Altice USA, Inc. (&#147;Altice&#148;) post-merger mobile virtual
network operator (&#147;MVNO&#148;) relationship with New <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile.</FONT> These commitments are summarized below, and the full text of the publicly available version of the Presentation can be accessed at the FCC
website at <U><FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">https://www.fcc.gov/transaction/t-mobile-sprint</FONT></U> and New T-Mobile website at <U>https://newtmobile.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/FCC-Filing-May-20.pdf</U>. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><I>Commitment to Build a World-Leading Nationwide 5G Network </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Parties have committed that, within three years of the Closing, New <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> will cover three-fourths of the U.S.
population with <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">mid-band</FONT> spectrum and 97&nbsp;percent of the U.S. population with <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">low-band</FONT> spectrum, resulting in almost
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">two-thirds</FONT> of Americans receiving download speeds in excess of 100 Mbps. Within six years of the Closing, the Parties committed to deploying a 5G network with: <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">low-band</FONT>
coverage of at least 99&nbsp;percent of the U.S. population; <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">mid-band</FONT> coverage of at least 88&nbsp;percent of the U.S. population; a minimum number of 5G sites nationwide; an average number of <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">low-band</FONT> and <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">mid-band</FONT> 5G spectrum deployed across the 5G sites; 99&nbsp;percent of the U.S. population experiencing download speeds equal to, or greater than, 50 Mbps; and
90&nbsp;percent of the U.S. population experiencing download speeds equal to, or greater than, 100 Mbps. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><I>Commitment to Provide High-Speed 5G Services
for Rural America </I></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Parties have also committed that New <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> will accelerate the deployment of <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">mid-band</FONT> and <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">low-band</FONT> sites in rural America. Specifically, high-capacity <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">mid-band</FONT> spectrum will be extended to 6.5&nbsp;million more
rural Americans in the first three years of deployment &#150; faster than originally planned. Further, by year six, New <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> will increase its <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">mid-band</FONT> coverage of
rural America by an additional 6.1&nbsp;million rural Americans. As a result, within three years of the Closing, New <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> will deliver 50 Mbps or higher to
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">two-thirds</FONT> of the rural population and 100 Mbps or higher to over half the rural population. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In addition, the
Parties have committed that, within six years of the Closing, New <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> will deploy a 5G network with <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">low-band</FONT> coverage of at least 90&nbsp;percent of the rural
population; <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">mid-band</FONT> coverage of at least 66.7&nbsp;percent of the rural population; a minimum number of 5G sites in rural areas; an average number of <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">low-band</FONT> and <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">mid-band</FONT> 5G spectrum deployed across the 5G sites; 90&nbsp;percent of the rural population experiencing download speeds equal to, or greater than, 50 Mbps; and 66.7&nbsp;percent of the rural population experiencing
download speeds equal to, or greater than, 100 Mbps. </P>

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><I>Commitment for <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">In-Home</FONT> Broadband </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Parties have committed that, within three years of the Closing, New <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> will market the <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">in-home</FONT> broadband service to at least 9.6&nbsp;million eligible households, of which at least 2.6&nbsp;million are rural households and will have a minimum number of supported households, including a minimum number
of supported rural households. In addition, within six years of the Closing, New <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> will market its <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">in-home</FONT> broadband service to at least 28&nbsp;million eligible
households, of which 5.6&nbsp;million are rural households, and will have a minimum number of supported households, including a minimum number of supported rural households. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><I>Commitment to Divest Boost Mobile </I></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Parties have
