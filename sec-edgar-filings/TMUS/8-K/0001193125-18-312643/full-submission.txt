 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;5.07 Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders. </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On October&nbsp;30, 2018, <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> US, Inc.
<FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">(&#147;T-Mobile&#148;),</FONT> announced that, following the declaration by the Securities and Exchange Commission on October&nbsp;29, 2018 that <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile&#146;s</FONT> registration
statement on Form <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">S-4</FONT> (No. <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">333-226435)</FONT> (the &#147;Registration Statement&#148;) became effective, it received a written consent of Deutsche Telekom Holding B.V., a
<I>besloten vennootschap met beperkte aansprakelijkheid</I> organized and existing under the laws of the Netherlands (&#147;DT Holding&#148;), with respect to 538,590,941 shares of common stock of <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile,</FONT>
par value $0.00001 per share (the <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">&#147;T-Mobile</FONT> Common Stock&#148;), in favor of the proposals summarized below to adopt (1)&nbsp;the share issuance and (2)&nbsp;an amended and restated certificate of
incorporation of <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile,</FONT> as further described below and contemplated by the Business Combination Agreement dated as of April&nbsp;29, 2018 (the &#147;Business Combination Agreement&#148;), by and among <FONT
STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile,</FONT> Huron Merger Sub LLC, a Delaware limited liability company and a wholly owned subsidiary of <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile,</FONT> Superior Merger Sub Corporation, a Delaware corporation and a

forth therein, Deutsche Telekom AG, an <I>Aktiengesellschaft</I> organized and existing under the laws of the Federal Republic of Germany, DT Holding, and SoftBank Group Corp., a Japanese <I>kabushiki kaisha</I> (&#147;SoftBank&#148;), pursuant to

completion of the Merger Transactions remains subject to regulatory approvals and certain other customary closing conditions, and is expected to occur during the first half of 2019. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The approval of the proposals by 538,590,941 shares of <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> Common Stock pursuant to the written
