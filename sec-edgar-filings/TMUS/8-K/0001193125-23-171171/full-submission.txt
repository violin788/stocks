 <p style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold">Item&#160;5.02 &#8212; Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </p> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As noted below under Item 5.07, at the Annual Meeting of Stockholders (the &#8220;Annual Meeting&#8221;) of <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> US, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) held on June&#160;16, 2023, the Company&#8217;s stockholders approved the <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> US, Inc. 2023 Incentive Award Plan (the &#8220;Incentive Plan&#8221;) and the <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> US, Inc. The Incentive Plan and the ESPP were adopted by the Board of Directors of the Company (the &#8220;Board&#8221;) on March&#160;24, 2023, subject to stockholder approval. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">A description of the material terms of the Plans was included in the Company&#8217;s Definitive Proxy Statement (&#8220;Proxy Statement&#8221;) on Schedule 14A filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (&#8220;SEC&#8221;) on April&#160;28, 2023.
