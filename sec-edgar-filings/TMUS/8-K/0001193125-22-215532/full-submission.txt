 <p style="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold">Item&#160;1.01 Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement </p> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On August&#160;8, 2022, <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> US, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;), through its direct, wholly-owned subsidiary <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> USA, Inc. <span style="white-space:nowrap">(&#8220;T-Mobile</span> USA&#8221;), and its affiliate <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> License LLC, entered into License Purchase Agreements (the &#8220;License Purchase Agreements&#8221;) with Channel 51 License Co LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (&#8220;51 License&#8221;), and LB License Co, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company (&#8220;LB License,&#8221; and together with 51 License, &#8220;Sellers&#8221;). Pursuant to the License Purchase Agreements, <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> License LLC will acquire spectrum in the 600 MHz band from Sellers (the &#8220;Licenses&#8221;) in exchange for total cash consideration of $3.5&#160;billion (the &#8220;Purchase Price&#8221;), of which approximately $1.9&#160;billion will be paid to 51 License and approximately $1.6&#160;billion will be paid to LB License. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The geographic areas covered by the Licenses include markets in San Francisco, California, Atlanta, Georgia, Chicago, Illinois, Los Angeles, California, Boston, Massachusetts, Tampa, Florida, Columbus, Ohio, Minneapolis, Minnesota, Seattle, Washington, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, Baltimore, Maryland, Washington, D.C., Dallas, Texas, Phoenix, Arizona, Houston, Texas, Salt Lake City, Utah, Saint Louis, Missouri and New Orleans, Louisiana. The Licenses range from 10MHz to 30MHz per market and cover over 108&#160;million pops&#8212;approximately one third of the US population.
