<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On and effective as of December&nbsp;20, 2017 (the &#147;Effective Date&#148;), <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</FONT> US, Inc. (the
&#147;Company&#148;) entered into an amended and restated employment agreement (the &#147;Employment Agreement&#148;) with J. Braxton Carter, pursuant to which Mr.&nbsp;Carter will continue to serve as the Company&#146;s Executive Vice President and
Chief Financial Officer. The Employment Agreement supersedes and replaces the prior employment agreement between the Company and Mr.&nbsp;Carter, dated as of January&nbsp;25, 2013. The Employment Agreement provides for an initial employment term
through March&nbsp;1, 2019 (the &#147;Initial Term&#148;), subject to extension as mutually agreed to by the parties. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Pursuant to the
Employment Agreement, Mr.&nbsp;Carter is entitled to (i)&nbsp;an annual base salary equal to $850,000, (ii) commencing with calendar year 2018, an annual short-term incentive (the &#147;STI Award&#148;) targeted at 150% of Mr.&nbsp;Carter&#146;s
eligible base earnings during the applicable year, payable based on the attainment of <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">pre-established</FONT> performance goals, and (iii)&nbsp;commencing with calendar year 2018, an annual long-term incentive or
other equity awards with a target grant-date value that is no less than 250% of Mr.&nbsp;Carter&#146;s total cash compensation (<I>i.e.</I>, base salary plus target short-term incentive award) as in effect at the time of grant. Mr.&nbsp;Carter may
also participate in Company employee benefit plans, to the same extent and on the same terms as apply to the Company&#146;s similarly-situated executives generally. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In connection with his entrance into the Employment Agreement, Mr.&nbsp;Carter will receive a <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">one-time</FONT>
cash bonus in an amount equal to $2,500,000 (the &#147;Special Cash Bonus&#148;), payable on or within 15 days after March&nbsp;1, 2019, subject to and conditioned upon his continued employment through March&nbsp;1, 2019 (except as otherwise
described below). In addition, the Company will grant Mr.&nbsp;Carter a <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">one-time</FONT> award of time-based restricted stock units under the Company&#146;s 2013 Omnibus Incentive Plan (as amended, the
&#147;Plan&#148;), with respect to a number of shares of Company common stock equal to $2,500,000 divided by the average closing price of the Company&#146;s common stock for the 30 calendar <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">day-period</FONT> ending
five business days prior to the grant date, rounded up to the nearest whole restricted stock unit (the &#147;Special Equity Award&#148;). The Special Equity Award will vest in full on March&nbsp;1, 2019, subject to and conditioned upon
Mr.&nbsp;Carter&#146;s continued employment through such date (except as otherwise described below). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Employment Agreement provides
