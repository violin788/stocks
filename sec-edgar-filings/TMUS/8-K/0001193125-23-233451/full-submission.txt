<td style="width:11%;vertical-align:top" align="left"><span style="font-weight:bold">Item&#160;1.01</span></td>
<td align="left" style="vertical-align:top"> <p style=" margin-top:0pt ; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:bold;text-align:left">Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement </p></td></tr></table> <p style="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On September&#160;12, 2023, <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> US, Inc. (the &#8220;Company&#8221;), its direct, wholly-owned subsidiary <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> USA, Inc. <span style="white-space:nowrap">(&#8220;T-Mobile</span> USA&#8221;), and its affiliate <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> License LLC (together with <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> USA, <span style="white-space:nowrap">&#8220;T-Mobile&#8221;),</span> entered into a License Purchase Agreement (the &#8220;License Purchase Agreement&#8221;) with Comcast Corporation and its affiliate Comcast OTR1, LLC (together with Comcast Corporation, &#8220;Comcast&#8221;). Pursuant to the License Purchase Agreement, <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> License LLC will acquire spectrum in the 600 MHz band from Comcast (the &#8220;Licenses&#8221;) in exchange for total cash consideration of between $1.2&#160;billion and $3.3&#160;billion (the &#8220;Purchase Price&#8221;). Consummation of the proposed transactions is subject to various customary conditions, including, among others, expiration of the applicable waiting period under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act of 1976, as amended, and approval of the transaction by the Federal Communications Commission (the &#8220;FCC&#8221;). The parties expect to make the requisite transfer filings with the FCC in the first half of 2027. The closing is anticipated by <span style="white-space:nowrap">T-Mobile</span> to occur in the first half of 2028. The License Purchase Agreement contains customary representations, warranties, and covenants. </p> <p style="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The final Purchase Price will be determined, in the aggregate and on a per License basis, based on the set of Licenses subject to the License Purchase Agreement at the time the parties make required transfer filings with the FCC.
