      <div style="text-align: justify;">VALLEY COTTAGE, NY&#8212;July 24, 2020&#8212;CreditRiskMonitor&#8217;s (OTCQX: CRMZ) preliminary unaudited results of revenue for the second quarter as of June 30, 2020;&#160; the
        first six months of the fiscal year 2020;&#160; and for the first quarter ended March 31, 2020 are up approximately 8%, 7%, and 6% as compared to the same periods&#160; in 2019, respectively.&#160; Management remains cautiously optimistic about the rest of the
        year, due to the fact that many of our subscribers continue to struggle through the COVID-19 economic downturn and are implementing cost cutting measures.</div>
      <div><br>
      </div>
      <div style="text-align: justify; font-weight: bold;">Overview</div>
      <div><br>
      </div>
      <div style="text-align: justify;">CreditRiskMonitor (http://www.crmz.com) is a web-based publisher of financial information that helps corporate credit and procurement professionals stay
        ahead of business financial risk quickly, accurately and cost effectively. The service offers comprehensive commercial credit reports and financial risk analysis covering public companies worldwide.</div>
      <div><br>
      </div>
      <div style="text-align: justify;">The Company also collects a significant amount of trade receivable data on both public and a select group of private companies every month, to help
        subscribers determine payment performance.</div>
      <div><br>
      </div>
      <div style="text-align: justify;">Over 35% of the Fortune 1000 plus over 1,000 other large companies worldwide depend on CreditRiskMonitor&#8217;s timely news alerts and reports featuring detailed
        analyses of financial statements, ratio analysis and trend reports, peer analyses, bond agency ratings, crowdsourcing of risk professionals as well as the Company&#8217;s proprietary FRISK&#174; and PAYCE&#174; scores.</div>
